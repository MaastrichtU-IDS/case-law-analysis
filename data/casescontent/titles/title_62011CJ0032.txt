Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 14 March 2013. Allianz Hungária Biztosító Zrt. and Others v Gazdasági Versenyhivatal. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Magyar Köztársaság Legfelsőbb Bírósága. Competition — Article 101(1) TFEU — Application of similar national regulations — Jurisdiction of the Court — Bilateral agreements between an insurance company and car repairers relating to hourly repair charges — Charges paid depending on the number of insurance contracts concluded for the insurance company by those repairers in their capacity as brokers — Concept of ‘agreement having as its object the restriction of competition’.
