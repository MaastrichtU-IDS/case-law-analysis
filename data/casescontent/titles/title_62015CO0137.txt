Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 17 November 2015. María Pilar Plaza Bravo v Servicio Público de Empleo Estatal Dirección Provincial de Álava. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court — Directive 79/7/EEC — Article 4(1) — Equal treatment of male and female workers — Part-time workers; primarily female — National legislation providing for a maximum amount of unemployment benefit — Legislation referring; for the purposes of the calculation of that amount; to the relationship between the working hours of the part-time employees concerned and the working hours of full-time employees.
