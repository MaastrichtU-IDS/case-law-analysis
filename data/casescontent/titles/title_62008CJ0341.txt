Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 12 January 2010. Domnica Petersen v Berufungsausschuss für Zahnärzte für den Bezirk Westfalen-Lippe. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Sozialgericht Dortmund - Germany. Directive 2000/78/EC - Articles 2(5) and 6(1) - Prohibition of discrimination on grounds of age - Provision of national law setting a maximum age of 68 for practice as a panel dentist - Aim pursued - Measure necessary for the protection of health - Consistency - Appropriateness of the measure.
