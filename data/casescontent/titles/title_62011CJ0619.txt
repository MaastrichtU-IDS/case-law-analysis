Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 21 February 2013.   Patricia Dumont de Chassart v Office national d’allocations familiales pour travailleurs salariés (ONAFTS).   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal du travail de Bruxelles - Belgium.   Social security - Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 - Articles 72; 78(2)(b) and 79(1)(a) - Family benefits for orphans - Aggregation of periods of insurance and employment - Periods completed by the surviving parent in another Member State - Not taken into account.
