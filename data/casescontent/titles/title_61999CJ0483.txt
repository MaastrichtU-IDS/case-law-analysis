Judgment of the Court of 4 June 2002.   Commission of the European Communities v French Republic.   Failure by a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Articles 52 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Article 43 EC) and 73b of the EC Treaty (now Article 56 EC) - Rights attaching to the 'golden share held by the French Republic in Société Nationale Elf-Aquitaine.
