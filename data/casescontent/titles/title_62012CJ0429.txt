Reference for a preliminary ruling — Equal treatment in employment and occupation — Article 21 of the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Article 45 TFEU — Directive 2000/78/EC — Difference in treatment on grounds of age — Determination of the reference date for the purposes of advancement on the salary scale — Limitation period — Principle of effectiveness.
