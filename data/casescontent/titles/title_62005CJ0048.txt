Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 25 January 2007. Adam Opel AG v Autec AG. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landgericht Nürnberg-Fürth - Germany. Reference for a preliminary ruling - Trade Marks - Article 5(1)(a) and (2); and Article 6(1)(b) of the First Directive 89/104/EEC - Right of a trade mark proprietor to prevent use by a third party of a sign identical or similar to the trade mark - Trade mark registered for motor vehicles and for toys - Reproduction of the trade mark by a third party on scale models of that make of vehicle.
