Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 26 May 2016. Bookit; Ltd v Commissioners for Her Majesty's Revenue and Customs. Request for a preliminary ruling from the First-tier Tribunal (Tax Chamber). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Common system of value added tax — Directive 2006/112/EC — Exemption — Article 135(1)(d) — Transactions concerning payments and transfers — Meaning — Purchase of cinema tickets by telephone or via the Internet — Payment by debit card or credit card – ‘Card handling’ services.
