Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 19 April 2012. Galina Meister v Speech Design Carrier Systems GmbH. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesarbeitsgericht. Directives 2000/43/EC; 2000/78/EC and 2006/54/EC – Equal treatment in employment and occupation – Worker showing that he meets the requirements listed in a job advertisement – Right of that worker to have access to information indicating whether the employer has recruited another applicant.
