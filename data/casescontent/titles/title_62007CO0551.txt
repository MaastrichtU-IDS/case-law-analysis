Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 19 December 2008. Deniz Sahin v Bundesminister für Inneres. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria. Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Directive 2004/38/EC - Articles 18 EC and 39 EC - Right to respect for family life - Right of residence of a national of a non-member country who entered the territory of a Member State as an asylum seeker and subsequently married a national of another Member State.
