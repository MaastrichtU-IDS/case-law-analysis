Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 15 January 2009.   Hecht-Pharma GmbH v Staatliches Gewerbeaufsichtsamt Lüneburg.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesverwaltungsgericht - Germany.   Directive 2001/83/EC - Articles 1(2) and 2(2) - Concept of ‘medicinal product by function’ - Product in respect of which it has not been established that it is a medicinal product by function - Account taken of the content in active substances.
