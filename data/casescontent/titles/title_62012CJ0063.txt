Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 19 November 2013.   European Commission v Council of the European Union.   Action for annulment - Decision 2011/866/EU - Annual adjustment of the remuneration and pensions of officials and other servants of the European Union - Staff Regulations - Article 65 of the Staff Regulations - Method of adjustment - Article 3 of Annex XI to the Staff Regulations - Exception clause - Article 10 of Annex XI to the Staff Regulations - Serious and sudden deterioration in the economic and social situation - Adjustment of correction coefficients - Article 64 of the Staff Regulations - Council decision - Refusal to adopt the Commission’s proposal.
