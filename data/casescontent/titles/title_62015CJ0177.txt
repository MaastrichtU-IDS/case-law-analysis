Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 23 November 2016. Nelsons GmbH v Ayonnax Nutripharm GmbH and Bachblütentreff Ltd. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesgerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Consumer information and protection — Regulation (EC) No 1924/2006 — Nutrition and health claims made on foods — Transitional measures — Article 28(2) — Products bearing trade marks or brand names existing before 1 January 2005 — ‘Bach flower’ remedies — European Union mark RESCUE — Products marketed as medicinal products before 1 January 2005 and as foodstuffs after that date.
