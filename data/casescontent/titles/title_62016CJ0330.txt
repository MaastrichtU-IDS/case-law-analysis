Judgment of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 1 June 2017. Piotr Zarski v Andrzej Stadnicki. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Sąd Okręgowy w Warszawie. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Combating late payments in commercial transactions — Directive 2011/7/EU — Commercial lease contracts of indefinite duration — Late rent payments — Contracts concluded before the period for transposing that directive had expired — National rules — Exclusion of such contracts from the temporal scope of that directive.
