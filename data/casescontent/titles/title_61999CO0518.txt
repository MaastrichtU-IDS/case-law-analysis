Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 5 April 2001.   Richard Gaillard v Alaya Chekili.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d'appel de Bruxelles - Belgium.   Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Brussels Convention - Article 16(1) - Exclusive jurisdiction in proceedings which have as their object rights in rem in immovable property - Scope - Action for rescission of a contract of sale of immovable property and for damages.
