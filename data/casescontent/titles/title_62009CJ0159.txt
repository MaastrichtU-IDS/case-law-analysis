Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 18 November 2010.   Lidl SNC v Vierzon Distribution SA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal de commerce de Bourges - France.   Directives 84/450/EEC and 97/55/EC - Conditions under which a comparative advertising is permitted - Price comparison based on selection of food products marketed by two competing retail store chains - Goods meeting the same needs or intended for the same purpose - Misleading advertising - Comparison based on a verifiable feature.
