Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 20 November 2008. Staatssecretaris van Financiën v Heuschen &amp; Schrouff Oriëntal Foods Trading BV. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands. Reference for a preliminary ruling - Validity of a classification regulation - Interpretation of the Annex to Regulation (EC) No 1196/97 - Articles 220 and 239 of the Customs Code - Articles 871 and 905 of Regulation (EEC) No 2454/93 - Dried sheets consisting of rice flour; salt and water - Tariff classification - Post-clearance recovery of import duties - Procedure for remission - Detectable error on the part of the customs authorities - Obvious negligence on the part of the importer.
