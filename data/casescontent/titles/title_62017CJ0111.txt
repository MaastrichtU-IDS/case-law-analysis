Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 8 June 2017. OL v PQ. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Monomeles Protodikeio Athinon. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Judicial cooperation in civil matters — Jurisdiction; recognition and enforcement of decisions in matrimonial matters and in the matters of parental responsibility — International child abduction — Hague Convention of 25 October 1980 — Regulation (EC) No 2201/2003 — Article 11 — Application for return — Concept of ‘habitual residence’ of an infant — Child born; as agreed by her parents; in a Member State other than that where they were habitually resident — Child continuing to reside for the first months of her life in the Member State of her birth — Mother’s decision not to return to the Member State where the couple had been habitually resident.
