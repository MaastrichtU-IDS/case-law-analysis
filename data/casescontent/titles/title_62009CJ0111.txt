Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 20 May 2010. Česká podnikatelská pojišťovna as; Vienna Insurance Group v Michal Bilas. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Okresní soud v Chebu - Czech Republic. Regulation (EC) No 44/2001- Action brought by an insurer before the court of its place of domicile seeking the payment of an insurance premium by the policyholder; domiciled in a different Member State - Appearance of the defendant entered before the court seised - Jurisdiction not contested and defence as to substance - Entering an appearance conferring jurisdiction.
