Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 28 May 2013.   Abdulbasit Abdulrahim v Council of the European Union and European Commission.   Appeal - Common foreign and security policy (CFSP) - Restrictive measures directed against certain persons and entities associated with Usama bin Laden; the Al-Qaida network and the Taliban - Regulation (EC) No 881/2002 - Action for annulment - Removal of the interested party from the list of persons and entities concerned - Interest in bringing proceedings.
