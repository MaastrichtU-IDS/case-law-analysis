Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 28 February 2013. Katja Ettwein v Finanzamt Konstanz. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Finanzgericht Baden-Württemberg. Agreement between the European Community and its Member States; of the one part; and the Swiss Confederation; of the other; on the free movement of persons — Equal treatment — Self-employed frontier workers — Nationals of a Member State of the Union — Business income received in that Member State — Transfer of residence to Switzerland — Refusal of a tax advantage in that Member State because of the transfer of residence.
