Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 29 October 2009. Commission of the European Communities v Republic of Finland. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Sixth VAT Directive - Article 2(1) and Article 4(1) and (2) - Meaning of ‘economic activities’ - Public legal aid offices - Legal aid services provided in legal proceedings in return for a part contribution paid by the recipient - Meaning of ‘direct link’ between the service rendered and the consideration received.
