Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 3 October 2013.   Luigi Marcuccio v European Commission.   Appeal - Assignment - Automatic transfer - Decision to reassign an official from a third country to the Commission headquarters in Brussels (Belgium) - Decision annulled by the General Court after referral back by the Court of Justice - Compensation for the harm purportedly suffered as a result of the annulment of a decision to reassign.
