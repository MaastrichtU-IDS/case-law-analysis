Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 12 December 2013.   Frédéric Hay v Crédit agricole mutuel de Charente-Maritime et des Deux-Sèvres.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour de cassation - France.   Directive 2000/78/EC - Equal treatment - Collective agreement which restricts a benefit in respect of pay and working conditions to employees who marry - Exclusion of partners entering into a civil solidarity pact - Discrimination based on sexual orientation.
