Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 12 May 2005.   Société financière et industrielle du Peloux v Axa Belgium and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d'appel de Grenoble - France.   Brussels Convention - Jurisdiction in respect of contracts of insurance - Agreement conferring jurisdiction between a policy-holder and an insurer both domiciled in the same Contracting State - Enforceability of a jurisdiction clause against an insured who did not approve that clause - Insured domiciled in another Contracting State.
