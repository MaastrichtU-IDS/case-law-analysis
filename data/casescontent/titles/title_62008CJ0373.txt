Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 11 February 2010. Hoesch Metals and Alloys GmbH v Hauptzollamt Aachen. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Düsseldorf - Germany. Community Customs Code - Article 24 - Non-preferential origin of goods - Origin-conferring processing or working - Silicon blocks originating in China - Separation; crushing and purification of the blocks and the sieving; sorting by size and packaging of the grains in India - Dumping - Validity of Regulation (EC) No 398/2004.
