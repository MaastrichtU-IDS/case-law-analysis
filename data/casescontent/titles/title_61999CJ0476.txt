Judgment of the Court of 19 March 2002.   H. Lommers v Minister van Landbouw; Natuurbeheer en Visserij.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Centrale Raad van Beroep - Netherlands.   Social policy - Equal treatment of men and women - Derogations - Measures to promote equality of opportunity between men and women - Subsidised nursery places made available by a Ministry to its staff - Places reserved only for children of female officials; save in cases of emergency; to be determined by the employer.
