Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 16 November 2017. Robeco Hollands Bezit NV and Others v Stichting Autoriteit Financiële Markten (AFM). Request for a preliminary ruling from the College van Beroep voor het Bedrijfsleven. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2004/39/EC — Markets in financial instruments — Article 4(1)(14) — Definition of ‘regulated market’ — Scope — System in which the participants are brokers representing investors and ‘open end’ investment fund agents required to execute orders relating to their funds.
