Order of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 13 July 2006.   Soffass SpA v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Figurative mark 'NICKY' - Opposition by the proprietor of the national figurative marks 'NOKY' and 'noky' - Purely factual assessment - Appeal manifestly inadmissible.
