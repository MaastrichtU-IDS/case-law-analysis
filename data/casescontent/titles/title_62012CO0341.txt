Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 21 March 2013.   Mizuno KK v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice - Community trade mark - Figurative mark including the letter ‘G’ and the two symbols of gender - Opposition by the proprietor of the figurative mark including the letter ‘G’ and the symbol ‘+’ - Refusal of registration by the Board of Appeal.
