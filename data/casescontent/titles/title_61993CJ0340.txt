Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 9 August 1994.   Klaus Thierschmidt GmbH v Hauptzollamt Essen.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Düsseldorf - Germany.   Value of goods for customs purposes - Inclusion of charges for "own" quotas issued free of charge - No separate declaration of quota charges excluded from customs value - Arrangements for imports of textile products from Taiwan.
