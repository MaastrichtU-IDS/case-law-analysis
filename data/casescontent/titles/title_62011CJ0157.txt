Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 15 March 2012.   Giuseppe Sibilio v Comune di Afragola.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale di Napoli - Italy.   Social policy - Framework agreement on fixed-term work concluded by ETUC; UNICE and CEEP - Directive 1999/70/EC - Clause 2 - Concept of 'an employment contract or relationship defined by law; collective agreements or practice in force in each Member State' - Scope of the framework agreement - Clause 4; point 1 - Principle of non-discrimination - Persons carrying out 'work of social utility' with public authorities - National rule excluding the existence of an employment relationship - National rule establishing a difference between the benefit paid to socially useful workers and the remuneration received by workers engaged under a contract of definite and/or indefinite duration by the same public authorities and carrying out the same activities.
