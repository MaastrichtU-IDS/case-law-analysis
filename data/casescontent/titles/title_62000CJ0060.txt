Judgment of the Court of 11 July 2002.   Mary Carpenter v Secretary of State for the Home Department.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Immigration Appeal Tribunal - United Kingdom.   Freedom to provide services - Article 49 EC - Directive 73/148/EEC - National of a Member State established in that State and providing services to persons established in other Member States - Right of residence in that State of a spouse who is a national of a third country.
