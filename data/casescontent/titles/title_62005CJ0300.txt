Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 23 November 2006. Hauptzollamt Hamburg-Jonas v ZVK Zuchtvieh-Kontor GmbH. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany. Directive 91/628/EEC - Protection of animals during transport - Watering and feeding intervals; journey times and resting periods - Definition of "travel' ("Transportdauer') - Account to be taken of the time taken to load and unload the animals.
