Order of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 16 June 2016. L'Oréal SA v European Union Intellectual Property Office. Appeal — EU trade mark AINHOA — Revocation proceedings — Genuine use of a mark — Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 — Article 15(1); second para.; (a) — Article 51(1)(a) — Proof of use — Regulation (EC) No 2868/95 — Rules 22 and 40(5) — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Appeal in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly unfounded.
