Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 21 October 2014.   Sindicato Nacional dos Profissionais de Seguros e Afins v Via Directa - Companhia de Seguros SA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal do Trabalho de Lisboa - Portugal.   Reference for a preliminary ruling - Article 53(2) of the Rules of Procedure - Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union - Principles of equal treatment and non-discrimination - National legislation establishing salary reductions for certain public sector employees - No implementation of EU law - Clear lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
