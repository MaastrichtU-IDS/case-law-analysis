Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 23 November 2006.   Lidl Italia Srl v Comune di Arcole (VR).   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Giudice di pace di Monselice - Italy.   Directive 2000/13/EC - Labelling of foodstuffs to be delivered as such to the ultimate consumer - Scope of the obligations under Articles 2; 3 and 12 - Compulsory statement of the alcoholic strength by volume for certain alcoholic beverages - Alcoholic beverage produced in a Member State other than that in which the distributor is established - "Amaro alle erbe' - Actual alcoholic strength by volume lower than that appearing on the label - Overstepping of the tolerance - Administrative fine - Liability of the distributor.
