Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 7 March 2013.   Gábor Fekete v Nemzeti Adó- és Vámhivatal Közép-dunántúli Regionális Vám- és Pénzügyőri Főigazgatósága.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Székesfehérvári Törvényszék - Hungary.   Community Customs Code - Article 137 - Regulation implementing the Customs Code - Article 561(2) - Conditions for total relief from import duties - Importation into a Member State of a vehicle whose owner is established in a third country - Private use of the vehicle authorised by the owner otherwise than by an employment contract concluded with the user - No relief.
