Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 14 October 2010. Günter Fuß v Stadt Halle. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgericht Halle - Germany. Social policy - Protection of the safety and health of workers - Directive 2003/88/EC - Organisation of working time - Fire fighters employed in the public sector - Operational service - Article 6(b) and Article 22(1)(b) - Maximum weekly working time - Refusal to work longer than that time - Compulsory transfer to another service - Direct effect - Consequence for national courts.
