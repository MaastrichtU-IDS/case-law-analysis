Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 2 July 2015. NLB Leasing d.o.o. v Republika Slovenija. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Vrhovno sodišče Republike Slovenije. Reference for a preliminary ruling — VAT — Directive 2006/112/EC — Supply of goods or services — Lease agreement — Return of immovable property that is the subject-matter of a lease agreement to the lessor — Concept of ‘cancellation; refusal or total or partial non-payment’ — Lessor’s right to a reduction of the taxable amount — Double taxation — Separate supplies — Principle of fiscal neutrality.
