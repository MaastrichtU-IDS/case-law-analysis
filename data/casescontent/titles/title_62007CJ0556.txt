Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 5 March 2009.   Commission of the European Communities v French Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Common Fisheries Policy - Regulation (EC) No 894/97 - Drift net - Meaning - ‘Thonaille’ fishing net - Prohibition on the fishing of certain species - Regulations (EEC) No 2847/93 and (EC) No 2371/2002 - Lack of an effective system of monitoring to ensure compliance with that prohibition.
