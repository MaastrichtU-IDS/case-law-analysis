Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — EU trade mark — Application for registration of the figurative and word marks THE DINING EXPERIENCE — Absolute ground for refusal — No distinctive character — Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 — Article 7(1)(b) — Dismissal of application.
