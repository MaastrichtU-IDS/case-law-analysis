Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 6 September 2006.   Portuguese Republic v Commission of the European Communities.   Action for annulment - State aid -Decision 2003/442/EC- Tax measures adopted by a regional or local authority - Reductions on the rate of income tax for natural and legal persons having their tax residence in the Azores - Classification as State aid - Selective nature - Justification by the nature and overall structure of the tax system - Obligation to state reasons - Compatibility with the common market.
