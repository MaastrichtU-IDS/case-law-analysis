Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 29 March 2007. Aktiebolaget NN v Skatteverket. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Regeringsrätten - Sweden. Sixth VAT Directive - Supply of goods - Article 8(1)(a) - Fibre-optic cable between two Member States running in part outside Community territory - Tax jurisdiction of each Member State limited to the length of cable installed on its territory - Non-taxation of the part lying in the exclusive economic zone; on the continental shelf or on the seabed.
