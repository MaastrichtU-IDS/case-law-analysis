Order of the Vice-President of the Court of 28 November 2013.   European Medicines Agency (EMA) v InterMune UK Ltd and Others.   Appeal - Order for interim measures - Regulation (EC) No 1049/2001 - Access to documents of the institutions - Documents held by the European Medicines Agency in the context of an application for authorisation to place a medicinal product on the market - Decision to grant a third party access to the documents - Information not available to the public - Serious and irreparable damage - Evidence.
