Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 13 July 2017. Ccc Event Management GmbH v Court of Justice of the European Union. Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Actions for damages — Alleged non-compliance with EU law of the Austrian legislation in respect of gambling contributions — Failure; by the national courts or tribunals; to refer the matter to the Court for a preliminary ruling — Manifest lack of jurisdiction of the EU judicature.
