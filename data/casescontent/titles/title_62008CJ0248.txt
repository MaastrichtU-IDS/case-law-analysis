Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 17 December 2009.   European Commission v Hellenic Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Regulation (EC) No 1774/2002 - Articles 4(2)(a) and (c); 5(2)(c); 6(2)(b); 10 to 15; 17; 18 and 26 - Animal by-products - Waste - Burial without prior treatment - Lack of official controls - Plants for the safe management of animal by-products - Operation - Lack of authorisation - Incineration of specified risk materials - No adequate process.
