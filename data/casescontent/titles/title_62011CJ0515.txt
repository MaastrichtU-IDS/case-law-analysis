Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 18 July 2013.   Deutsche Umwelthilfe eV v Bundesrepublik Deutschland.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgericht Berlin - Germany.   Public access to environmental information - Directive 2003/4/EC - Power of the Member States to exclude bodies acting in a legislative capacity from the definition of ‘public authority’ under that directive - Limits.
