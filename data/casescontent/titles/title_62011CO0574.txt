Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 9 February 2012.   Novartis AG v Actavis Deutschland GmbH &amp; Co. KG and Actavis Ltd.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landgericht Düsseldorf - Germany.   The first subparagraph of Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Medicinal products for human use - Supplementary protection certificate - Regulation (EC) No 469/2009 - Articles 4 and 5 - Sole active ingredient for which such a certificate is granted - Scope of protection - Medicinal product containing more than one active ingredient; including the active ingredient which is the subject of a certificate.
