Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 28 October 1999.   Commission of the European Communities v Hellenic Republic.   Failure by a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Article 119 of the EC Treaty (Articles 117 to 120 of the EC Treaty have been replaced by Articles 136 EC to 143 EC) - Directives 75/117/EEC and 79/7/EEC - Equal pay for men and women - Family and marriage allowances - Old-age pensions - Calculation - Failure to abolish discriminatory conditions retroactively.
