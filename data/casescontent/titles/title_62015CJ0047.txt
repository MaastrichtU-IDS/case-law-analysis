Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 7 June 2016. Sélina Affum v Préfet du Pas-de-Calais and Procureur général de la Cour d'appel de Douai. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Cour de cassation. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Area of freedom; security and justice — Directive 2008/115/EC — Common standards and procedures for returning illegally staying third-country nationals — Police custody — National legislation providing for a sentence of imprisonment in the event of illegal entry — Situation of ‘transit’ — Multilateral readmission arrangement.
