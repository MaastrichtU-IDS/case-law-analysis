Judgment of the Court of 14 December 1995.   Ursula Megner and Hildegard Scheffel v Innungskrankenkasse Vorderpfalz; now Innungskrankenkasse Rheinhessen-Pfalz.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Sozialgericht Speyer - Germany.   Equal treatment for men and women in matters of social security - Article 4(1) of Directive 79/7/EEC - Minor and short-term employment - Exclusion from compulsory old-age insurance and sickness insurance and from the obligation to pay unemployment insurance contributions.
