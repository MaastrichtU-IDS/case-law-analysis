Judgment of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 19 April 2018. Fiesta Hotels &amp; Resorts; SL v European Union Intellectual Property Office. Appeal — EU trade mark — Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 — Article 8(4) — Article 65 — Unregistered trade name GRAND HOTEL PALLADIUM — Figurative mark containing the word elements ‘PALLADIUM PALACE IBIZA RESORT &amp; SPA’ — Application for a declaration of invalidity based on a prior right acquired pursuant to national law — Conditions — Sign of more than merely local significance — Right to prohibit the use of a subsequent trade mark.
