Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 14 October 2010.   Gudrun Schwemmer v Agentur für Arbeit Villingen-Schwenningen - Familienkasse.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany.   Social security - Regulations (EEC) Nos 1408/71 and 574/72 - Family benefits - ‘Anti-overlap’ rules - Article 76(2) of Regulation No 1408/71 - Article 10(1)(a) of Regulation No 574/72 - Children residing in a Member State with their mother who fulfils the conditions for drawing family benefits there; and the father of whom; working in Switzerland and fulfilling; at first sight; the conditions for drawing family benefits of the same type under Swiss legislation; refrains from applying for the grant of those benefits.
