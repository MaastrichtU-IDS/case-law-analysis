Request for a preliminary ruling from the Krajowa Izba Odwoławcza. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Public procurement — Principles of awarding contracts — Equal treatment of tenderers — Duration of validity of the tender and of the deposit — Extension not explicitly provided for — Exclusion from the tender procedure.
