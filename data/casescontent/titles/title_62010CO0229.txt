Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 21 March 2013.   Maria Alice Pendão Lapa Costa Ferreira and Alexandra Pendão Lapa Ferreira v Companhia de Seguros Tranquilidade SA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal Cível da Comarca do Porto - Portugal.   Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure - Insurance against civil liability in respect of the use of motor vehicles - Directives 72/166/EEC; 84/5/EEC and 90/232/EEC - Right to compensation by means of compulsory insurance against civil liability in respect of the use of motor vehicles - Civil liability of the insured person - Victim’s contribution to loss or injury - Exclusion or limitation of the right to compensation.
