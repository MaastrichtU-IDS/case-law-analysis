Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 17 March 2005. Karl Robert Kranemann v Land Nordrhein-Westfalen. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesverwaltungsgericht - Germany. Article 48 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Article 39 EC) - Freedom of movement for workers - Civil servant undergoing preparatory practical training - Practical training completed in another Member State - Reimbursement of travel expenses limited to the domestic stretch of the journey.
