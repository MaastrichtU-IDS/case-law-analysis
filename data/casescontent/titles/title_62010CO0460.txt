Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 14 April 2011.   Luigi Marcuccio v Court of Justice of the European Union.   Appeal - Non-contractual liabity - Representation of the appellant - Lawyer not authorised to act - Service of an appeal - Claim for damages - Court of Justice of the European Union - Rejection - Action for annulment - Harm allegedly suffered - Action for damages - Appeal in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly unfounded.
