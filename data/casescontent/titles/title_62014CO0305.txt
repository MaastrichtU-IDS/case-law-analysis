Order of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 10 February 2015. Sergiu Lucian Băbășan v Inspectoratul Județean de Poliție Satu Mare. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunalul Satu Mare. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — National legislation according to which prior authorisation is required for the organisation of a public meeting — Failure to implement EU law — Manifest lack of jurisdiction of the Court of Justice.
