Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 21 February 2013.   Ministero per i beni e le attività culturali and Others v Ordine degli Ingegneri di Verona e Provincia and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Consiglio di Stato - Italy.   Directive 85/384/EEC - Mutual recognition of qualifications in the field of architecture - Articles 10 and 11(g) - National legislation recognising equivalence of qualifications in architecture and civil engineering; but reserving work on classified heritage buildings to architects - Principle of equal treatment - Situation purely internal to a Member State.
