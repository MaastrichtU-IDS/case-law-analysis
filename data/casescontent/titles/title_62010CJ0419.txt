Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 26 April 2012. Wolfgang Hofmann v Freistaat Bayern. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Bayerischer Verwaltungsgerichtshof. Directive 2006/126/EC — Mutual recognition of driving licences — Refusal by a Member State to recognise; in favour of a person whose driving licence was withdrawn on its territory; the validity of a driving licence issued by another Member State.
