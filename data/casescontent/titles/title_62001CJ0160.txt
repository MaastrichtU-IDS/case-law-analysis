Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 15 May 2003.   Karen Mau v Bundesanstalt für Arbeit.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Sozialgericht Leipzig - Germany.   Council Directive 80/987/EEC - National legislation fixing the final date for the guarantee period as that of the decision to open the procedure for the collective settlement of claims where the employment relationship still exists at that date - Article 141 EC - Indirect discrimination against female employees on child raising leave - Liability of a Member State in the event of infringement of Community law.
