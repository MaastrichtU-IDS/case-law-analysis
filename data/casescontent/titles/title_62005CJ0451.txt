Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 11 October 2007.   Européenne et Luxembourgeoise d’investissements SA (ELISA) v Directeur général des impôts and Ministère public.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour de cassation - France.   Direct taxation - Tax on the commercial value of immovable property in France owned by legal persons - Holding companies under Luxembourg law - Refusal of exemption - Directive 77/799/EEC - Non-exhaustive list of specified taxes and duties - Tax similar in nature - Limits to exchange of information - Bilateral convention - Article 73b of the EC Treaty (now Article 56 EC) - Free movement of capital - Combating tax evasion.
