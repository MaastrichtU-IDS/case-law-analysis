Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 29 September 2010. VAV-Autovermietung GmbH v Inspecteur van de Belastingdienst/Douane Zuid; kantoor Roosendaal. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Rechtbank Breda - Netherlands. First subparagraph of Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Freedom to provide services - Articles 49 EC to 55 EC - Motor vehicles - Use in one Member State of a motor vehicle registered and hired in another Member State - Taxation of that vehicle in the first Member State upon its first use on the national road network.
