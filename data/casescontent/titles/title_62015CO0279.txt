Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 21 April 2016.   Alexandre Borde and Carbonium v European Commission.   Appeal - Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court - Intra-ACP programme (African; Caribbean and Pacific States) of the Global Climate Change Alliance (GCCA) - European Commission’s request to terminate the mission of an expert chosen by the other contracting party - Action for annulment - Right to an effective judicial remedy.
