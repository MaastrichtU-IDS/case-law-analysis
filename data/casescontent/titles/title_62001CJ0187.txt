Judgment of the Court of 11 February 2003. Criminal proceedings against Hüseyin Gözütok (C-187/01) and Klaus Brügge (C-385/01). References for a preliminary ruling: Oberlandesgericht Köln - Germany and Rechtbank van eerste aanleg te Veurne - Belgium. Convention implementing the Schengen Agreement - Ne bis in idem principle - Scope - Decisions by which the Public Prosecutor definitively discontinues criminal proceedings; without the involvement of a court; once the accused has satisfied certain conditions.
