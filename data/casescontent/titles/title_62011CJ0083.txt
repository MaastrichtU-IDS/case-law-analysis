Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber); 5 September 2012. Secretary of State for the Home Department v Muhammad Sazzadur Rahman and Others. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Upper Tribunal (Immigration and Asylum Chamber). Directive 2004/38/EC — Right of citizens of the Union and their family members to move and reside freely within the territory of the Member States — Article 3(2) — Obligation to facilitate; in accordance with national legislation; entry and residence for ‘any other family members’ who are dependants of a Union citizen.
