Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 22 November 2012.   European Commission v Federal Republic of Germany.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Free movement of capital - Taxation of dividends and interest paid to pension funds and pension insurance schemes - Treatment of dividends and interest paid to non-resident institutions - Deduction of operating costs directly linked to the receipt of income in the form of dividends and interest - Burden of proof.
