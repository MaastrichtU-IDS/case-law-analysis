Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 16 December 1999.   Union Deutsche Lebensmittelwerke GmbH v Schutzverband gegen Unwesen in der Wirtschaft eV.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesgerichtshof - Germany.   Protection of designations used in marketing of milk and milk products - Regulation (EEC) No 1898/87 - Directive 89/398/EEC - Use of the designation "cheese" to describe a dietary product in which the natural fat has been replaced by vegetable fat.
