Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 21 June 2016. New Valmar BVBA v Global Pharmacies Partner Health Srl. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Rechtbank van Koophandel te Gent. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Free movement of goods — Prohibition of measures having equivalent effect to quantitative restrictions on exports — Article 35 TFEU — Company established in the Dutch-speaking region of the Kingdom of Belgium — Legislation requiring invoices to be drawn up in Dutch; failing which they are null and void — Cross-border concession agreement — Restriction — Justification — Disproportionate.
