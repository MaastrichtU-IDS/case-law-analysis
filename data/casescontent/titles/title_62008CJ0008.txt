Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 4 June 2009. T-Mobile Netherlands BV; KPN Mobile NV; Orange Nederland NV and Vodafone Libertel NV v Raad van bestuur van de Nederlandse Mededingingsautoriteit. Reference for a preliminary ruling: College van Beroep voor het bedrijfsleven - Netherlands. Reference for a preliminary ruling - Article 81(1) EC - Concept of ‘concerted practice’ - Causal connection between concerted action and the market conduct of undertakings - Appraisal in accordance with the rules of national law - Whether a single meeting is sufficient or whether concerted action on a regular basis over a long period is necessary.
