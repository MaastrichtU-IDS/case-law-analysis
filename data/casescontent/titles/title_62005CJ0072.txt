Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 14 September 2006. Hausgemeinschaft Jörg und Stefanie Wollny v Finanzamt Landshut. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht München - Germany. Sixth VAT Directive - Article 11A(1)(c) - Use of property forming part of the assets of a business for private purposes by a taxable person - Treatment of that use as a supply of services for consideration - Determination of the taxable amount - Definition of full cost to the taxable person of providing those services.
