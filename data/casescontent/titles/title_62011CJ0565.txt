Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 18 April 2013.   Mariana Irimie v Administraţia Finanţelor Publice Sibiu and Administraţia Fondului pentru Mediu.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunalul Sibiu - Romania.   Repayment of taxes levied by a Member State in breach of European Union law - National system limiting the interest payable by the Member State on the repaid tax - Interest calculated from the day following the date of the claim for repayment of the tax - Non-compliance with European Union law - Principle of effectiveness.
