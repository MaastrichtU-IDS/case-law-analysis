Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 8 November 2012. Staatssecretaris van Financiën v Gemeente Vlaardingen. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden. Taxation — VAT — Taxable transactions — Application for the purposes of a business of goods obtained ‘in the course of such business’ — Treatment as a supply for consideration — Sports pitches belonging to the taxable person and transformed by a third person.
