Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 26 November 2015. MedEval - Qualitäts-; Leistungs- und Struktur-Evaluierung im Gesundheitswesen GmbH. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Verwaltungsgerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Public procurement — Directive 89/665/EEC — Principles of effectiveness and equivalence — Review procedures concerning the award of public contracts — Period allowed for commencing proceedings — National legislation making an action for damages subject to a precondition that the procedure be declared unlawful — Limitation period which starts to run irrespective of the applicant’s knowledge of the unlawfulness.
