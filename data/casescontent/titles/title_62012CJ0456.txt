Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber); 12 March 2014. O. v Minister voor Immigratie; Integratie en Asiel and Minister voor Immigratie; Integratie en Asiel v B. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Raad van State (Netherlands). Directive 2004/38/EC — Article 21(1) TFEU — Right to move and reside freely within the territory of the Member States — Beneficiaries — Right of residence of a third-country national who is a family member of a Union citizen in the Member State of which that citizen is a national — Return of the Union citizen to that Member State after short periods of residence spent in another Member State.
