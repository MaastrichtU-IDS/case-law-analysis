Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 1 February 2005.   Commission of the European Communities v Republic of Austria.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Articles 249 EC and 307 EC - Articles 2 and 3 of Directive 76/207/EEC - Equal treatment for men and women - Prohibition of the employment of women in underground work in mining or in a high-pressure atmosphere or in diving work.
