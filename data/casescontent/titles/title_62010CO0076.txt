Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 16 November 2010. Pohotovosť s.r.o. v Iveta Korčkovská. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Krajský súd v Prešove - Slovakia. Preliminary ruling – Consumer protection – Directive 93/13/EEC – Unfair terms – Directive 2008/48/EC – Directive 87/102/EEC – Consumer credit contracts – Annual percentage rate of charge – Arbitration proceedings – Arbitration award – Power of the national court to examine of its own motion whether certain terms are unfair.
