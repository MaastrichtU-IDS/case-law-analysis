Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 14 June 2011. Paul Miles and Others v Écoles européennes. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Chambre de recours des écoles européennes. Reference for a preliminary ruling - Concept of ‘court or tribunal of a Member State’ within the meaning of Article 267 TFEU - Complaints Board of the European Schools - System of remuneration of teachers seconded to the European Schools - No adjustment of remuneration following depreciation in sterling - Compatibility with Articles 18 TFEU and 45 TFEU.
