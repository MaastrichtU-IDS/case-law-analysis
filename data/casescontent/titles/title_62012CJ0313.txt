Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber); 7 November 2013. Giuseppa Romeo v Regione Siciliana. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Corte dei conti; sezione giurisdizionale per la Regione Siciliana. National administrative procedure — Purely internal situation — Administrative acts — Obligation to state reasons — Whether it is possible for failure to state reasons to be remedied during legal proceedings against an administrative act — Interpretation of the second paragraph of Article 296 TFEU and Article 41(2)(c) of the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
