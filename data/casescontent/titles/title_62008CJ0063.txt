Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 29 October 2009.   Virginie Pontin v T-Comalux SA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal du travail d’Esch-sur-Alzette - Luxembourg.   Social policy - Protection of pregnant workers and workers who have recently given birth or are breastfeeding - Directive 92/85/EEC - Articles 10 and 12 - Prohibition of dismissal from the beginning of pregnancy until the end of maternity leave - Judicial protection of rights enjoyed by individuals under Community law - Equal treatment for men and women - Directive 76/207/EEC - Article 2(7); third subparagraph - Less favourable treatment of a woman related to pregnancy or maternity leave - Restriction of remedies available to women dismissed during pregnancy.
