Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 22 September 2011. Richard Lebrun and Marcelle Howet v Belgian State. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal de première instance de Liège - Belgium. Articles 92(1); 103(1); 104(3); first subparagraph; of the Rules of Procedure - Reference for a preliminary ruling - Examination of the conformity of a national provision with both European Union law and the national constitution - National legislation granting priority to an interlocutory procedure for the review of constitutionality - Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union - Requirement of link with European Union law - Clear absence of jurisdiction of the Court.
