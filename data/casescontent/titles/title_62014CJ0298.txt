Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 6 October 2015. Alain Laurent Brouillard v Jury du concours de recrutement de référendaires près la Cour de cassation and État belge. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Conseil d'État (Belgium). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Freedom of movement of persons — Articles 45 TFEU and 49 TFEU — Workers — Employment in the public service — Directive 2005/36/EC — Recognition of professional qualifications — Definition of ‘regulated profession’ — Admission to a competition to recruit legal secretaries at the Cour de cassation (Belgium).
