Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 7 June 2012.   VBV - Vorsorgekasse AG v Finanzmarktaufsichtsbehörde (FMA).   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Free movement of capital - Articles 63 TFEU and 65 TFEU - Severance funds - Investment of assets - Investment funds established in another Member State - Investment in such funds permitted only when they are authorised to market their units within the national territory.
