Order of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 7 July 2016. H. M. v Agentsia za darzhavna finansova inspektsia (ADFI). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Public supply contracts — Directive 2004/18/EC — Article 1(9) — Concept of ‘body governed by public law’ — Hospital set up to make a profit; whose capital is entirely private — Over 50% or 30% of income coming from payments by the public sickness insurance scheme in exchange for the provision of medical services — Article 7(b) — Estimated value of the contract — Threshold not met — Certain cross-border interest — Lack of information — Article 53(2) of the Court’s Rules of Procedure — Manifest inadmissibility.
