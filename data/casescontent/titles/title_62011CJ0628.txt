Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber); 18 March 2014. Criminal proceedings against International Jet Management GmbH. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberlandesgericht Braunschweig. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 18 TFEU — Prohibition of any discrimination on the ground of nationality — Commercial flights from a third State to a Member State — Legislation of a Member State providing that European Union air carriers not having an operating licence issued by that State must obtain an authorisation for each flight from a third State.
