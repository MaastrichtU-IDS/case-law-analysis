Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 15 November 2012. Finanzamt Steglitz v Ines Zimmermann. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesfinanzhof. Sixth VAT Directive – Exemptions – Article 13A(1)(g) and (2) – Services closely linked to welfare and social security work supplied by bodies governed by public law or organisations recognised as charitable – Recognition – Conditions not applicable to organisations other than bodies governed by public law – Discretion of the Member States – Limits – Principle of fiscal neutrality.
