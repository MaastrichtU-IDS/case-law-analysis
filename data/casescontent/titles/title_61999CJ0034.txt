Judgment of the Court of 15 May 2001.   Commissioners of Customs &amp; Excise v Primback Ltd.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: House of Lords - United Kingdom.   Value added tax - Sixth Directive 77/388/EEC - Taxable amount - Retail credit sales of goods - Credit granted by a person other than the seller and at no cost to the customer - Payment by finance company to the seller of less than the price of the goods.
