Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 11 December 2008.   Gateway; Inc. v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM) and Fujitsu Siemens Computers GmbH.   Appeal - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 8(1)(b) and (5) - Earlier marks including the word sign ‘GATEWAY’ - Word sign ‘ACTIVY Media Gateway’ - Absence of similarity of the signs - Lack of likelihood of confusion - Taking into account of the renown of earlier marks when carrying out a global assessment of opposing signs.
