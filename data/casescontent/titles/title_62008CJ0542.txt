Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 15 April 2010.   Friedrich G. Barth v Bundesministerium für Wissenschaft und Forschung.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Freedom of movement for persons - Workers - Equal treatment - Special length-of-service increment for university professors provided for by national legislation held to be incompatible with Community law by a judgment of the Court - Limitation period - Principles of equivalence and effectiveness.
