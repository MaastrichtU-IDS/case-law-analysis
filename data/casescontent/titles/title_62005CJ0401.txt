Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 14 December 2006.   VDP Dental Laboratory NV v Staatssecretaris van Financiën.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands.   Sixth VAT Directive - Exemptions - Article 13A(1)(e) - Scope of the exemption - Manufacture and repair of dental prostheses by an intermediary who does not have the status of dentist or dental technician - Subcontracting to a dental technician.
