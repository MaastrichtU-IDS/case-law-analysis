Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 17 July 2014.   Marjan Noorzia v Bundesministerin für Inneres.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Request for a preliminary ruling - Right to family reunification - Directive 2003/86/EC - Article 4(5) - Provision of national law under which the sponsor and his/her spouse must have reached the age of 21 by the date on which the application for family reunification is lodged - Interpretation in conformity with EU law.
