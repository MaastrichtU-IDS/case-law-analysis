Judgment of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 21 September 2017. Halina Socha and Others v Szpital Specjalistyczny im. A. Falkiewicza we Wrocławiu. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Sąd Rejonowy dla Wrocławia-Śródmieścia. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Social policy — Collective redundancies — Directive 98/59/EC — Article 1(1) — Concept of ‘redundancies’ — Assimilation to redundancies of ‘terminations of an employment contract which occur on the employer’s initiative’ — Unilateral amendment by the employer of pay and working conditions.
