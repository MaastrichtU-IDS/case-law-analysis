Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 12 February 2015. Claudia Concu and Isabella Melis. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale di Cagliari. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure — Identical questions referred — Articles 49 TFEU and 56 TFEU — Freedom of establishment — Freedom to provide services — Games of chance — National legislation — Reorganisation of the licencing system through an alignment of licence expiry dates — New call for tenders — Licences with a shorter period than that of licences awarded in the past — Restriction — Overriding reasons of general interest — Proportionality.
