Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber); 27 March 2012. Post Danmark A/S v Konkurrencerådet. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Højesteret. Article 82 EC — Postal undertaking with a dominant position and subject to a universal service obligation with regard to certain addressed mail — Low prices charged to certain former customers of a competitor — No evidence relating to intention — Price discrimination — Selectively low prices — Actual or likely exclusion of a competitor — Effect on competition and; thereby; on consumers — Objective justification.
