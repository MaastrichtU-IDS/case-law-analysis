Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 29 November 2011. National Grid Indus BV v Inspecteur van de Belastingdienst Rijnmond/kantoor Rotterdam. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Gerechtshof Amsterdam - Netherlands. Transfer of a company’s place of effective management to a Member State other than that in which it is incorporated - Freedom of establishment - Article 49 TFEU - Taxation of unrealised capital gains relating to the assets of a company transferring its place of management between Member States - Determination of the amount of tax at the time of the transfer of the place of management - Immediate recovery of the tax - Proportionality.
