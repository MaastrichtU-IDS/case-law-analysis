Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 27 April 2017. Michael Tibor Bachman v FAER IFN SA. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunalul Specializat Mureș. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court — Consumer protection — Directive 93/13/EEC — Article 2(b) — Unfair terms in consumer contracts — Notion of ‘consumer’ — Natural person having concluded an agreement for novation with a credit institution in order to meet repayment obligations to that institution in respect of credit obtained by a commercial company.
