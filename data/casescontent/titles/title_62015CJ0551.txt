Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 9 March 2017. Pula Parking d.o.o. v Sven Klaus Tederahn. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Općinski sud u Puli-Pola. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Judicial cooperation in civil matters — Regulation (EU) No 1215/2012 — Temporal and material scope — Civil and commercial matters — Enforcement proceedings relating to the recovery of an unpaid public parking debt — Included — Concept of ‘court’ — Notary who has issued a writ of execution based on an ‘authentic document’.
