Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 12 July 2012. Südzucker AG and Others v Hauptzollamt Hamburg-Jonas. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Finanzgericht Hamburg. Agriculture — Export refunds — Incorrect indication of the exporter in the export declaration — National legislation making entitlement to an export refund subject to the entry of the applicant as the exporter in the export declaration — Correction of the export declaration after the goods have been released.
