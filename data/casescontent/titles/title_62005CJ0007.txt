Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 8 June 2006. Saatgut-Treuhandverwaltungs GmbH v Ulrich Deppe and Others (C-7/05); Siegfried Hennings (C-8/05) and Hartmut Lübbe (C-9/05). Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesgerichtshof - Germany. Plant varieties - Level of equitable remuneration to be paid to the holder of a Community plant variety right - Article 5(2); (4) and (5) of Regulation (EC) No 1768/95; as amended by Regulation (EC) No 2605/98 - Concept of "level of remuneration sensibly lower than the amount charged for the licensed production of propagating material'.
