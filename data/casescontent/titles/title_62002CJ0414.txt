Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 23 September 2004. Spedition Ulustrans; Uluslararasi Nakliyat ve. Tic. A.S. Istanbul v Finanzlandesdirektion für Oberösterreich. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria. Community Customs Code - Article 202 - Accrual of the customs debt - Unlawful introduction into the Community customs territory - Meaning of "debtor" of such a debt - Extension to the employer of liability for the debt of an employee who has committed irregularities in the performance of customs obligations.
