Judgment of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 14 January 2016. European Commission v Hellenic Republic. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations — Freedom to provide services — Motor vehicles — Leasing or hiring of a motor vehicle by a resident of a Member State from a supplier established in another Member State — Taxation of that vehicle at the time it is registered in the first Member State — Levy of the full amount of vehicle registration tax.
