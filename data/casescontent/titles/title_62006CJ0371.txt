Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 20 September 2007. Benetton Group SpA v G-Star International BV. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands. Trade marks - Directive 89/104/EEC - Article 3(1)(e); third indent; and Article 3(3) - Sign - Shape which gives substantial value to goods - Use - Advertising campaigns - Attractiveness of a shape acquired prior to the date of application for registration on account of recognition of it as a distinctive sign.
