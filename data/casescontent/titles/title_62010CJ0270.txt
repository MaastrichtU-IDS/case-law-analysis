Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 28 July 2011.   Lotta Gistö.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Korkein hallinto-oikeus - Finland.   Protocol on the Privileges and Immunities of the European Communities - Article 14; first paragraph - Determination of the domicile for tax purposes of the spouse of a European Union official - National legislation under which a person who has lived abroad for three years is no longer regarded as resident in the country and thus no longer subject to general tax liability.
