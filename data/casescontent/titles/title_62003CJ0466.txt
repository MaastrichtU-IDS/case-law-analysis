Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 28 June 2007. Albert Reiss Beteiligungsgesellschaft mbH v Land Baden-Württemberg. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landgericht Baden-Baden - Germany. Directive 69/335/EEC - Indirect taxes on the raising of capital - National provisions which provide for notarial fees to be charged for the authentication of the transfer of shares in limited liability companies - Tax decision - Classification as a ‘duty similar to capital duty’ - Prior formality - Duties on the transfer of securities - Duties paid by way of fees or dues.
