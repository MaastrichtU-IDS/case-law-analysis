Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 21 November 2013. Deutsche Lufthansa AG v Flughafen Frankfurt-Hahn GmbH. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberlandesgericht Koblenz. State aid — Articles 107 TFEU and 108 TFEU — Benefits granted by a public airport operator to a low-cost airline — Decision to initiate a formal investigation procedure in respect of that measure — Obligation of Member States’ courts to abide by the Commission’s assessment in that decision concerning the existence of aid.
