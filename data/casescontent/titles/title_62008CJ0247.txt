Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 1 October 2009.   Gaz de France - Berliner Investissement SA v Bundeszentralamt für Steuern.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Köln - Germany.   Free movement of capital - Profits distributed to a parent company exempt from withholding tax in the Member State of the subsidiary - Concept of ‘company of a Member State’ - ‘Société par actions simplifiée’ under French law.
