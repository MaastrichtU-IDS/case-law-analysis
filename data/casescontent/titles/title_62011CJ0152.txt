Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 6 December 2012. Johann Odar v Baxter Deutschland GmbH. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Arbeitsgericht München. Equal treatment in employment and occupation – Directive 2000/78/EC – Prohibition against any discrimination on grounds of age or disability – Compensation on termination of employment – Social plan providing for a reduction in the amount of redundancy compensation paid to disabled workers.
