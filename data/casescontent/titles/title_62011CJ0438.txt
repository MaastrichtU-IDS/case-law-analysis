Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 8 November 2012.   Lagura Vermögensverwaltung GmbH v Hauptzollamt Hamburg-Hafen.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Hamburg - Germany.   Community customs code - Article 220(2)(b) - Post-clearance recovery of import duties - Legitimate expectations - Impossibility of verifying the accuracy of a certificate of origin - Notion of ‘certificate based on an incorrect account of the facts provided by the exporter’ - Burden of proof - Scheme of generalised tariff preferences.
