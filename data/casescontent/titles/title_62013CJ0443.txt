Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber); 13 November 2014. Ute Reindl v Bezirkshauptmannschaft Innsbruck. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Unabhängiger Verwaltungssenat in Tirol. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Approximation of laws on animal health — Regulation (EC) No 2073/2005 — Annex I — Microbiological criteria applicable to foodstuffs — Salmonella in fresh poultry meat — Failure to comply with microbiological criteria found at the distribution stage — National legislation imposing a penalty on a food business operator active only at the stage of retail sale — Compatibility with EU law — Effective; dissuasive and proportionate nature of the penalty.
