Order of the Court (Third Chamber) of 17 January 2012.   Infopaq International A/S v Danske Dagblades Forening.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Højesteret - Denmark.   Copyright - Information society - Directive 2001/29/EC - Article 5(1) and (5) - Literary and artistic works - Reproduction of short extracts of literary works - Newspaper articles - Temporary and transient reproductions - Technological process consisting in scanning of articles followed by conversion into text file; electronic processing of the reproduction and storage of part of that reproduction - Acts of temporary reproduction which form an integral and essential part of such a technological process - Purpose of those acts being the lawful use of a work or protected subject-matter - Independent economic significance of those acts.
