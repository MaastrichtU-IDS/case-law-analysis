Order of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 25 September 2015. Openbaar Ministerie v A. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Rechtbank Amsterdam. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Urgent preliminary-ruling procedure — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Police and judicial cooperation in criminal matters — Framework Decision 2002/584/JHA — European arrest warrant — Article 2(4) and Article 4.1 — Conditions of execution — National criminal law making the execution of a European arrest warrant subject to; in addition to double criminality; the condition that the criminal act is punishable by a custodial sentence or a detention order for a maximum period of at least 12 months under the law of the executing Member State.
