Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 8 September 2015. Criminal proceedings against Ivo Taricco and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale di Cuneo. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Criminal proceedings concerning offences in relation to value added tax (VAT) — Article 325 TFEU — National legislation laying down absolute limitation periods which may give rise to impunity in respect of offences — Potential prejudice to the financial interest of the European Union — Obligation; for the national court; to disapply any provision of national law liable to affect fulfilment of the Member States’ obligations under EU law.
