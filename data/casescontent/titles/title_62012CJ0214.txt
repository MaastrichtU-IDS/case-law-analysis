Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 24 October 2013.   Land Burgenland (C-214/12 P); Grazer Wechselseitige Versicherung AG (C-215/12 P) and Republic of Austria (C-223/12 P) v European Commission.   Appeal - Competition - State aid - Aid declared illegal and incompatible with the common market - Aid granted to the Grazer Wechselseitige group (GRAWE) at the time of the privatisation of Bank Burgenland - Determination of the market price - Tender procedure - Unlawful conditions with no impact on the highest offer - ‘Private vendor’ test - Distinction between a State’s obligations in cases where it acts as a public authority and where it acts as a shareholder - Distortion of evidence - Obligation to state reasons.
