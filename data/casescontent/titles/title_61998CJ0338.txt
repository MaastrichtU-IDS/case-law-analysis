Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 8 November 2001.   Commission of the European Communities v Kingdom of the Netherlands.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Articles 17(2)(a) and 18(1)(a) of the Sixth VAT Directive - National legislation allowing an employer to deduct; as input tax; a certain percentage of an allowance paid to an employee for business use of a private vehicle.
