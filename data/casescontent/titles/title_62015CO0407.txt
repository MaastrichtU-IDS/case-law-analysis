Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 18 February 2016. Ovidiu Rîpanu v Compania Națională « Loteria Română ». Request for a preliminary ruling from the Curtea de Apel Bacău. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 53(2) Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Right to fair legal process — Decision of a constitutional court — Binding effect — Publication in the official journal of the Member State concerned — No suspensive effect between delivery and publication — No implementation of EU law — Clear lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
