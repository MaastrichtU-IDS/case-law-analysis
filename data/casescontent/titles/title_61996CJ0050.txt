Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 10 February 2000.   Deutsche Telekom AG v Lilli Schröder.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landesarbeitsgericht Hamburg - Germany.   Equal pay for men and women - Article 119 of the EC Treaty (Articles 117 to 120 of the EC Treaty have been replaced by Articles 136 EC to 143 EC) - Protocol concerning Article 119 of the EC Treaty - Occupational social security schemes - Exclusion of part-time workers from a supplementary occupational retirement pension scheme - Retroactive membership - Entitlement to a pension - Relationship between national law and Community law.
