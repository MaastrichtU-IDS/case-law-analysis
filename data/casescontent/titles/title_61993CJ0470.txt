Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 6 July 1995.   Verein gegen Unwesen in Handel und Gewerbe Köln e.V. v Mars GmbH.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landgericht Köln - Germany.   Free movement of goods - Measures having an effect equivalent to quantitative restrictions - Presentation of a product likely to restrict freedom to fix retail prices and mislead the consumer.
