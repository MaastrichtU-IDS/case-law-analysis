Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 11 April 2013. Land Berlin v Ellen Mirjam Sapir and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesgerichtshof. Regulation (EC) No 44/2001 — Articles 1(1) and 6(1) — Concept of ‘civil and commercial matters’ — Undue payment made by a State entity — Claim for recovery of that payment in legal proceedings — Determination of the court having jurisdiction in the case where claims are connected — Close connection between the claims — Defendant domiciled in a non-member State.
