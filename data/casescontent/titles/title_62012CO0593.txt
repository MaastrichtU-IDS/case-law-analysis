Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 24 October 2013.   Lancôme parfums and beauté &amp; Cie v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Word mark Color Focus - Application for a declaration of invalidity made by the proprietor of the Community word mark Focus - Declaration of invalidity - Surrender - Article 149 of the Rules of Procedure - Appeal which has become devoid of purpose - No need to adjudicate.
