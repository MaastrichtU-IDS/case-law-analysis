Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 22 April 2010.   Zentralbetriebsrat der Landeskrankenhäuser Tirols v Land Tirol.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landesgericht Innsbruck - Austria.   Social policy - Framework agreements on part-time work and on fixed-term work - Disadvantageous provisions provided for by national legislation for contractual public servants working part-time; on a casual basis; or under a fixed-term contract - Principle of equal treatment.
