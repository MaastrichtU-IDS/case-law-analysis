Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 7 June 2005.   Vereniging voor Energie; Milieu en Water and Others v Directeur van de Dienst uitvoering en toezicht energie.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: College van Beroep voor het bedrijfsleven - Netherlands.   Internal market in electricity - Preferential access to the system for cross-border transmission of electricity - Undertaking previously responsible for the operation of services of general economic interest - Long-term contracts existing prior to the liberalisation of the market - Directive 96/92/EC - Principle of non-discrimination - Principles of the protection of legitimate expectation and of legal certainty.
