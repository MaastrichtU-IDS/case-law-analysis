Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 27 April 2006. Levi Strauss &amp; Co. v Casucci SpA. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour de cassation - Belgium. Trade marks - Directive 89/104/EEC - Article 5(1)(b) - Relevant time for assessing likelihood of confusion between a trade mark and a similar sign - Loss of distinctive character owing to conduct of the proprietor of the trade mark after use of the sign has commenced.
