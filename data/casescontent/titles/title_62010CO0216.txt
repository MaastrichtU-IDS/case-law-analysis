Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 25 November 2010.   Lufthansa AirPlus Servicekarten GmbH v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Articles 8(1)(b) and (5); 73; 74 and 79 - Figurative mark A+ - Opposition by the proprietor of the Community word mark AirPlus International - Opposition rejected.
