Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber); 20 March 2014. European Commission v Republic of Poland. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations — Registration of motor vehicles — Articles 34 TFEU and 36 TFEU — Directive 70/311/EEC — Directive 2007/46/EC — Driving on the right in a Member State — Obligation; for the purpose of registration; to reposition to the left-hand side the steering equipment of passenger vehicles positioned on the right-hand side.
