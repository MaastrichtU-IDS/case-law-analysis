Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 14 June 2007. Werner Haderer v Finanzamt Wilmersdorf. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany. Sixth VAT Directive - Exemptions - Article 13A(1)(j) - Tuition given privately by teachers and covering school or university education - Education provided in the context of courses organised by adult education centres - No direct contractual link with pupils.
