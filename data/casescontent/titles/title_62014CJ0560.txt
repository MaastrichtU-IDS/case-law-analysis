Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 9 February 2017. M v Minister for Justice and Equality Ireland and the Attorney General. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Supreme Court (Ireland). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Area of freedom; security and justice — Directive 2004/83/EC — Minimum standards for the qualification and status of third country nationals or stateless persons as refugees — Application for subsidiary protection — Lawfulness of the national procedure for examining an application for subsidiary protection made after the rejection of an application for refugee status — Right to be heard — Scope — Right to an interview — Right to call and cross-examine witnesses).
