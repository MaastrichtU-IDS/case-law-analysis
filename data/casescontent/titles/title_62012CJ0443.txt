Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 12 December 2013.   Actavis Group PTC EHF and Actavis UK Ltd v Sanofi.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: High Court of Justice (England &amp; Wales); Chancery Division - United Kingdom.   Medicinal products for human use - Supplementary protection certificate - Regulation (EC) No 469/2009 - Article 3 - Conditions for obtaining such a certificate - Successive marketing of two medicinal products containing; wholly or partially; the same active ingredient - Combination of active ingredients; one of which has already been marketed in the form of a medicinal product with a single active ingredient - Whether it is possible to obtain a number of certificates on the basis of the same patent and two marketing authorisations.
