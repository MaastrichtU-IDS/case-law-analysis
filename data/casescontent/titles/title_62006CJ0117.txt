Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 11 October 2007.   Gerda Möllendorf and Christiane Möllendorf-Niehuus.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Kammergericht Berlin - Germany.   Common foreign and security policy - Specific restrictive measures directed against persons and entities associated with Usama bin Laden; the Al-Qaida network and the Taliban - Freezing of funds and economic resources - Regulation (EC) No 881/2002 - Articles 2(3) and 4(1) - Prohibition on making economic resources available to persons listed in Annex I to that regulation - Scope of prohibition - Sale of immovable property - Contract concluded before inclusion of a buyer in the list in Annex I - Application for registration of the transfer of ownership in the Land Register subsequent to that inclusion.
