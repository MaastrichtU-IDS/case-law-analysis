Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 16 June 2011.   Logstor ROR Polska sp. z o.o. v Dyrektor Izby Skarbowej w Katowicach.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Wojewódzki Sąd Administracyjny w Gliwicach - Poland.   Taxation - Capital duty - Directive 69/335/EEC - Indirect taxes on the raising of capital - Taxation of a loan taken up by a capital company from a person entitled to a percentage of the profits of the same company - Right of a Member State to reintroduce a tax which was no longer in force at the date of its accession to the European Union.
