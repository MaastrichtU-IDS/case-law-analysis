Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 24 October 2002.   Criminal proceedings against Gottfried Linhart and Hans Biffl.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Approximation of laws - Articles 30 and 36 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Articles 28 EC and 30 EC) - Directive 76/768/EEC relating to cosmetic products - Directive 84/450/EEC concerning misleading advertising - National legislation laying down restrictions on advertising.
