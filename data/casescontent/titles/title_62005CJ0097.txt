Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 14 December 2006. Mohamed Gattoussi v Stadt Rüsselsheim. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgericht Darmstadt - Germany. Euro-Mediterranean Agreement - Tunisian worker with permission to remain in a Member State and to work there - Principle of non-discrimination as regards working conditions; remuneration and dismissal - Curtailment of the period of validity of the residence permit.
