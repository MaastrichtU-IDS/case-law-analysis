Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 28 April 2016. Matratzen Concord GmbH v European Union Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO). Appeal — Article 181 Rules of Procedure — EU trade mark — Word mark Matratzen Concord — Relative ground for refusal — Opposition from the proprietor of the national word mark MATRATZEN — Likelihood of confusion — Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 — Article 8(1)(b) — Refusal in part of the registration — Proof of use of the earlier mark — Article 42(2).
