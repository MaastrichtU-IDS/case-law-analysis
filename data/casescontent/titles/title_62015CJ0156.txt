Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 10 November 2016. ‘Private Equity Insurance Group’ SIA v ‘Swedbank’ AS. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Augstākā tiesa. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2002/47/EC — Scope — Definition of ‘financial collateral’; ‘relevant financial obligations’ and ‘provision’ of financial collateral — Whether it is possible to enforce financial collateral notwithstanding the commencement of insolvency proceedings — Current account agreement including a financial collateral clause.
