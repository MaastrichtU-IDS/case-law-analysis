Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 4 October 2012. Finnair Oyj v Timy Lassooy. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Korkein oikeus. Air transport — Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 — Compensation for passengers in the event of denied boarding — Concept of ‘denied boarding’ — Exclusion from characterisation as ‘denied boarding’ — Cancellation of a flight caused by a strike at the airport of departure — Rescheduling of flights after the cancelled flight — Right to compensation of the passengers on those flights.
