Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 9 January 2003.   Nani Givane and Others v Secretary of State for the Home Department.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Immigration Appeal Tribunal - United Kingdom.   Freedom of movement for workers - Regulation (EEC) No 1251/70 - Right of workers to remain in the territory of a Member State after having been employed in that State - Right of residence of members of the family of a deceased worker - Requirement of the worker's continuous residence for at least two years.
