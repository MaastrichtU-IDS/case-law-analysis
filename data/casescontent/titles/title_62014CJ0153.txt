Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 9 July 2015. Minister van Buitenlandse Zaken v K and A. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Raad van State. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2003/86/EC — Article 7(2) — Family reunification — Integration measures — National legislation requiring the family members of a third country national residing lawfully in that Member State to pass a civic integration exam in order to enter the territory of that Member State — Cost of such an exam — Compatibility.
