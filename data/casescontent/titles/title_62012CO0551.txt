Order of the Vice-President of the Court of 7 March 2013.   Électricité de France SA (EDF) v European Commission.   Appeal - Application for interim measures - Concentrations - European electricity market - Acquisition of control of Segebel SA by EDF - Decision declaring the concentration compatible with the common market; subject to compliance with the commitments given by EDF - Refusal by the Commission to grant EDF postponement of the deadline for compliance with some of those commitments - Concepts of urgency and of serious and irreparable harm.
