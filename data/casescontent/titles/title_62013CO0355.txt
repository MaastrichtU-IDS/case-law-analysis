Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 12 December 2013.   Umbra Packaging srl v Agenzia delle Entrate - Direzione Provinciale di Perugia.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Commissione tributaria regionale dell'Umbria - Italy.   Request for a preliminary ruling - Rules of Procedure - Articles 53(2) and 99 - Possible to infer the answer to a question referred for a preliminary ruling clearly from the case-law - Request manifestly inadmissible - Electronic communications networks and services - Directive 2002/20/EC (Authorisation Directive) - Article 3 - Imposition of a government authorisation charge when a telephone subscription is taken out - Charge not applied to the use of prepaid telephone cards - Article 102 TFEU.
