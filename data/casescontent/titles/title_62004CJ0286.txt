Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 30 June 2005.   Eurocermex SA v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Article 7(1)(b) and (3) of Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Three-dimensional shape of a long-neck bottle in the neck of which a slice of lemon has been plugged - Absolute ground for refusal - Distinctive character.
