Appeal — Consumer protection — Regulation (EC) No 1924/2006 — Health claims made on foods; other than those referring to the reduction of disease risk and to children’s development and health — Dismissal of the application to include certain claims despite the positive advice of the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA).
