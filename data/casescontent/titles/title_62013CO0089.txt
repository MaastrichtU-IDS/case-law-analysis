Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 30 April 2014.   Luigi D’Aniello and Others v Poste Italiane SpA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale di Napoli - Italy.   Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court - Reference for a preliminary ruling - Social policy - Directive 1999/70/EC - Principle of non-discrimination - National legislation providing for a compensation scheme in cases of the unlawful setting of an expiry date for an employment contract different from that in cases where an employment contract of unlimited duration is unlawfully terminated - Economic consequences - Comparability of the claims.
