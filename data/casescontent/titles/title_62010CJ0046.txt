Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 14 July 2011.   Viking Gas A/S v Kosan Gas A/S.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Højesteret - Denmark.   Trade marks - Directive 89/104/EEC - Articles 5 and 7 - Gas bottles protected as a three-dimensional mark - Placing on the market by an exclusive licensee - Business activity of a competitor of the licensee consisting in the refilling of those bottles.
