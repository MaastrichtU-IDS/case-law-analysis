Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 7 June 2012.   Anton Vinkov v Nachalnik Administrativno-nakazatelna deynost.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Administrativen sad Sofia-grad - Bulgaria.   Reference for a preliminary ruling - Non-recognition in national law of the right to a judicial remedy in respect of decisions imposing a financial penalty and the deduction of points for certain breaches of road traffic regulations - Purely internal situation - Inadmissibility of the reference.
