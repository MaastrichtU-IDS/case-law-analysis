Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 15 June 2017. Saale Kareda v Stefan Benkö. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberster Gerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Jurisdiction in civil and commercial matters — Regulation (EU) No 1215/2012 — Article 7(1) — Concepts of ‘matters relating to a contract’ and of a ‘contract for the provision of services’ — Recourse claim between jointly and severally liable debtors under a credit agreement — Determination of the place of performance of the credit agreement.
