Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 17 January 2008.   Lammers &amp; Van Cleeff NV v Belgische Staat.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Rechtbank van eerste aanleg te Antwerpen - Belgium.   Freedom of establishment - Free movement of capital - Tax legislation - Corporation tax - Interest paid by a subsidiary on funds lent by the parent company established in another Member State - Reclassification of the interest as taxable dividends - No such reclassification where interest payments are made to a resident company.
