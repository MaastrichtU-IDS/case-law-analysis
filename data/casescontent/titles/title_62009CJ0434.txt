Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 5 May 2011. Shirley McCarthy v Secretary of State for the Home Department. Reference for a preliminary ruling from null. Freedom of movement for persons – Article 21 TFEU – Directive 2004/38/EC – ‘Beneficiary’ – Article 3(1) – National who has never made use of his right of free movement and has always resided in the Member State of his nationality – Effect of being a national of another Member State – Purely internal situation.
