Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 14 July 2016. Maria Cristina Elisabetta Ornano v Ministero della Giustizia; Direzione Generale dei Magistrati del Ministero. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Consiglio di Stato. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Social policy — Article 119 of the EC Treaty (subsequently Article 141 EC) — Directive 75/117/EEC — Equal pay for men and women — Article 1 — Directive 92/85/EEC — Measures to improve the safety and health at work of pregnant workers and workers who have recently given birth or are breastfeeding — Article 11(2)(b) and Article 11(3) — National law providing for an allowance for ordinary magistrates in respect of the expenses they incur in the performance of their professional functions — No entitlement for an ordinary magistrate to that allowance in the case of compulsory maternity leave taken prior to 1 January 2005.
