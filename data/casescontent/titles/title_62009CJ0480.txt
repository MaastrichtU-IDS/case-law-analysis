Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 16 December 2010.   AceaElectrabel Produzione SpA v European Commission.   Appeal - State aid - Aid declared compatible with the common market - Condition requiring prior repayment by the beneficiary of earlier aid declared unlawful - Concept of ‘economic unit’ - Joint control by two separate parent companies - Distortion of the pleas in law relied on in the application - Errors and defective reasoning.
