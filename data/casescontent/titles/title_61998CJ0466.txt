Judgment of the Court of 5 November 2002.   Commission of the European Communities v United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.   Failure by a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Conclusion and application by a Member State of a bilateral agreement with the United States of America - Agreement authorising the United States of America to revoke; suspend or limit the traffic rights of air carriers designated by the United Kingdom which are not owned by the latter or its nationals - Article 52 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Article 43 EC).
