Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 17 April 2018. Vera Egenberger v Evangelisches Werk für Diakonie und Entwicklung e.V. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesarbeitsgericht. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Social policy — Directive 2000/78/CE — Equal treatment — Difference of treatment on grounds of religion or belief — Occupational activities within churches and other organisations the ethos of which is based on religion or belief — Religion or belief constituting a genuine; legitimate and justified occupational requirement; having regard to the organisation’s ethos — Concept — Nature and context of the activities — Article 17 TFEU — Articles 10; 21 and 47 of the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union.
