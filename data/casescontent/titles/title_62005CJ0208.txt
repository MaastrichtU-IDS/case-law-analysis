Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 11 January 2007.   ITC Innovative Technology Center GmbH v Bundesagentur für Arbeit.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Sozialgericht Berlin - Germany.   Freedom of movement for workers - Freedom to provide services - National legislation - Payment by the Member State of the fee due to a private-sector recruitment agency in respect of recruitment - Employment subject to compulsory social security contributions in that Member State - Restriction - Justification - Proportionality.
