Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 26 February 2015. Wucher Helicopter GmbH and Euro-Aviation Versicherungs AG v Fridolin Santer. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberster Gerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Regulation (EC) No 785/2004 — Air carriers and aircraft operators — Insurance — Requirements — Definitions of ‘passenger’ and ‘member of the crew’ — Helicopter — Carriage of an expert in the blasting of avalanches using explosives — Injury suffered during a work flight — Compensation.
