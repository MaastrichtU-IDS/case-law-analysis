Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 12 February 2009. Vereniging Noordelijke Land- en Tuinbouw Organisatie v Staatssecretaris van Financiën. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands. Sixth VAT Directive - Goods and services forming part of the assets of a business for use in taxable transactions and in transactions other than taxable transactions - Right to an immediate and full deduction of the tax paid in respect of the acquisition of such goods and services.
