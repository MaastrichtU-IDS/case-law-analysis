Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 19 May 2011.   European Commission v Republic of Malta.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Regulation (EC) No 2037/2000 - Article 4(4)(v) and Article 16 - Requirement to decommission fire protection systems and fire extinguishers containing halons for non-critical uses on board ships - Exceptions - Critical uses of halons 1301 and 2402.
