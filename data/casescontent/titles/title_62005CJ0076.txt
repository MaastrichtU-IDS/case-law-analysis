Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 11 September 2007. Herbert Schwarz and Marga Gootjes-Schwarz v Finanzamt Bergisch Gladbach. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Köln - Germany. Article 8 A of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Article 18 EC) - European Citizenship - Article 59 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Article 49 EC) - Freedom to provide services - Income tax legislation - School fees - Tax deductibility limited to school fees paid to national private establishments.
