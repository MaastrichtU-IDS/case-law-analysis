Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 21 June 2007.   Office national des pensions v Emilienne Jonkman (C-231/06) and Hélène Vercheval (C-232/06) and Noëlle Permesaen v Office national des pensions (C-233/06).   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour du travail de Bruxelles - Belgium.   Equal treatment for men and women - Statutory pension scheme -Directive 79/7/EEC - Air hostesses - Grant of a pension equal to that of stewards - Payment of adjustment contributions in a single payment - Interest payments - Principle of effectiveness - Obligations on a Member State as a result of a preliminary ruling.
