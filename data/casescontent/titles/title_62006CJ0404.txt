Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 17 April 2008. Quelle AG v Bundesverband der Verbraucherzentralen und Verbraucherverbände. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesgerichtshof - Germany. Consumer protection - Directive 1999/44/EC - Sale of consumer goods and associated guarantees - Right of the seller; where goods not in conformity are replaced; to require the consumer to pay compensation for the use of those goods - No charge for the use of the goods not in conformity.
