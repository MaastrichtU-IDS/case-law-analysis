Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 28 June 2011.   Verein Deutsche Sprache eV v Council of the European Union.   Appeal - Contents of the internet site of the Presidency of the Council - Council’s failure to guarantee that the entire contents of the site exist in the German language - Action for failure to act - Position taken - Action for annulment - Actionable measures - Effective judicial protection - Appeal clearly unfounded.
