Order of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 13 June 2013.   Bianca Brandes v Land Niedersachsen.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Arbeitsgericht Nienburg - Germany.   Social policy - Directive 2003/88/EC - Entitlement to paid annual leave - Framework agreement on part-time work - Full-time worker having been unable to exercise her entitlement to paid annual leave during the reference period - Move of that worker to a scheme of part-time work - National provision or practice providing for a reduction in the number of days of paid leave previously accumulated thereby to be in proportion to the number of days of part-time work per week.
