Judgment of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 21 July 2011. Telefónica de España SA v Administración del Estado. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal Supremo - Spain. Directive 97/13/EC - Common framework for general authorisations and individual licences in the field of telecommunications - Fees and charges applicable to undertakings holding general authorisations - Article 6 - Interpretation - National legislation imposing an annual fee calculated on the basis of a percentage of gross operating income.
