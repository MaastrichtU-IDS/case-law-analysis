Order of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 14 April 2016. Roland SE v European Union Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO). Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure — EU trade mark — Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 — Article 8(1)(b) — Mark in the category ‘other’; consisting of a nuance of the colour red applied to the sole of a shoe — Opposition from the proprietor of the figurative mark registered internationally; containing the word elements ‘my SHOES’ — Rejection of the opposition.
