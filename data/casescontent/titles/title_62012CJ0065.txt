Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 6 February 2014. Leidseplein Beheer BV and Hendrikus de Vries v Red Bull GmbH and Red Bull Nederland BV. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Trade marks — Directive 89/104/EEC — Rights conferred by a trade mark — Trade mark with a reputation — Protection extended to non-similar goods or services — Use by a third party; without due cause; of a sign identical with or similar to the trade mark with a reputation — Definition of ‘due cause’.
