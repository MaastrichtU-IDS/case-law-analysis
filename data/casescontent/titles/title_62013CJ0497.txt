Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 4 June 2015. Froukje Faber v Autobedrijf Hazet Ochten BV. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Gerechtshof Arnhem-Leeuwarden. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 1999/44/EC — Sale of consumer goods and associated guarantees — Status of the purchaser — Consumer status — Lack of conformity of the goods delivered — Duty to inform the seller — Lack of conformity which became apparent within six months of delivery of the goods — Burden of proof.
