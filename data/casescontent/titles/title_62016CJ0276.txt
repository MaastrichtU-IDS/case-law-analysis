Judgment of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 20 December 2017. Prequ' Italia Srl v Agenzia delle Dogane e dei Monopoli. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Corte suprema di cassazione. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Principle of protection of the rights of the defence — Right to be heard — Regulation (EEC) No 2913/92 — Community Customs Code — Article 244 — Recovery of a customs debt — Lack of prior hearing of the addressee before the adoption of an amended tax assessment — Right of the addressee to obtain suspension of the implementation of the amended tax assessment — Lack of automatic suspension in the event of the bringing of administrative proceedings — Reference to the conditions provided for in Article 244 of the Customs Code.
