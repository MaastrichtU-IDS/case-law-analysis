Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 30 April 2009. BIOS Naturprodukte GmbH v Saarland. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesverwaltungsgericht - Germany. Directive 2001/83/EC - Article 1(2)(b) - Concept of ‘medicinal product by function’ - Dosage of the product - Normal conditions of use - Risk to health - Ability to restore; correct or modify physiological functions in human beings.
