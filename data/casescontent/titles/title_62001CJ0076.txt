Judgment of the Court of 30 September 2003.   Comité des industries du coton et des fibres connexes de l'Union européenne (Eurocoton) and Others v Council of the European Union.   Appeal - Dumping - Failure by the Council to adopt a proposal for a regulation imposing definitive anti-dumping duties - Lack of simple majority necessary for the adoption of the regulation - Expiry of the time-limit for the anti-dumping investigation - Definition of a reviewable act - Obligation to state reasons.
