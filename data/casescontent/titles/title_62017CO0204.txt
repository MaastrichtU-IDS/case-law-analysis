Order of the Vice-President of the Court of 11 October 2017. Hungary v European Commission. Appeal — Order for interim measures — State aid — Hungarian tax on advertisement turnover — Progressive nature of the tax rate — Limitation of the possibility to deduct losses carried forward to companies which did not make a profit in 2013 — Decision of the European Commission declaring the aid incompatible with the internal market and ordering its recovery — Application to suspend the operation of that decision — Urgency — No need to adjudicate.
