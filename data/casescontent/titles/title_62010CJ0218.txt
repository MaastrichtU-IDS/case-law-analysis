Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 26 January 2012. ADV Allround Vermittlungs AG; in liquidation v Finanzamt Hamburg-Bergedorf. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Finanzgericht Hamburg. VAT — Sixth Directive — Articles 9; 17 and 18 — Determination of the place where services are supplied — Concept of ‘supply of staff’ — Self-employed persons — Need to ensure that a provision of services is assessed identically in relation to the provider and in relation to the recipient.
