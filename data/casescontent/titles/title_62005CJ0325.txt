Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 18 July 2007.   Ismail Derin v Landkreis Darmstadt-Dieburg.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgericht Darmstadt - Germany.   EEC-Turkey Association - Article 59 of the Additional Protocol - Articles 6; 7 and 14 of Decision No 1/80 of the Association Council - Right of free access to employment under the second indent of the first paragraph of Article 7 - Corollary right of residence - Turkish national over the age of 21 years who is no longer dependent on his parents - Criminal convictions - Conditions governing the loss of acquired rights - Compatibility with the rule that the Republic of Turkey is not to receive more favourable treatment than that applicable between Member States.
