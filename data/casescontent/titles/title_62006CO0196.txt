Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 9 March 2007.   Alecansan SL v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeals - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 8(1)(b) - Application for registration of a figurative mark - Opposition of the proprietor of an earlier national figurative mark - Likelihood of confusion - None - No similarity between the goods and services covered by the conflicting trade marks.
