Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 15 July 2004.   Anneliese Lenz v Finanzlandesdirektion für Tirol.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Free movement of capital - Tax on revenue from capital - Revenue from capital of Austrian origin: tax rate of 25 % in discharge or rate equal to half of the average tax rate on aggregate income - Income from capital originating in another Member State: normal tax rate.
