Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 15 October 2015. Direktor na Agentsia "Mitnitsi" v Biovet AD. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Varhoven administrativen sad. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 92/83/EEC — Harmonisation of the structures of excise duties on alcohol and alcoholic beverages — Article 27(1)(d) — Exemption from the harmonised excise duty — Ethyl alcohol — Use for cleaning and disinfection of equipment and facilities used for the production of medicines.
