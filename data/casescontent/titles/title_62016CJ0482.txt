Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 14 March 2018. Georg Stollwitzer v ÖBB Personenverkehr AG. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberlandesgericht Innsbruck. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Social policy — Article 45 TFEU — Principle of non-discrimination on grounds of age — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Article 21(1) — Directive 2000/78/EC — Articles 2; 6 and 16 — Reference date for the purpose of advancement — Discriminatory legislation of a Member State which does not allow periods of activity completed before reaching the age of 18 to be taken into account for the purpose of determining remuneration — Abolition of provisions that are contrary to the principle of equal treatment.
