Judgment of the Court of 6 November 2003. Criminal proceedings against Piergiorgio Gambelli and Others. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale di Ascoli Piceno - Italy. Right of establishment - Freedom to provide services - Collection of bets on sporting events in one Member State and transmission by internet to another Member State - Prohibition enforced by criminal penalties - Legislation in a Member State which reserves the right to collect bets to certain bodies.
