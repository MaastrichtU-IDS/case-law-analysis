Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 16 February 2012.   Belgische Vereniging van Auteurs; Componisten en Uitgevers CVBA (SABAM) v Netlog NV.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Rechtbank van eerste aanleg te Brussel - Belgium.   Information society - Copyright - Internet - Hosting service provider - Processing of information stored on an online social networking platform - Introducing a system for filtering that information in order to prevent files being made available which infringe copyright - No general obligation to monitor stored information.
