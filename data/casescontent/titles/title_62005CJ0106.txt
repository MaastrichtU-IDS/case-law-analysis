Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 8 June 2006. L.u.P. GmbH v Finanzamt Bochum-Mitte. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany. Sixth VAT Directive - Exemptions - Article 13A(1)(b) and (c) and (2)(a) - Medical care provided by bodies other than those governed by public law - Medical care provided in the exercise of a medical profession - Medical tests carried out by a laboratory governed by private law outside a centre for treatment on prescription from general practitioners - Conditions for the exemption - Member States' discretion - Limits.
