Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 29 November 2011.   Evropaïki Dynamiki - Proigmena Systimata Tilepikoinonion Pliroforikis kai Tilematikis AE v European Commission.   Appeal - Article 119 of the Rules of Procedure - Public contracts awarded by the European Union institutions on their own behalf - Call for tenders concerning the provision of IT and user support services relating to the Community emissions trading scheme (CITL and CR) - Rejection of tender - Obligation to state the reasons on which the decision is based - Principle of equal treatment - Appeal clearly inadmissible and clearly unfounded.
