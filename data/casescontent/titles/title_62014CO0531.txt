Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 2 September 2015. Giorgio Giorgis v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs). Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Community trade mark — Invalidity proceedings — Three-dimensional Community trade mark — Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 — Article 7(1)(b) — Absolute ground for invalidity — No distinctive character — Shape of two packaged goblets.
