Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 14 January 2010. Zentrale zur Bekämpfung unlauteren Wettbewerbs eV v Plus Warenhandelsgesellschaft mbH. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesgerichtshof - Germany. Directive 2005/29/EC - Unfair commercial practices - National legislation laying down a prohibition in principle of commercial practices which make the participation of consumers in a lottery conditional on the purchase of goods or the use of services.
