Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 17 December 2010.   Dimitris Platis v Council of the European Union and Hellenic Republic.   Appeal - Action for annulment - Council regulations laying down provisions on the introduction of the euro - National legislative measures laying down provisions to implement those regulations - Manifest inadmissibility of the action - General Court’s manifest lack of jurisdiction - Appeal in part clearly unfounded and in part clearly inadmissible - Article 119 of the Rules of Procedure.
