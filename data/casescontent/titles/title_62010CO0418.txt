Appeal - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 8(1)(b) - Opposition proceedings - Earlier mark STABILAT - Figurative sign 'stabilator' - Relative ground for refusal - Likelihood of confusion - Absence of similarity of the goods and services.
