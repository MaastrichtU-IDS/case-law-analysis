Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 2 April 2009.   Hauptzollamt Bremen v J. E. Tyson Parketthandel GmbH hanse j.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany.   Regulation (EC) No 2193/2003- Additional customs duties on imports of certain products originating in the United States of America - Temporal scope - Article 4(2) - Products exported after the entry into force of that regulation for which it can be demonstrated that they were already been on their way to the Community when those duties were first applied - Whether subject to duty.
