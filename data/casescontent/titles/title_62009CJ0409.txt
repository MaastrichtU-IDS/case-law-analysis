Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 9 June 2011. José Maria Ambrósio Lavrador and Maria Cândida Olival Ferreira Bonifácio v Companhia de Seguros Fidelidade-Mundial SA. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Supremo Tribunal de Justiça - Portugal. Insurance against civil liability in respect of the use of motor vehicles - Directives 72/166/EEC; 84/5/EEC and 90/232/EEC - Right to compensation by means of compulsory insurance against civil liability in respect of the use of motor vehicles - Conditions for limitation - Victim’s contribution to his own loss or injury - Liability for risk - Provisions applicable to a child third party who is the victim of an accident.
