Order of the Court (First Chamber) of 6 September 2017. Peter Schotthöfer &amp; Florian Steiner GbR v Eugen Adelsmayr. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bezirksgericht Linz. Requests for a preliminary ruling — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Extradition of a national of a Member State of the European Union to a third State where he risks being subjected to the death penalty — Citizenship of the Union — Articles 18 and 21 TFEU — Article 19(2) of the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Protection against extradition.
