Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 17 February 2016. Shoe Branding Europe BVBA v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs). Appeal — Community trade mark — Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 — Article 65 — Position mark — Two parallel stripes on the side of a shoe — Opposition by the proprietor of Community and national figurative marks and international registration representing three parallel stripes applied to shoes and clothing — Opposition rejected.
