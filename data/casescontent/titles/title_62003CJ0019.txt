Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 14  September  2004.   Verbraucher-Zentrale Hamburg eV v O2 (Germany) GmbH &amp; Co. OHG.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landgericht München I - Germany.   Economic and monetary policy - Regulation (EC) No 1103/97 - Introduction of the euro - Conversion of national currency units to the euro unit - Rounding of monetary amounts to be paid or accounted for after conversion - Contract concluded in the telecommunications sector - Meaning of "monetary amounts to be paid or accounted for' - Per-minute tariffs for telephone calls.
