Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 27 April 2017. Onix Asigurări SA v Istituto per la Vigilanza Sulle Assicurazioni (IVASS). Request for a preliminary ruling from the Consiglio di Stato. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 73/239/EEC — Directive 92/49/EEC — Principle of single authorisation — Principle of supervision by the home Member State — Article 40(6) — Concept of ‘irregularities’ — Reputation of shareholders — Prohibition on insurance companies established in a Member State concluding new contracts within the territory of another Member State.
