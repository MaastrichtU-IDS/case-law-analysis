Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 8 February 2018. Lloyd's of London v Agenzia Regionale per la Protezione dell'Ambiente della Calabria. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale Amministrativo Regionale Calabria. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Public procurement — Articles 49 and 56 TFEU — Directive 2004/18/EC — Reasons for exclusion from a tendering procedure — Insurance services — Participation of several Lloyd’s of London syndicates in the same tendering procedure — Signature of tenders by the Lloyd’s of London General Representative for the country concerned — Principles of transparency; equal treatment and non-discrimination — Proportionality.
