Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 28 April 2011. Hassen El Dridi; alias Soufi Karim. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Corte d’appello di Trento - Italy. Area of freedom; security and justice - Directive 2008/115/EC - Return of illegally staying third-country nationals - Articles 15 and 16 - National legislation providing for a prison sentence for illegally staying third-country nationals in the event of refusal to obey an order to leave the territory of a Member State - Compatibility.
