Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 7 October 2010. Commissioners for Her Majesty’s Revenue and Customs v Loyalty Management UK Ltd (C-53/09) and Baxi Group Ltd (C-55/09). Reference for a preliminary ruling: House of Lords - United Kingdom. Sixth VAT Directive - Taxable amount - Sales promotion scheme - Loyalty rewards scheme allowing customers to earn points from traders and to redeem them for loyalty rewards - Payments made by the operator of the scheme to redeemers supplying the loyalty rewards - Payments made by the trader to the operator of the scheme supplying the loyalty rewards.
