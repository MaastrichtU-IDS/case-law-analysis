Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 27 November 2007. C. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Korkein hallinto-oikeus - Finland. Judicial cooperation in civil matters - Jurisdiction; recognition and enforcement of judgments in matrimonial matters and the matters of parental responsibility - Regulation (EC) No 2201/2003 - Substantive and temporal scope - Definition of ‘civil matters’ - Decision concerning the taking into care and placement of children outside the family home - Public law measures for child protection.
