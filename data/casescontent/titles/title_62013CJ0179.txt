Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 15 January 2015.   Raad van bestuur van de Sociale verzekeringsbank v L. F. Evans.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Centrale Raad van Beroep - Netherlands.   Reference for a preliminary ruling - Determination of the social security legislation applicable to a worker - Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 - Applicability - Employment of a national of a Member State at the consulate of a third State in the territory of another Member State in whose territory he resides - Vienna Convention on consular relations - Article 71(2) - National legislation according facilities; privileges and immunities to permanent residents.
