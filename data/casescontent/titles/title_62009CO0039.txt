Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 22 March 2010.   Société des plantations de Mbanga SA (SPM) v Council of the European Union and European Commission.   Appeal - Article 119 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice - Non-contractual liability of the Community - Common organisation of the banana market - Arrangements for importing bananas originating in ACP countries into the Community - Loss allegedly suffered by an independent producer - Failure to comply with the rules on competition in the field of the common agricultural policy - Infringement of general principles of law and; in particular; of the principle of sound administration - Appeal manifestly inadmissible or manifestly unfounded.
