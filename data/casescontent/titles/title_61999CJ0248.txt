Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 8 January 2002.   French Republic v Monsanto Company and Commission of the European Communities.   Appeal - Regulation (EEC) No 2377/90 - Application to include a recombinant bovine somatotrophin (BST) in the list of substances not subject to a maximum residue limit - Prohibition on placing that substance on the market - Rejection of the application for inclusion.
