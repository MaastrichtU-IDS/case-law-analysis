Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 3 February 2009.   Massimo Giannini v Commission of the European Communities.   Appeal - Community civil service - Right to a fair hearing - Infringement of Articles 4; 27 and 29 of the Staff Regulations - Principle of non-discrimination - Interest of the service and duty of care - Distortion of the evidence and rules on evidence - Appeal manifestly inadmissible in part and manifestly unfounded in part.
