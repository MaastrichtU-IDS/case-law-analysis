Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 17 March 2016. Minister Finansów v Aspiro SA. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Naczelny Sąd Administracyjny. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Taxation — Value added tax — Directive 2006/112/EC — Article 135(1)(a) — Exemption for insurance — Definition of ‘insurance’ transactions and of ‘related services performed by insurance brokers and insurance agents’ — Claims settlement services provided by and on behalf of an insurer.
