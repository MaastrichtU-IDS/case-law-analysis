Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 7 February 2002.   Commission of the European Communities v Federal Republic of Germany.   Failure by a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Directive 89/391/EEC - Measures to encourage improvements in the safety and health of workers at work - Articles 9(1)(a) and 10(3)(a) - Employer's duty to keep documents containing an assessment of the risks to safety and health at work.
