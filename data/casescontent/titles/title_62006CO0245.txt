Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 9 March 2007.   Saiwa SpA v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Article 8(1)(b) of Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Likelihood of confusion - Application for a figurative mark including the word element 'SELEZIONE ORO Barilla' - Opposition by the holder of the national and international trade mark ORO and the national trade mark ORO SAIWA - Appeal in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly unfounded.
