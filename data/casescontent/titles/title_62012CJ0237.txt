Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 4 September 2014. European Commission v French Republic. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations — Directive 91/676/EEC — Article 5(4) — Annex II.A; points 1 to 3 and 5 — Annex III.1; points 1 to 3; and Annex III.2 — Protection of waters against pollution caused by nitrates from agricultural sources — Periods for land application — Capacity of storage vessels for livestock manure — Limitation of land application — Prohibition on land application on steeply sloping ground or on snow-covered or frozen ground — Non-compliance of national legislation.
