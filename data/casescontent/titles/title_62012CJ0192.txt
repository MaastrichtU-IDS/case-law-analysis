Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 28 June 2012.   Melvin West.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Korkein oikeus - Finland.   Police and judicial cooperation in criminal matters - Framework Decision 2002/584/JHA - European arrest warrant and surrender procedures between Member States - European arrest warrant issued for the purposes of execution of a custodial sentence - Article 28 - Subsequent surrender - ‘Chain’ of European arrest warrants - Execution of a third European arrest warrant in respect of the same person - Concept of ‘executing Member State’ - Consent to surrender - Urgent preliminary ruling procedure.
