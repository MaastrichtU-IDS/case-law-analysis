Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 1 March 2011. Association Belge des Consommateurs Test-Achats ASBL and Others v Conseil des ministres. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour constitutionnelle - Belgium. Reference for a preliminary ruling - Fundamental rights - Combating discrimination - Equal treatment for men and women - Access to and supply of goods and services - Insurance premiums and benefits - Actuarial factors - Sex as a factor in the assessment of insurance risks - Private life assurance contracts - Directive 2004/113/EC - Article 5(2) - Derogation not subject to any temporal limitation - Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union - Articles 21 and 23 - Invalidity.
