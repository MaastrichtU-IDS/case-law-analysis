Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 22 January 2010.   Hellenic Republic v European Commission.   Appeal - Commission decision reducing the financial assistance initially granted by the Cohesion Fund for the project for the new Athens International Airport at Spata - Action for annulment - Principles of non-retroactivity; legal certainty and proportionality - Appeal manifestly inadmissible in part and manifestly unfounded in part.
