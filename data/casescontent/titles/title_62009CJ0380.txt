Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 13 March 2012.   Melli Bank plc v Council of the European Union.   Appeal - Politique étrangère et de sécurité commune - Mesures restrictives prises à l’encontre de la Appeal - Common foreign and security policy - Restrictive measures against the Islamic Republic of Iran to prevent nuclear proliferation - Freezing the funds of a bank’s subsidiary - Principle of proportionality - Ownership or control of the entity.
