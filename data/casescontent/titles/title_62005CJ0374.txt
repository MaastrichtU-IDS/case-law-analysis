Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 8 November 2007.   Gintec International Import-Export GmbH v Verband Sozialer Wettbewerb eV.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesgerichtshof - Germany.   Directives 2001/83/EC and 92/28/EEC - National legislation prohibiting advertising of medicinal products by way of statements of third parties or prize draws - Use of generally positive results of a consumer survey and a monthly prize draw to win a pack of the product.
