Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 27 April 2006.   Commission of the European Communities v Federal Republic of Germany.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Articles 8a and 48 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Articles 18 EC and 39 EC) - Directives 64/221/EEC; 73/148/EEC and 90/364/EEC - Regulation (EEC) No 1612/68 - Freedom of movement for nationals of Member States - Public policy - Right to respect for family life - National legislation relating to the prohibition on residence and expulsion - Administrative practice - Criminal conviction - Expulsion.
