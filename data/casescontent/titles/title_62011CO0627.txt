Order of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 27 November 2012. S.C. „AUGUSTUS” S.R.L. Iaşi v Agenţia de Plăţi pentru Dezvoltare Rurală şi Pescuit. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Inalta Curte de Casaţie şi Justiţie — Interpretation of Council Regulation (EC) No 1268/1999 of 21 June 1999 on Community support for pre-accession measures for agriculture and rural development in the applicant countries of central and eastern Europe in the pre-accession period (OJ 1999 L 161; p. 87) and Council Regulation (EC) No 1260/1999 of 21 June 1999 laying down general provisions on the Structural Funds (OJ 1999 L 161; p. 1) — Cancellation and recovery; in the event of improper conduct; of Community funding granted under the SAPARD programme — Eligibility of expenditure — Cases of force majeure — Justification — Notions of ‘economic efficiency’ and ‘profitability’. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Manifest inadmissibility.
