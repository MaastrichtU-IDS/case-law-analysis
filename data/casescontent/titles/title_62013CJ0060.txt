Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber); 3 April 2014. European Commission v United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations — European Union’s own resources — Decision 2000/597/EC; Euratom — Article 8 — Regulation (EC; Euratom) No 1150/2000 — Articles 2; 6; 9; 10 and 11 — Refusal to make own resources available to the European Union — Erroneous Binding Tariff Information documents — Imports of fresh garlic as frozen garlic — Imputability of the error to the national customs authorities — Financial liability of the Member States.
