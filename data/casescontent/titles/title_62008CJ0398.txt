Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 21 January 2010. Audi AG v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM). Appeal - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Articles 7(1)(b) and 63 - Word mark Vorsprung durch Technik - Marks consisting of advertising slogans - Distinctive character - Application for a trade mark in respect of a large number of goods and services - Relevant public - Global assessment and reasoning - New documents.
