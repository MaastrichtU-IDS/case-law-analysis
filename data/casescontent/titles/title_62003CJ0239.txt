Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 7  October  2004.   Commission of the European Communities v French Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Convention for the protection of the Mediterranean Sea against pollution - Articles 4(1) and 8 - Protocol for the protection of the Mediterranean Sea against pollution from land-based sources - Article 6(1) and (3) - Failure to adopt appropriate measures to prevent; abate and combat heavy and prolonged pollution of the Étang de Berre - Discharge authorisation.
