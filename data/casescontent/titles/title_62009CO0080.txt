Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 5 February 2010.   Volker Mergel; Klaus Kampfenkel; Bill Burkart and Andreas Herden v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 7(1)(c) - Refusal to register - Word mark Patentconsult - Absolute ground for refusal - Descriptive character - Appeal in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly unfounded.
