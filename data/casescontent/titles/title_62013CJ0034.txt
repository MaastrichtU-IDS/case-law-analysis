Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 10 September 2014. Monika Kušionová v SMART Capital; a.s. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Krajský súd v Prešove. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 93/13/EEC — Unfair terms — Consumer credit agreement — Article 1(2) — Term reflecting a mandatory statutory provision — Scope of the directive — Articles 3(1); 4; 6(1) and 7(1) — Security for credit in the form of a charge on immovable property — Whether it is possible to enforce the charge by means of a sale by auction — Judicial review.
