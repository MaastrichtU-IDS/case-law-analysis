Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 11 December 2008.   Danfoss A/S and AstraZeneca A/S v Skatteministeriet.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Vestre Landsret - Denmark.   Sixth VAT Directive - Article 6(2) - Supplies of services carried out free of charge by a taxable person for purposes other than those of his business - Right to deduct VAT - Second subparagraph of Article 17(6) - Member States’ option to retain exclusions from the right to deduct which were provided for under their national laws when the Sixth Directive came into force.
