Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 16 July 2009.   Infopaq International A/S v Danske Dagblades Forening.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Højesteret - Denmark.   Copyright - Information society - Directive 2001/29/EC - Articles 2 and 5 - Literary and artistic works - Concept of ‘reproduction’ - Reproduction ‘in part’ - Reproduction of short extracts of literary works - Newspaper articles - Temporary and transient reproductions - Technological process consisting in scanning of articles followed by conversion into text file; electronic processing of the reproduction; storage of part of that reproduction and printing out.
