Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 12 December 2008.   Aktieselskabet af 21. november 2001 v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 8(5) - Reputation - Taking unfair advantage of the distinctive character or the repute of the earlier mark - Application for registration as a Community trade mark of the word mark ‘TDK’ - Opposition by the proprietor of the Community and national word and figurative marks TDK - Refusal to register.
