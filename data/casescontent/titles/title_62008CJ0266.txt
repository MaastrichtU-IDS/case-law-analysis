Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 14 May 2009.   Commission of the European Communities v Kingdom of Spain.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 2004/81/EC - Residence permit issued to third-country nationals who are victims of trafficking in human beings or who have been the subject of an action to facilitate illegal immigration; who cooperate with the competent authorities - Failure to transpose completely - Failure to communicate the measures to transpose the directive.
