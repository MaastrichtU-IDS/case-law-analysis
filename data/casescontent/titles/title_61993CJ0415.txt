Judgment of the Court of 15 December 1995.   Union royale belge des sociétés de football association ASBL v Jean-Marc Bosman; Royal club liégeois SA v Jean-Marc Bosman and others and Union des associations européennes de football (UEFA) v Jean-Marc Bosman.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d'appel de Liège - Belgium.   Freedom of movement for workers - Competition rules applicable to undertakings - Professional footballers - Sporting rules on the transfer of players requiring the new club to pay a fee to the old club - Limitation of the number of players having the nationality of other Member States who may be fielded in a match.
