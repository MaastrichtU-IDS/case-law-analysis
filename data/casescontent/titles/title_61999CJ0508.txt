Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 16 May 2002.   Palais am Stadtpark Hotelbetriebsgesellschaft mbH &amp; Co. KG v Finanzlandesdirektion für Wien; Niederösterreich und Burgenland.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Raising of capital - Directive 69/335/EEC - Scope of application - Limited partnership - Assignment of limited partner's share to a company with limited liability - Levy; after the assignment and the entry into force of the directive; of duty directly proportional to the amount of the contribution.
