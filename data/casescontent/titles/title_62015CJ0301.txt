Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 16 November 2016. Marc Soulier and Sara Doke v Premier ministre and Ministre de la Culture et de la Communication. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Conseil d'État (France). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Intellectual and industrial property rights — Directive 2001/29/EC — Copyright and related rights — Articles 2 and 3 — Rights of reproduction and communication to the public — Scope — ‘Out-of-print’ books which are not or no longer published — National legislation giving a collecting society rights to exploit out-of-print books for commercial purposes — Legal presumption of the authors’ consent — Lack of a mechanism ensuring authors are actually and individually informed.
