Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 9 March 2017. GE Healthcare GmbH v Hauptzollamt Düsseldorf. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Finanzgericht Düsseldorf. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Customs Union — Community Customs Code — Article 32(1)(c) — Determination of the customs value — Royalties or licence fees in respect of the goods being valued — Meaning — Regulation (EEC) No 2454/93 — Article 160 — ‘Condition of sale’ of the goods being valued — Payment of royalties or licence fees to an undertaking related to both the seller and the buyer of the goods — Article 158(3) — Adjustment and apportionment measures.
