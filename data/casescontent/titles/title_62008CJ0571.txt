Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 24 June 2010.   European Commission v Italian Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 95/59/EC - Taxes other than turnover taxes which affect the consumption of manufactured tobacco - Article 9(1) - Free determination; by manufacturers and importers; of the maximum retail selling prices of their products - National legislation imposing a minimum retail selling price for cigarettes - Justification - Protection of public health.
