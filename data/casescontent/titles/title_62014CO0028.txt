Request for a preliminary ruling from the Sąd Okręgowy w Częstochowie. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 53(2) of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Implementation of EU law — No such implementation — Clear lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
