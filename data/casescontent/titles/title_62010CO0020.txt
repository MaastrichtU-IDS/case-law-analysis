Order of the President of the Sixth Chamber of the Court of 11 November 2010.   Vino Cosimo Damiano v Poste Italiane SpA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale di Trani - Italy.   Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Social policy- Directive 1999/70/EC -Clauses 3 and 8 of the framework agreement on fixed-term work - Fixed-term employment contracts in the public sector - First or single use of a contract - Obligation to state the objective reasons - Elimination - Reduction in the general level of protection of employees - Principle of non-discrimination - Articles 82 EC and 86 EC.
