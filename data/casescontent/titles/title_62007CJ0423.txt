Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 22 April 2010.   European Commission v Kingdom of Spain.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 93/37/EEC - Articles 3 and 11 - Public works concession contracts - Obligations regarding advertising - Extent of the obligations - Contract notice - Description of the object of the concession and of the location of the works - Additional works not expressly set out in the contract notice or in the tender specifications - Principle of equal treatment.
