Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 17 January 2013.   Abbott Laboratories v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Word mark 'RESTORE' - Refusal to register - Absolute grounds for refusal - Descriptive character - Lack of distinctiveness - Right to be heard - Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 - Articles 7 (1(b) and (c) and 75; second sentence - Equal treatment.
