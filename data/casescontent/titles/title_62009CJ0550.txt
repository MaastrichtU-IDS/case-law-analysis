Common foreign and security policy - Specific restrictive measures directed against certain persons and entities with a view to combating terrorism - Common Position 2001/931/CFSP - Regulation (EC) No 2580/2001 - Articles 2 and 3 - Inclusion of an organisation on the list of persons; groups and entities implicated in acts of terrorism - Transfer to an organisation; by members of that organisation; of funds originating from the collection of donations and the sale of publications.
