Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 22 December 2008. Belgian State - SPF Finances v Truck Center SA. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d’appel de Liège - Belgium. Freedom of establishment - Article 52 of the EC Treaty (now; following amendment; Article 43 EC) and Article 58 of the EC Treaty (now Article 48 EC) - Free movement of capital - Articles 73b and 73d of the EC Treaty (now Articles 56 EC and 58 EC respectively) - Taxation of legal persons - Income from capital and movable property - Retention of tax at source - Withholding tax- Charging of withholding tax on interest paid to non-resident companies - No charging of withholding tax on interest paid to resident companies - Double taxation convention - Restriction - None.
