Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 10 February 2000.   Ömer Nazli; Caglar Nazli and Melike Nazli v Stadt Nürnberg.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgericht Ansbach - Germany.   EEC-Turkey Association Agreement - Freedom of movement for workers - Articles 6(1) and 14(1) of Decision No 1/80 of the Association Council - Registration as duly belonging to the labour force of a Member State - Turkish worker detained pending trial and subsequently sentenced to a suspended term of imprisonment - Expulsion on general preventive grounds.
