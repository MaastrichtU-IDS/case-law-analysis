Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 1 October 2015. Weltimmo s.r.o. v Nemzeti Adatvédelmi és Információszabadság Hatóság. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Kúria. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Protection of individuals with regard to the processing of personal data — Directive 95/46/EC — Articles 4(1) and 28(1); (3) and (6) — Controller who is formally established in a Member State — Impairment of the right to the protection of personal data concerning natural persons in another Member State — Determination of the applicable law and the competent supervisory authority — Exercise of the powers of the supervisory authority — Power to impose penalties.
