Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 28 June 2012. Georges Erny v Daimler AG - Werk Wörth. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Arbeitsgericht Ludwigshafen am Rhein. Freedom of movement for workers — Article 45 TFEU — Regulation (EEC) No 1612/68 — Article 7(4) — Principle of non-discrimination — Top-up amount on wages paid to workers placed on a scheme of part-time work prior to retirement — Cross-border workers subject to income tax in the Member State of residence — Notional taking into account of the tax on wages of the Member State of employment.
