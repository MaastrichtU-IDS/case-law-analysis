Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 4 April 2017. Sharif University of Technology v Council of the European Union. Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Restrictive measures against the Islamic Republic of Iran — List of persons and entities subject to the freezing of funds and economic resources — Criterion relating to the material; logistical or financial support to the Government of Iran — Cooperation in research and technological development in military or military-related fields.
