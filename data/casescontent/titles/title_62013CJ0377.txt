Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 12 June 2014. Ascendi Beiras Litoral e Alta; Auto Estradas das Beiras Litoral e Alta SA v Autoridade Tributária e Aduaneira. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal Arbitral Tributário (Centro de Arbitragem Administrativa — CAAD). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Concept of ‘court or tribunal of a Member State’ — Tribunal Arbitral Tributário — Directive 69/335/EEC — Articles 4 and 7 — Increase of the share capital of a capital company — Stamp duty in force on 1 July 1984 — That stamp duty subsequently abolished; and then re-introduced.
