Combined Nomenclature — Tariff headings — Medicaments within the meaning of heading 3004 — Meaning — Nutritional preparations intended to be administered enterally under medical supervision to persons undergoing medical treatment — Beverages within the meaning of heading 2202 — Meaning — Nutritional liquids intended to be administered enterally and not to be drunk.
