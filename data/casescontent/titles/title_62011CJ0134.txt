Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber); 16 February 2012. Jürgen Blödel-Pawlik v HanseMerkur Reiseversicherung AG. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Landgericht Hamburg. Directive 90/314/EEC — Package travel; package holidays and package tours — Article 7 — Protection against the risk of insolvency or bankruptcy on the part of the package organiser — Scope — Insolvency of the organiser on account of its fraudulent use of the funds transferred by consumers.
