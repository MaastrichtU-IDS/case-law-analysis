Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 15 May 2003.   Préservatrice foncière TIARD SA v Staat der Nederlanden.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands.   Brussels Convention - Article 1 - Scope - Concept of civil and commercial matters - Concept of customs matters - Action based on a guarantee contract between the State and an insurance company - Contract entered into in order to satisfy a condition imposed by the State on associations of carriers; principal debtors; under Article 6 of the TIR Convention.
