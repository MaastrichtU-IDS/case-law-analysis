Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 19 July 2012. International Bingo Technology SA v Tribunal Económico-Administrativo Regional de Cataluña (TEARC). Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal Superior de Justicia de Cataluña. Sixth VAT Directive — Articles 11A(1)(a); 17(5) and 19(1) — Organisation of games of bingo — Legal obligation to use part of the card price to pay winnings to players — Calculation of the basis of assessment.
