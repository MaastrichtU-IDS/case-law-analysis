Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 14 April 2011.   Mensch und Natur AG v Freistaat Bayern.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bayerischer Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Germany.   Fourth paragraph of Article 249 EC - Measures adopted by the institutions - Commission decision addressed to a particular person - Regulation (EC) No 258/97 - Novel food or novel food ingredient - Decision 2000/196/EC - ‘Stevia rebaudiana Bertoni: plants and dried leaves’ - Refusal to authorise placing on the market - Effects in relation to a person other than the addressee.
