Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 21 November 2017. Zoltán Rózsavölgyi and Zoltánné Rózsavölgyi v Unicredit Leasing Hungary Zrt. and Unicredit Leasing Immo Truck Zrt. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Budai Központi Kerületi Bíróság. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 53(2) and Article 94 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Consumer protection — Unfair terms in consumer contracts — Loan contract denominated in a foreign currency — Insufficient information regarding the factual and regulatory context of the dispute in the main proceedings and the reasons justifying the need for an answer to the questions referred for a preliminary ruling — Manifest inadmissibility.
