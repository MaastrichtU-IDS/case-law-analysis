Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 22 May 2003.   IHW Rebmann GmbH v Hauptzollamt Weiden.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany.   Free movement of goods - Trade with third countries - Procedure for returned goods - Article 187 of Regulation (EEC) No 2913/92 - Reimportation of compensating products originally re-exported subsequent to an inward processing procedure - Determination of the import duty legally owed - Burden of proof in respect of the proportion of the value of the reimported products corresponding to inward processing.
