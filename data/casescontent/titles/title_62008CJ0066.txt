Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 17 July 2008. Proceedings concerning the execution of a European arrest warrant issued against Szymon Kozłowski. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Oberlandesgericht Stuttgart - Germany. Police and judicial cooperation in criminal matters - Framework Decision 2002/584/JHA - European arrest warrant and surrender procedures between Member States - Article 4(6) - Ground for optional non-execution of a European arrest warrant - Interpretation of the terms ‘resident’ and ‘staying’ in the executing Member State.
