Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 17 July 2008.   Commission of the European Communities v French Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Articles 43 EC and 49 EC - Freedom of establishment and freedom to provide services - Animal health - Artificial insemination centre for bovine animals - National rules conferring on authorised centres the exclusive right to provide the service of artificially inseminating bovine animals in a defined geographical area and making the issue of an inseminator’s licence subject to the conclusion of an agreement with one of those centres.
