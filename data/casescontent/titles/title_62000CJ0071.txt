Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 17 October 2002.   Develop Baudurchführungs- und Stadtentwicklungs GmbH v Finanzlandesdirektion für Wien; Niederösterreich und Burgenland.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Directive 69/335/EEC - Indirect taxes on the raising of capital - Capital duty - Contribution of assets of any kind - Meaning - Payments made by the parent company of a company which has acquired dividend certificates issued by a capital company.
