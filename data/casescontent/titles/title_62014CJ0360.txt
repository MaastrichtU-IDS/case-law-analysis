Judgment of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 9 July 2015. Federal Republic of Germany v European Commission. Appeal — Approximation of laws — Directive 2009/48/EC — Toy safety — Limit values for lead; barium; arsenic; antimony; mercury and nitrosamines and nitrosatable substances in toys — Commission decision not to approve in full the national provisions notified by the German authorities maintaining the limit values for those substances — Proof of a higher level of protection for human health offered by the national provisions.
