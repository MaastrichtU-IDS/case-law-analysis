Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 12 August 2008. Ignacio Pedro Santesteban Goicoechea. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d'appel de Montpellier - France. Police and judicial cooperation in criminal matters - Framework Decision 2002/584/JHA - Articles 31 and 32 - European arrest warrant and the surrender procedures between Member States - Possibility for the State executing an extradition request to apply a convention adopted before 1 January 2004 but applicable in that State from a later date.
