Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 27 April 2017. Criminal proceedings against Noria Distribution SARL. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal de grande instance de Perpignan. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2002/46/EC — Approximation of the laws of the Member States relating to food supplements — Vitamins and minerals which may be used in the manufacture of food supplements — Maximum amounts — Competence of the Member States — National legislation setting those amounts — Mutual recognition — Absence — Conditions to be respected and factors to be taken into consideration when setting those amounts.
