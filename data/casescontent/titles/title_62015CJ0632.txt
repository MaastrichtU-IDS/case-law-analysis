Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 26 April 2017. Costin Popescu v Guvernul României and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Înalta Curte de Casație şi Justiție. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Transport — Road transport — Driving licences — Directive 2006/126/EC — Article 13(2) — Concept of ‘entitlement to drive granted before 19 January 2013’ — National legislation transposing the directive — Obligation to obtain a driving licence imposed on persons who were allowed to ride a moped without a licence before the entry into force of that legislation.
