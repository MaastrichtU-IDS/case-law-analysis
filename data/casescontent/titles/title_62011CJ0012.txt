Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 31 January 2013.   Denise McDonagh v Ryanair Ltd.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Dublin Metropolitan District Court - Ireland.   Air transport - Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 - Notion of ‘extraordinary circumstances’ - Obligation to provide assistance to passengers in the event of cancellation of a flight due to ‘extraordinary circumstances’ - Volcanic eruption leading to the closure of air space - Eruption of the Icelandic volcano Eyjafjallajökull.
