Judgment of the Court of 11 March 1997.   Empresa Nacional de Urânio SA (ENU) v Commission of the European Communities.   Appeal - EAEC - Supply - Right of option and exclusive right of the Euratom Supply Agency to conclude contracts for the supply of ores; source materials and special fissile materials - Infringement of the rules of the Treaty - Community preference - Principles of good faith and legitimate expectations - Non-contractual liability.
