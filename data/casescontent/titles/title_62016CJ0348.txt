Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 26 July 2017. Moussa Sacko v Commissione Territoriale per il riconoscimento della Protezione internazionale di Milano. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale di Milano. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Asylum policy — Directive 2013/32/EU — Articles 12; 14; 31 and 46 — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Article 47 — Right to effective judicial protection — Appeal against a decision refusing an application for international protection — Whether it is possible for the court to adjudicate without hearing the applicant.
