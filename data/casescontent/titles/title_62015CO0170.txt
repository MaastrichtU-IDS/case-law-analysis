Appeal — Community trade mark — Regulation No 207/2009 — Article 43(2) — Amendment of Community trade mark — Reclassification of a colour mark as a figurative mark — Article 7(1)(b) — No distinctive character — Refusal of registration — Colour mark made up of certain nuances of the colour green.
