Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 13 March 2007. Unibet (London) Ltd and Unibet (International) Ltd v Justitiekanslern. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Högsta domstolen - Sweden. Principle of judicial protection - National legislation not providing for a self-standing action to challenge the compatibility of a national provision with Community law - Procedural autonomy - Principles of equivalence and effectiveness - Interim protection.
