Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 11 December 1997.   Mary Teresa Magorrian and Irene Patricia Cunningham v Eastern Health and Social Services Board and Department of Health and Social Services.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Office of the Industrial Tribunal and the Fair Employment Tribunal; Belfast - United Kingdom.   Equal pay for men and women - Article 119 of the EC Treaty - Protocol No 2 annexed to the Treaty on European Union - Occupational social security schemes - Exclusion of part-time workers from status conferring entitlement to certain additional retirement pension benefits - Date from which such benefits are payable - National procedural time-limits.
