Order of the Court (Third Chamber) of 6 April 2006.   Daniel Halbritter v Freistaat Bayern.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bayerisches Verwaltungsgericht München - Germany.   First subparagraph of Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Directive 91/439/EEC - Mutual recognition of driving licences - Confiscation of driving licence in one Member State accompanied by temporary ban on acquiring a new licence - Licence issued in another Member State after the expiry of the temporary ban - Recognition and exchange of that licence in the first Member State - Presentation of a report on aptitude to drive required by national law.
