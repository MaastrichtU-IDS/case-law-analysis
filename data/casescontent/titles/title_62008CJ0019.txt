Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 29 January 2009. Migrationsverket v Edgar Petrosian and Others. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Kammarrätten i Stockholm - Migrationsöverdomstolen - Sweden. Right of asylum - Regulation (EC) No 343/2003 - Taking back by a Member State of an asylum seeker whose application has been refused and who is in another Member State where he has submitted a fresh asylum application - Start of the period for implementation of transfer of the asylum seeker - Transfer procedure the subject-matter of an appeal having suspensive effect.
