Judgment of the Court of 26 September 2000.   Didier Mayeur v Association Promotion de l'information messine (APIM).   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Conseil de prud'hommes de Metz - France.   Maintenance of workers' rights in the event of transfer of an undertaking - Transfer to a municipality of an activity previously carried out; in the interests of that municipality; by a legal person established under private law.
