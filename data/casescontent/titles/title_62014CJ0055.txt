Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 77/388/EEC — VAT — Exemptions — Article 13B(b) — Concept of ‘exempted letting of immovable property’ — Provision; for consideration; of a football stadium — Contract for provision reserving certain rights and prerogatives to the owner — Supply; by the owner; of various services representing 80% of the charge specified in the contract.
