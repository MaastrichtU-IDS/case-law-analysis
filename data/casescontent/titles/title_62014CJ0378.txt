Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 22 October 2015. Bundesagentur für Arbeit - Familienkasse Sachsen v Tomislaw Trapkowski. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesfinanzhof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Social security — Regulation (EC) No 883/2004 — Article 67 — Regulation (EC) No 987/2009 — Article 60(1) — Payment of family benefits where parents are divorced — Definition of the ‘person concerned’ — Law of a Member State providing for the payment of family benefits to the parent who has taken the child into his household — Residence of that parent in another Member State — Failure of that parent to claim family benefits — Possibility of entitlement of the other parent to claim those family benefits.
