Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 3 March 2011. European Commission v Kingdom of Belgium. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 2002/22/EC - Article 31 - Criteria for awarding ‘must-carry’ status - General interest objectives permitting the award of that status - Impact of the number of end-users of communications networks on the award of that status - Principle of proportionality.
