Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 5 October 2016. TMD Gesellschaft für transfusionsmedizinische Dienste mbH v Finanzamt Kassel II - Hofgeismar. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Hessisches Finanzgericht. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Taxation — Value added tax — Directive 2006/112/EC — Exemptions for certain activities in the public interest — Article 132(1)(d) — Supplies of human organs; blood and milk — Scope — Plasma of human blood transformed and used for industrial purposes.
