Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 21 January 2010.   Iride SpA and Iride Energia SpA v European Commission.   Appeal - State aid - Aid declared compatible with the common market on condition that its recipient repays earlier aid declared unlawful - Compatibility with Article 87(1) EC - Errors of law - Distortion of the appellants' arguments - Failure to state grounds - Appeal in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly unfounded.
