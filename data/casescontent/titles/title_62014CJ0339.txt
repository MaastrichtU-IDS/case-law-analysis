Judgment of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 21 May 2015. Criminal proceedings against Andreas Wittmann. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberlandesgericht Nürnberg. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2006/126/EC — Mutual recognition of driving licences — Period of prohibition — Issue of the driving licence by a Member State before the entry into force of a period of prohibition in the Member State of normal residence — Grounds for refusing to recognise in the Member State of normal residence the validity of a driving licence issued by another Member State.
