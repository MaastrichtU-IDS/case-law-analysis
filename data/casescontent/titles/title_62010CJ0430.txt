Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 17 November 2011. Hristo Gaydarov v Director na Glavna direktsia "Ohranitelna politsia" pri Ministerstvo na vatreshnite raboti. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Administrativen sad Sofia-grad - Bulgaria. Freedom of movement of a Union citizen - Directive 2004/38/EC - Prohibition on leaving national territory due to a criminal conviction in another country - Drug trafficking - Whether measure can be justified on grounds of public policy.
