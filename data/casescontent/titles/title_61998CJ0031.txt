Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 28 April 1999.   Peter Luksch v Hauptzollamt Weiden.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht München - Germany.   Agriculture - Common organisation of the markets - Fruit and vegetables - Importation of sour cherries from a third country - Levy of a countervailing charge equal to the difference between the minimum price and the import price - Applicability to spoiled goods.
