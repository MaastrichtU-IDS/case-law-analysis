Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 27 February 2014. European Commission v EnBW Energie Baden-Württemberg AG. Appeal — Regulation (EC) No 1049/2001 — Access to documents of the institutions — Documents relating to a proceeding under Article 81 EC — Regulation (EC) No 1/2003 and Regulation (EC) No 773/2004 — Access refused — Exceptions relating to the protection of investigations; commercial interests and the decision-making process of the institutions — Obligation upon the institution concerned to carry out a specific; individual examination of the content of the documents covered by the request for access.
