Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 19 September 2013. CHS Tour Services GmbH v Team4 Travel GmbH. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberster Gerichtshof. Directive 2005/29/EC — Unfair commercial practices — Sales brochure containing false information — Treated as ‘misleading commercial practice’ — Case in which the trader cannot be criticised for any breach of the duty of diligence.
