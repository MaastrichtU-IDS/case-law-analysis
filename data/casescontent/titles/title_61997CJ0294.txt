Judgment of the Court of 26 October 1999.   Eurowings Luftverkehrs AG v Finanzamt Dortmund-Unna.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Münster - Germany.   Freedom to provide services - Trade tax - Add-back to the taxable amount - Exemption inapplicable to the lessee where the proprietor of the goods leased is established in another Member State and is therefore not liable to the tax.
