Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber); 24 September 2013. Leyla Ecem Demirkan v Bundesrepublik Deutschland. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberverwaltungsgericht Berlin-Brandenburg. EEC-Turkey Association Agreement — Additional Protocol — Article 41(1) — ‘Standstill’ clause — Visa requirement for admission to the territory of a Member State — Freedom to provide services — The right of a Turkish national to enter a Member State in order to visit a family member and; potentially; to receive services.
