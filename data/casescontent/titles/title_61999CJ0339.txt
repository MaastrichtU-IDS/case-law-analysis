Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 17 October 2002.   Energie Steiermark Holding AG v Finanzlandesdirektion für Steiermark.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Directive 69/335/EEC - Indirect taxes on the raising of capital - Capital duty - Increase in a company's capital by the issue of new shares - Payments made upon the entry of a new member as a shareholder in the company - Payments made by the parent company of the new member - Payments made to the subsidiaries of the company increasing its capital - Payments not yet made.
