Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 25 June 2009.   Devrajan Srinivasan v European Ombudsman.   Appeal in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly unfounded - European Ombudsman - Decision to take no further action on a complaint of maladministration - Action for annulment - Dismissal on the grounds of manifest inadmissibility and manifest lack of jurisdiction - Challenge.
