Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 21 December 2011.   Teresa Cicala v Regione Siciliana.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Corte dei Conti - sezione giurisdizionale per la Regione Siciliana - Italy.   National administrative procedure - Administrative acts - Obligation to state reasons - Possibility of failure to state reasons being remedied during legal proceedings against an administrative act - Interpretation of the second paragraph of Article 296 TFEU and Article 41(2)(c) of the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union - Lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
