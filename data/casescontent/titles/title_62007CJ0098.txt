Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 6 March 2008.   Nordania Finans A/S and BG Factoring A/S v Skatteministeriet.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Højesteret - Denmark.   Sixth VAT Directive - Article 19(2) - Calculation of the deductible proportion - Exclusion of amounts of turnover attributable to the supplies of capital goods used by the taxable person for the purposes of his business - Notion of ‘capital goods used by the taxable person for the purposes of his business’ - Vehicles purchased by a leasing company to be leased and subsequently sold on termination of the respective leasing contracts.
