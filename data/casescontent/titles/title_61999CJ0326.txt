Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 4 October 2001.   Stichting "Goed Wonen" v Staatssecretaris van Financiën.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands.   Sixth VAT Directive - Power of a Member State to treat certain rights in rem in immovable property as tangible property capable of supply - Restriction of the exercise of that power to cases where the price of the right in rem is at least equal to the economic value of the property concerned - Letting and leasing of immovable property - Exemptions.
