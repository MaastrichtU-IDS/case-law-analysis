Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 12 May 2011.   Grand Duchy of Luxemburg v European Parliament and Council of the European Union.   Action for annulment - Directive 2009/12/EC - Airport charges - Scope - Airports whose annual traffic is over 5 million passenger movements per year and those with the highest passenger movements in each Member State - Validity - Principles of equal treatment; proportionality and subsidiarity.
