Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 1 December 2016. Mohamed Daouidi v Bootes Plus SL and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Juzgado de lo Social de Barcelona. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Social policy — Directive 2000/78/EC — Equal treatment in employment and occupation — Articles 1 to 3 — Prohibition of all discrimination based on a disability — Whether a ‘disability’ exists — Concept of ‘long-term physical; mental; intellectual or sensory impairments’ — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Articles 3; 15; 21; 30; 31; 34 and 35 — Dismissal of a worker who is temporarily unable to work; within the definition of national law; for an indeterminate period of time.
