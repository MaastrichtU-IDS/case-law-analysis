Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 12 July 2012. European Commission v Kingdom of Spain. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations — Articles 18 EC; 39 EC and 43 EC — Articles 28 and 31 of the EEA Agreement — Tax legislation — Transfer abroad of a taxpayer’s residence — Obligation to include any income not yet charged to tax in the tax base for the preceding tax year — Loss of the advantage to be gained by deferring the tax debt.
