Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 12 February 2009. Margarete Block v Finanzamt Kaufbeuren. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany. Free movement of capital - Articles 56 EC and 58 EC - Inheritance tax - National rules not allowing inheritance tax in respect of capital claims; paid by an heir in one Member State; to be credited against inheritance tax payable in another Member State where the owner of the assets was resident at the time of death - Double taxation - Restriction - None.
