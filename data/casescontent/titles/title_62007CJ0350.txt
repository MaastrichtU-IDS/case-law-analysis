Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 5 March 2009. Kattner Stahlbau GmbH v Maschinenbau- und Metall- Berufsgenossenschaft. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Sächsisches Landessozialgericht - Germany. Competition - Articles 81 EC; 82 EC and 86 EC - Compulsory affiliation to a body providing insurance against accidents at work and occupational diseases - Concept of an ‘undertaking’ - Abuse of dominant position - Freedom to provide services - Articles 49 EC and 50 EC - Restriction - Justification - Risk of serious harm to the financial equilibrium of the social security scheme.
