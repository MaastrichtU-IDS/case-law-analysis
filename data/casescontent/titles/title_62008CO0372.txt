Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 5 May 2009.   Atlantic Dawn Ltd and Others v Commission of the European Communities.   Appeal - Regulation (EC) No 147/2007 - Reduction in mackerel quotas allocated to Ireland for years 2007 to 2012 - Action for annulment of Regulation No 147/2007 brought by a group of Irish fishermen comprising 20 out of 23 licence holders from the Refrigerated Sea Water pelagic fleet - Inadmissibility - Appeal clearly unfounded.
