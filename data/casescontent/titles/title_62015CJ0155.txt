Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 7 June 2016. George Karim v Migrationsverket. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Kammarrätten i Stockholm - Migrationsöverdomstolen. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Regulation (EU) No 604/2013 — Determining the Member State responsible for examining applications for asylum lodged in one of the Member States by a third-country national — Article 18 — Taking back an asylum seeker whose application is under examination — Article 19 — Cessation of responsibilities — Absence from the territory of the Member States for a period of at least three months — New procedure for determining the Member State responsible — Article 27 — Remedy — Extent of judicial review.
