Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 10 July 2009. Apple Computer; Inc. v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM). Appeal - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 8(1)(b) - Word mark QUARTZ - Opposition by the proprietor of the Community figurative mark QUARTZ - Refusal to register - Similarity between goods - Likelihood of confusion - Appeal clearly inadmissible.
