Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 16  September  2004.   Commission of the European Communities v Republic of Austria.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Freedom of movement for workers - Nationals of the Union or the EEA - Nationals of a non-member State associated with the Community by way of an agreement - Eligibility for election to workers' chambers and works councils - Principle of non-discrimination as regards conditions of work.
