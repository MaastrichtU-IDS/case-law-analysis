Order of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 3 September 2015. Manuel Orrego Arias v Subdelegación del Gobierno en Ciudad Real. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal Superior de Justicia de Castilla-La Mancha. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Area of freedom; security and justice — Directive 2001/40/EC — Mutual recognition of decisions on the expulsion of third country nationals — Article 3(1)(a) — Concept of ‘offence punishable by a penalty involving deprivation of liberty of at least one year’ — Decision on the expulsion of a third country national owing to a criminal conviction — Situation which is not covered by Directive 2001/40 — Manifest lack of jurisdiction.
