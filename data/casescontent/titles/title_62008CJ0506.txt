Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 21 July 2011.   Kingdom of Sweden v European Commission and MyTravel Group plc.   Appeal - Access to documents of the institutions - Regulation (EC) No 1049/2001 - Article 4(2); second indent; and Article 4(3); second subparagraph - Exceptions to the right of access concerning the protection of court proceedings and legal advice and the decision-making process - Control of concentrations - Commission documents drawn up in the context of a procedure which led to a decision declaring a concentration operation incompatible with the common market - Documents drafted following the annulment of that decision by the General Court.
