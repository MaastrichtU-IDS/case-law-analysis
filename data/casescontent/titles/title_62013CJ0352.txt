Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 21 May 2015. Cartel Damage Claims (CDC) Hydrogen Peroxide SA v Evonik Degussa GmbH and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Landgericht Dortmund. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Area of freedom; security and justice — Judicial cooperation in civil and commercial matters — Regulation (EC) No 44/2001 — Special jurisdiction — Article 6(1) — Action; brought against several defendants domiciled in various Member States and which have participated in a cartel found to be contrary to Article 81 EC and Article 53 of the Agreement on the European Economic Area; seeking an order for the defendants to pay damages jointly and severally and for disclosure of information — Jurisdiction of the court seised with regard to the other defendants — Withdrawal of the action in relation to the defendant domiciled in the Member State of the court seised — Jurisdiction in tort; delict or quasi-delict — Article 5(3) — Jurisdiction clauses — Article 23 — Effective enforcement of the prohibition of anti-competitive agreements; decisions and concerted practices.
