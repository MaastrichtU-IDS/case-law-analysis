Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber); 18 September 2014. Vueling Airlines SA v Instituto Galego de Consumo de la Xunta de Galicia. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Juzgado de lo Contencioso-Administrativo No 1 de Ourense. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Air Transport — Common rules for the operation of air services in the European Union — Regulation (EC) No 1008/2008 — Pricing freedom — Checking in baggage — Price supplement — Concept of ‘air fares’ — Consumer protection — Imposition of a fine on an air carrier for an unfair contract term — National law requiring the carriage of passenger and checked-in baggage to be included in the base price of a plane ticket — Whether compatible with EU law.
