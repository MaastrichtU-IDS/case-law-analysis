Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 13 January 2015.   Asos plc v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Word mark ASOS - Opposition by the proprietor of the Community word mark ASSOS - Partial refusal to register - Assessment of the facts and evidence by the General Court of the European Union - Challenge - Distortion of the facts or evidence - No distortion - Appeal manifestly inadmissible.
