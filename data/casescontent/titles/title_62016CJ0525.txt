Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 19 April 2018. MEO – Serviços de Comunicações e Multimédia SA v Autoridade da Concorrência. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal da Concorrência; Regulação e Supervisão. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Competition — Abuse of dominant position — Article 102; second paragraph; point (c); TFEU — Concept of ‘competitive disadvantage’ — Discriminatory prices on a downstream market — Cooperative for the management of rights relating to copyright — Royalty payable by domestic entities which provide a paid television signal transmission service and television content.
