Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 24 October 2013.   Andreas Ingemar Thiele Meneses v Region Hannover.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgericht Hannover - Germany.   Citizenship of the Union - Articles 20 TFEU and 21 TFEU - Right of free movement and residence - National of a Member State - Studies pursued in another Member State - Education or training grant - Permanent residence requirement - Place of education or training located in the applicant’s State of residence or in a neighbouring State - Limited exception - Applicant’s specific circumstances.
