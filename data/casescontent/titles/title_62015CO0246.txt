Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 14 July 2016. Pollmeier Massivholz GmbH &amp; Co. KG v European Commission. Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court — State aid — State measures concerning the establishment of a sawmill in Land Hessen (Germany) — Decision finding no State aid — Formal investigation procedure not initiated — Appeal manifestly inadmissible in part and manifestly unfounded in part.
