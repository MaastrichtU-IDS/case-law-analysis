Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 8 December 2011. Residex Capital IV CV v Gemeente Rotterdam. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands. Article 88(3) EC - State aid - Aid granted in the form of a guarantee to a lender for the purpose of enabling the latter to grant a loan to a borrower - Infringement of procedural rules - Obligation to recover - Nullity - Powers of the national court.
