Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 13 October 2011.   Waypoint Aviation SA v Belgian State - SPF Finances.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d'appel de Bruxelles - Belgium.   Freedom to provide services - Tax legislation - Tax credit on income from loans granted for the acquisition of assets used on national territory - Exclusion of assets for which the right to use is transferred to a third party established in another Member State.
