Order of the Court (Third Chamber) of 6 October 2015. Ford Motor Company v Wheeltrims srl. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale ordinario di Torino. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Designs — Directive 98/71/EC — Article 14 — Regulation (EC) No 6/2002 — Article 110 — So-called ‘repair’ clause — Use by a third party of a trade mark; without the consent of the owner; for replacement parts or accessories for motor vehicles identical to the goods for which the trade mark is registered.
