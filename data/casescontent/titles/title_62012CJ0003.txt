Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 13 June 2013.   Syndicat OP 84 v Établissement national des produits de l’agriculture et de la mer (FranceAgriMer).   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Conseil d'État - France.   Agriculture - European Agricultural Guidance and Guarantee Fund - ‘Scrutiny period’ - Possibility for a Member State to extend the scrutiny period where it is impossible to carry out that scrutiny in the time allowed - Repayment of financial assistance - Penalties.
