Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 20 October 2016. Evelyn Danqua v Minister for Justice and Equality and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Court of Appeal. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2004/83/EC — Minimum standards for granting refugee status or subsidiary protection status — National procedural rule laying down; for the submission of an application for subsidiary protection; a period of 15 working days from notification of the rejection of the application for asylum — Procedural autonomy of the Member States — Principle of equivalence — Principle of effectiveness — Proper conduct of the procedure for examining the application for subsidiary protection — Proper conduct of the return procedure — Not compatible.
