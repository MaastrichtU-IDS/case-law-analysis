Judgment of the Court of 13 March 2001. PreussenElektra AG v Schhleswag AG; in the presence of Windpark Reußenköge III GmbH and Land Schleswig-Holstein. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landgericht Kiel - Germany. Electricity - Renewable sources of energy - National legislation requiring electricity supply undertakings to purchase electricity at minimum prices and apportioning the resulting costs between those undertakings and upstream network operators - State aid - Compatibility with the free movement of goods.
