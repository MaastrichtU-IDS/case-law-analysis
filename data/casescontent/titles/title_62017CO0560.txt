Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 13 March 2018. Mediaexpert sp. z o.o. v European Union Intellectual Property Office. Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — EU trade mark — Invalidity proceedings — Figurative mark in black; yellow and white; containing the word element ‘mediaexpert’ — Failure to submit a translation of the registration certificate for the earlier mark — Rejection of the application for a declaration of invalidity.
