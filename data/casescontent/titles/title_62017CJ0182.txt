Judgment of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 22 February 2018. Nagyszénás Településszolgáltatási Nonprofit Kft. v Nemzeti Adó- és Vámhivatal Fellebbviteli Igazgatósága. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Kúria. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Value added tax (VAT) — Directive 2006/112/EC — Article 2(1)(c); Article 9 and Article 13(1) — Treatment as a non-taxable person — Definition of ‘body governed by public law’ — Commercial company 100% owned by a municipality and responsible for performing certain public tasks incumbent on that municipality — Those tasks and their remuneration determined in a contract between the company and the municipality.
