Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 12 September 2013.   The Commissioners for Her Majesty's Revenue &amp; Customs v Sunico ApS and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Østre Landsret - Denmark.   Judicial cooperation in civil matters - Jurisdiction and the recognition and enforcement of judgments in civil and commercial matters - Regulation (EC) No 44/2001 - Article 1(1) - Scope - Concept of ‘civil and commercial matters’ - Action brought by a public authority - Damages in respect of involvement in a tax fraud by a third party not subject to VAT.
