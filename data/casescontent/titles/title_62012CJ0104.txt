Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 21 February 2013. Finanzamt Köln-Nord v Wolfram Becker. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesfinanzhof. Sixth VAT Directive — Article 17(2)(a) — Right to deduct input tax — Need for a direct and immediate link between an input and an output transaction — Criterion for determining that link — Services of lawyers performed in the context of criminal proceedings for corruption brought in a personal capacity against the managing director and main partner of a limited company.
