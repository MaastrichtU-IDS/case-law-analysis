Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 17 July 2008.   Commission of the European Communities v Republic of Austria.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 89/105/EEC - Inclusion of medicinal products for human use in national health insurance systems - Article 6(1) - List of medicinal products covered by the national health insurance system establishing three separate categories with different conditions for reimbursement - Time-limit for adopting a decision on an application for inclusion of a medicinal product in the categories of that list offering the most favourable reimbursement conditions.
