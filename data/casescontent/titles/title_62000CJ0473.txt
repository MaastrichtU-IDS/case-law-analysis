Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 21 November 2002.   Cofidis SA v Jean-Louis Fredout.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal d'instance de Vienne - France.   Directive 93/13/EEC - Unfair terms in consumer contracts - Action brought by a seller or supplier - National provision prohibiting the national court from finding a term unfair; of its own motion or following a plea raised by the consumer; after the expiry of a limitation period.
