Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 10 March 2005. QDQ Media SA v Alejandro Omedas Lecha. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Juzgado de Primera Instancia nº 35 de Barcelona - Spain. Directive 2000/35/EC - Definition of recovery costs - Expenses of abogado or procurador in judicial proceedings where use of those legal practitioners is not required - Impossible to include in the costs on the basis of national law - Impossible to rely on the directive against an individual.
