Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 26 May 2005. Kingscrest Associates Ltd and Montecello Ltd v Commissioners of Customs &amp; Excise. Reference for a preliminary ruling: VAT and Duties Tribunal; London - United Kingdom. Sixth VAT Directive - Article 13A(1)(g) and (h) - Exempt transactions - Supplies closely linked to welfare and social security work - Supplies closely linked to the protection of children and young persons - Supplies made by bodies other than those governed by public law and recognised as charitable by the Member State concerned - Private; profit-making entity - Meaning of "charitable".
