Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber); 21 June 2012. Titus Alexander Jochen Donner. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesgerichtshof. Free movement of goods — Industrial and commercial property — Sale of copies of works in a Member State in which the copyright on those works is not protected — Transport of those goods to another Member State in which the infringement of the copyright is sanctioned under criminal law — Criminal proceedings against the transporter for aiding and abetting the unlawful distribution of a work protected by copyright law.
