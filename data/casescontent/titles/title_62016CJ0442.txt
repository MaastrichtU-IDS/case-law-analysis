Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 20 December 2017. Florea Gusa v Minister for Social Protection and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Court of Appeal (Ireland). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2004/38/EC — Person no longer working in a self-employed capacity — Retention of the status of self-employed person — Right of residence — Legislation of a Member State restricting eligibility for a jobseeker’s allowance to persons who have a right of residence on the territory of that Member State.
