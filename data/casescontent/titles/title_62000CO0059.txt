Order of the Court (Second Chamber) of 3 December 2001.   Bent Mousten Vestergaard v Spøttrup Boligselskab.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Vestre Landsret - Denmark.   Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Public works contracts - Contracts with a value below the threshold values laid down in Directive 93/37/EEC - Clause requiring the use of a product of a specified make; without any possibility of using a similar product - Free movement of goods.
