Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 14 November 2006. Mark Kerckhaert and Bernadette Morres v Belgische Staat. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Rechtbank van eerste aanleg te Gent - Belgium. Income tax - Dividends - Tax burden on dividends from shareholdings in companies established in another Member State - No possibility in the State of residence to set off income tax levied at source in another Member State.
