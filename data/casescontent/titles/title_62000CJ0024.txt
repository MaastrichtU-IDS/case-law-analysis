Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 5 February 2004.   Commission of the European Communities v French Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Articles 30 and 36 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Articles 28 EC and 30 EC) - National legislation exhaustively listing the nutrients which may be added to foodstuffs - Measure having equivalent effect - Justification - Public health - Consumer protection - Proportionality.
