Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 30 June 2016. Slovenská pošta a.s. v European Commission. Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Public undertakings — Amendment of Slovakian legislation on postal services — Exclusive rights granted to incumbent operator Slovenská pošta a.s. to provide ‘hybrid’ mail services — Decision declaring those provisions incompatible with Articles 86 EC and 82 EC.
