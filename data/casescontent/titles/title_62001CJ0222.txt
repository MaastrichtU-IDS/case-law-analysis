Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 29 April 2004.   British American Tobacco Manufacturing BV v Hauptzollamt Krefeld.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany.   Free movement of goods - External Community transit - Temporary removal of transit and transport documents - Breaking of seals and partial unloading of the goods - Removal of goods from customs supervision - Incurring of a customs debt on importation - Unsuspected presence of undercover customs agents - Special circumstances justifying the remission or repayment of import duties - Responsibility of the principal in the case of deception or obvious negligence on the part of persons engaged by him.
