Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 5 July 2012. Michel Bourgès-Maunoury and Marie-Louise Heintz; wife of Bourgès-Maunoury v Direction des services fiscaux d’Eure-et-Loir. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the tribunal de grande instance de Chartres. Privileges and immunities of the European Communities – Exemption from national taxes on salaries paid by the European Union – Inclusion of income paid by the European Union in the calculation of the cap for wealth tax.
