Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 19 December 2013.   Corman-Collins SA v La Maison du Whisky SA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal de commerce de Verviers - Belgium.   Jurisdiction in civil and commercial matters - Regulation (EC) No 44/2001 - Article 2 - Article 5(1)(a) and (b) - Special jurisdiction in matters relating to contract - Concepts of ‘sale of goods’ and ‘supply of services’ - Agreement for the distribution of goods.
