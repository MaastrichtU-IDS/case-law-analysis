Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 11 May 2012.   Lan Airlines SA v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Article 119 of the Rules of Procedure - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 8(1)(b) - Community word mark LÍNEAS AÉREAS DEL MEDITERRÁNEO LAM - Application for registration - Opposition by the proprietor of the earlier Community word and figurative marks LAN - Rejection of the opposition - No likelihood of confusion - Appeal manifestly inadmissible.
