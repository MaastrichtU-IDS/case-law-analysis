Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 4 February 2015. Office national de l'emploi v Marie-Rose Melchior. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Cour du travail de Bruxelles. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Social security — Conditions governing eligibility for unemployment benefit in a Member State — Taking into account periods of work completed as a member of the contract staff of an institution of the European Union which is established in that Member State — Treatment of days of unemployment for which an allowance is paid under the Conditions of Employment of Other Servants of the European Communities as working days — Principle of sincere cooperation.
