Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 17 March 2011. Josep Peñarroja Fa. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour de cassation - France. Article 43 EC - Freedom of establishment - Article 49 EC - Freedom to provide services - Restrictions - Court experts who are professional translators - Exercise of official authority - National legislation reserving appointment as a court expert for persons enrolled in registers established by the national judicial authorities - Justification - Proportionality - Directive 2005/36/EC - Concept of ‘regulated profession’.
