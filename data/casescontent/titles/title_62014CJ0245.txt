Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 22 October 2015. Thomas Cook Belgium NV v Thurner Hotel GmbH. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Handelsgericht Wien. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Area of freedom; security and justice — Regulation (EC) No 1896/2006 — European order for payment procedure — Late statement of opposition — Article 20(2) — Application for review of the European order for payment — Objection to the jurisdiction of the court of origin — European order for payment wrongly issued having regard to the requirements laid down in the regulation — Not ‘clearly’ wrongly issued — No ‘exceptional’ circumstances.
