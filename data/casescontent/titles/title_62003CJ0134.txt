Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 17 February 2005.   Viacom Outdoor Srl v Giotto Immobilier SARL.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Giudice di pace di Genova-Voltri - Italy.   Freedom to provide services - Competition - Bill-posting services - Domestic legislation imposing a municipal tax on advertising - Supply by municipalities of a public bill-posting service - Power of the municipalities to regulate the supply of bill-posting services - Internal taxation not discriminatory.
