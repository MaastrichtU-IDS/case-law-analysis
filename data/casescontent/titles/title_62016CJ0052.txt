Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 6 March 2018. 'SEGRO' Kft. v Vas Megyei Kormányhivatal Sárvári Járási Földhivatala and Günther Horváth v Vas Megyei Kormányhivatal. Requests for a preliminary ruling from the Szombathelyi Közigazgatási és Munkaügyi Bíróság. References for a preliminary ruling — Article 63 TFEU — Free movement of capital — Rights of usufruct over agricultural land — National legislation permitting such rights to be acquired in the future only by close family members of the owner of the land and cancelling; without providing for compensation; the rights previously acquired by legal persons or by natural persons who cannot demonstrate a close family tie with the owner of the land.
