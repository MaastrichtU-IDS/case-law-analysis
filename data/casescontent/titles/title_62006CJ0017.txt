Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 11 September 2007. Céline SARL v Céline SA. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d'appel de Nancy - France. Trade marks - Articles 5(1)(a) and 6(1)(a) of First Directive 89/104/EEC - Right of the proprietor of a registered trade mark to oppose the use by a third party of a sign which is identical to the mark - Use of the sign as a company; trade or shop name - Right of the third party to use his name.
