Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 3 October 2006. Nicolae Bot v Préfet du Val-de-Marne. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Conseil d'État - France. Convention implementing the Schengen Agreement - Article 20(1) - Conditions of movement of nationals of a third country not subject to a visa requirement - Maximum stay for a period of three months during the six months following the date of first entry into the Schengen Area - Successive stays - Definition of "first entry'.
