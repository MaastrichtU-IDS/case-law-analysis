Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 13 July 2006. Vincenzo Manfredi v Lloyd Adriatico Assicurazioni SpA (C-295/04); Antonio Cannito v Fondiaria Sai SpA (C-296/04) and Nicolò Tricarico (C-297/04) and Pasqualina Murgolo (C-298/04) v Assitalia SpA. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Giudice di pace di Bitonto - Italy. Article 81 EC- Competition - Agreements; decisions and concerted practices - Accidents caused by motor vehicles; vessels and mopeds - Compulsory civil liability insurance - Increase in premiums - Effect on trade between Member States - Right of third parties to claim compensation for harm suffered - National courts and tribunals having jurisdiction - Limitation period - Punitive damages.
