Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 20 January 2011. General Química SA and Others v European Commission. Appeal - Competition - Agreements; decisions and concerted practices - Rubber chemicals sector - Decision finding an infringement of Article 81 EC - Group of undertakings - Joint and several liability of a parent company for infringements of the competition rules committed by its subsidiaries - Attribution of liability to the parent company at the head of a group.
