Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 17 July 2008. S. Coleman v Attridge Law and Steve Law. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Employment Tribunal; London South - United Kingdom. Social policy - Directive 2000/78/EC - Equal treatment in employment and occupation - Articles 1; 2(1); (2)(a) and (3) and 3(1)(c) - Direct discrimination on grounds of disability - Harassment related to disability - Dismissal of an employee who is not himself disabled but whose child is disabled - Included - Burden of proof.
