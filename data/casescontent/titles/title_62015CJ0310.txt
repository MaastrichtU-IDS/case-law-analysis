Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 7 September 2016. Vincent Deroo-Blanquart v Sony Europe Limited. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Cour de cassation (France). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Consumer protection — Unfair commercial practices — Directive 2005/29/EC — Articles 5 and 7 — Combined offer — Sale of a computer equipped with pre-installed software — Material information relating to the price — Misleading omission — Consumer unable to obtain the same model of computer not equipped with software.
