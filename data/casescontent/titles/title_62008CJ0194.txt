Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 1 July 2010.   Susanne Gassmayr v Bundesminister für Wissenschaft und Forschung   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   Social policy - Directive 92/85/EEC - Introduction of measures to encourage improvements in the safety and health at work of pregnant workers and workers who have recently given birth or are breastfeeding - Articles 5(3) and 11(1) to (3) - Direct effect - Pregnant worker granted leave during her pregnancy - Worker on maternity leave - Right to payment of an on-call duty allowance.
