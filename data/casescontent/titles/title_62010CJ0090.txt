Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 22 September 2011.   European Commission v Kingdom of Spain.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - 'Habitats' directive - Conservation of natural habitats - Wild fauna and flora - Articles 4(4) and 6(1) and (2) - Establishment of priorities for special areas of conservation and of adequate protection thereof - Failure to ensure adequate legal protection of the special areas of conservation in the Canary Islands.
