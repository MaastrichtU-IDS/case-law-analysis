Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 10 May 2007.   Ypourgos Oikonomikon and Proïstamenos DOY Amfissas v Charilaos Georgakis.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Symvoulio tis Epikrateias - Greece.   Directive 89/592/EEC - Insider dealing - Meaning of ‘inside information’ and ‘taking advantage of inside information’ - Stock-market transactions agreed on in advance and carried out within a group of persons capable of being insider dealers - Artificial increase in the price of transferable securities disposed of.
