Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 13 March 2007.   Arizona Chemical BV; Eastman Belgium BVBA and Cray Valley Iberica SA v Commission of the European Communities.   Appeal - Directive 67/548/EEC - Refusal to declassify rosin as a dangerous substance - Action for annulment - Act not open to challenge - Breach of the right to an effective legal remedy - Action for damages - Appeal clearly unfounded.
