Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 29 April 2004.   Commission of the European Communities v Federal Republic of Germany.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Articles 30 and 36 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Articles 28 EC and 30 EC) - Directive 65/65/EEC - Food preparations containing three times more vitamins than the recommended daily amount - Preparations lawfully marketed as food supplements in the Member State of exportation - Preparations classified as medicinal products in the Member State of importation - "Medicinal product" - Obstacle - Justification - Public health - Proportionality - Admissibility of the application.
