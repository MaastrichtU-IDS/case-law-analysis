Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 15 December 2011. Banca Antoniana Popolare Veneta SpA v Ministero dell'Economia e delle Finanze and Agenzia delle Entrate. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Corte suprema di cassazione - Italy. VAT - Recovery of VAT paid but not due - National legislation under which actions may be brought for the recovery of sums paid but not due; before different courts and subject to different time-limits; depending on whether the claimant is the recipient of the services or their supplier - Possibility for the recipient to claim a VAT refund from the supplier after the expiry of the time-limits within which the supplier is able to bring an action against the tax authority - Principle of effectiveness.
