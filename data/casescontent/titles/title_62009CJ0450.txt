Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 31 March 2011. Ulrich Schröder v Finanzamt Hameln. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Niedersächsisches Finanzgericht - Germany. Free movement of capital - Direct taxation - Taxation of income from the letting of immovable property - Deductibility of annuities paid to a relative in the context of an anticipated succession inter vivos - Condition of being subject to unlimited tax liability in the Member State at issue.
