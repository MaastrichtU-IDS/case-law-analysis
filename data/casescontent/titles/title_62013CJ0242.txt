Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 17 September 2014. Commerz Nederland NV v Havenbedrijf Rotterdam NV. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Competition — State aid — Article 107(1) TFEU — Definition of aid — Guarantees provided by a public undertaking to a bank to facilitate lending to third party creditors — Guarantees deliberately provided by the director of that public undertaking in disregard of that undertaking’s statutes — Presumption of opposition by the public body that owns that undertaking — Whether the guarantees may be imputed to the State.
