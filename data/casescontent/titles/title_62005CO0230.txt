Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 27 April 2006.   L v Commission of the European Communities.   Appeal - Officials - Harassment - Commission's duty to provide assistance - Liability - Refusal by the Court of First Instance to allow the examination of witnesses - Offer of supplementary evidence which did not exist at the closing of the written procedure - Refusal to withdraw an allegedly defamatory document from the file - Obligation to provide reasons - Principal of sound administration - Appeal in part manifestly unfounded and in part manifestly inadmissible.
