Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 23 April 2009. Copad SA v Christian Dior couture SA; Vincent Gladel and Société industrielle lingerie (SIL). Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour de Cassation - France. Directive 89/104/EEC - Trade-mark law - Exhaustion of the rights of the proprietor of the trade mark - Licence agreement - Sale of goods bearing the trade mark in disregard of a clause in the licence agreement - No consent of the proprietor of the mark - Sale to discount stores - Damage to the reputation of the trade mark.
