Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 13 July 2017. Assens Havn v Navigators Management (UK) Limited. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Højesteret. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Judicial cooperation in civil matters — Regulation (EC) No 44/2001 — Jurisdiction in insurance matters — National legislation providing; on certain conditions; for an injured person’s right to bring legal proceedings directly against the insurer of the person responsible for an accident — Agreement on jurisdiction concluded between the insurer and the party which caused the damage.
