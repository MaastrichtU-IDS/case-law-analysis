Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 15 June 2000.   Commission of the European Communities v Federal Republic of Germany.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Trade with the German Democratic Republic prior to German reunification - Regulation (EEC) No 2252/90 - Abolition of customs formalities - Failure to charge import levies in inter-German trade - Failure to make own resources available to the Commission.
