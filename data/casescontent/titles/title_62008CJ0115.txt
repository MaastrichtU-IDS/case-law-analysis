Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 27 October 2009. Land Oberösterreich v ČEZ as. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landesgericht Linz - Austria. Action for cessation of actual or potential nuisance caused to land by the activities of a nuclear power plant situated on the territory of another Member State - Obligation to tolerate actual or potential nuisance caused by installations which have been officially authorised in the Member State where the action is brought - Authorisations issued in other Member States not taken into account - Equal treatment - Principle of non-discrimination on grounds of nationality under the EAEC Treaty.
