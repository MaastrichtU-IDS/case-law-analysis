Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 10 September 2002.   Ambulanter Pflegedienst Kügler GmbH v Finanzamt für Körperschaften I in Berlin.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany.   Article 13(A)(1)(c) and (g) of the Sixth Directive (77/388/EEC) - Exemption of care provided by capital companies - Services closely linked to welfare and social security work supplied by organisations; not being bodies governed by public law; recognised as charitable by the Member State concerned - Direct effect.
