Judgment of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 13 February 2014.   European Commission v Kingdom of Belgium.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Area of freedom; security and justice - Standards for security features and biometrics in passports and travel documents issued by Member States - Regulation (EC) No 2252/2004 - Biometric passport - Inclusion of digital fingerprints - Failure to implement - Failure to issue within the prescribed periods.
