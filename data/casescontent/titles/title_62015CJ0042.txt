Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 9 November 2016. Home Credit Slovakia a.s. v Klára Bíróová. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Okresný súd Dunajská Streda. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2008/48/EC — Consumer protection — Consumer credit — Article 1; Article 3(m); Article 10(1) and (2); Article 22(1) and Article 23 — Interpretation of the expressions ‘on paper’ and ‘on another durable medium’ — Contract referring to another document — Requirement for the agreement to be in ‘written form’ within the meaning of national law — Indication of information required by reference to objective criteria — Information to be included in a fixed-term credit agreement — Effect of failure to include mandatory information — Proportionality.
