Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 17 February 2009. Meki Elgafaji and Noor Elgafaji v Staatssecretaris van Justitie. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Raad van State - Netherlands. Directive 2004/83/EC - Minimum standards for determining who qualifies for refugee status or for subsidiary protection status - Person eligible for subsidiary protection - Article 2(e) - Real risk of suffering serious harm - Article 15(c) - Serious and individual threat to a civilian’s life or person by reason of indiscriminate violence in situations of armed conflict - Proof .
