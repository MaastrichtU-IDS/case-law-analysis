Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 7 November 2013. K. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Korkein hallinto-oikeus. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Articles 63 TFEU and 65 TFEU — Free movement of capital — Tax legislation of a Member State which does not allow deduction of the loss on the sale of immovable property situated in another Member State from the gain on the sale of securities in the Member State of taxation.
