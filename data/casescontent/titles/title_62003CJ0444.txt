Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 12 May 2005.   Meta Fackler KG v Bundesrepublik Deutschland.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgericht Berlin - Germany.   Medicinal products for human use - Homeopathic medicinal products - National provision excluding from the special; simplified registration procedure a medicinal product composed of known homeopathic substances if its use as a homeopathic medicinal product is not generally known.
