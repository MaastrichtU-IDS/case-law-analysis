Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 20 January 2011.   European Commission v Hellenic Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Articles 12 EC; 18 EC; 39 EC and 43 EC - Articles 4; 28 and 31 of the Agreement on the European Economic Area - Tax legislation - Conditions for exemption from transfer tax on the first purchase of immoveable property - Exemption granted solely to persons residing in Greece and to persons of Greek origin not residing in Greece at the date of purchase.
