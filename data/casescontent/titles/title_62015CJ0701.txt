Judgment of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 13 July 2017. Malpensa Logistica Europa SpA v SEA - Società Esercizi Aeroportuali SpA. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale Amministrativo Regionale per la Lombardia. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Public procurement — Transport — Definition of ‘exploitation of a geographical area for the purpose of the provisions of airports or other terminal facilities to carriers by air’ — Directives 2004/17/EC and 96/67/EC — National legislation which does not require a tendering procedure to be conducted prior to the allocation of areas within an airport.
