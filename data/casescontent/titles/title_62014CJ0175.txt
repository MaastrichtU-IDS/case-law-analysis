Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 5 March 2015. Ralph Prankl. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Verwaltungsgerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Taxation — Directive 92/12/EEC — General arrangements for products subject to excise duty — Imposition of duty on smuggled goods — Goods released for consumption in one Member State and transported to another Member State — Determination of the competent Member State — Right of the transit State to impose duty on those goods.
