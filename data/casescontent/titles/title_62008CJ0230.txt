Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 29 April 2010. Dansk Transport og Logistik v Skatteministeriet. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Østre Landsret - Denmark. Community Customs Code - Articles 202; 215(1) and (3); 217(1) and point (d) of the first paragraph of Article 233 - Notion of goods which are ‘seized and simultaneously or subsequently confiscated’ - Regulation implementing the Customs Code - Article 867a - Directive 92/12/EEC - Articles 5(1) and (2); 6; 7(1); 8 and 9 - Sixth VAT Directive - Articles 7; 10(3) and 16(1) - Unlawful introduction of goods - Transport of goods with a TIR carnet - Seizure and destruction - Determination of the Member State in which the customs debt is incurred and VAT and excise duty become chargeable - Extinction of the customs and tax debt.
