Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 20 June 2013. Her Majesty’s Commissioners of Revenue and Customs v Paul Newey. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Upper Tribunal (Tax and Chancery Chamber). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Sixth VAT Directive — Article 2(1) and Article 6(1) — Meaning of ‘supply of services’ — Supply of advertising and loan broking services — Exemptions — Economic and commercial reality of the transactions — Abusive practices — Transactions with the sole aim of obtaining a tax advantage.
