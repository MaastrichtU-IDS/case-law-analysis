Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 3 July 2014. Eycke Braun v Land Baden-Württemberg. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Amtsgericht Karlsruhe. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Taxation — Directive 69/335/EEC — Indirect taxes on the raising of capital — Article 10(c) — Conversion of a capital company into a different type of capital company not involving any increase in capital — Fees demanded for the drawing up of a notarial act recording that conversion.
