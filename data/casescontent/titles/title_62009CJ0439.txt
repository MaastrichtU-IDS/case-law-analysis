Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 13 October 2011. Pierre Fabre Dermo-Cosmétique SAS v Président de l’Autorité de la concurrence and Ministre de l’Économie; de l’Industrie et de l’Emploi. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d'appel de Paris - France. Article 101(1) and (3) TFEU - Regulation (EC) No 2790/1999 - Articles 2 to 4 - Competition - Restrictive practice - Selective distribution network - Cosmetics and personal care products - General and absolute ban on internet sales - Ban imposed by the supplier on authorised distributors.
