Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 29 April 2004. Commission of the European Communities v Republic of Austria. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Articles 28 EC and 30 EC - Directive 65/65/EEC - Food preparations containing vitamins A; D or K or minerals in the chromate group or containing more than once the daily amount of other vitamins or minerals - Preparations lawfully marketed as food supplements in the Member State of exportation - Preparations classified as medicinal products in the Member State of importation - ‘Medicinal product’ - Obstacle - Justification - Public health - Proportionality.
