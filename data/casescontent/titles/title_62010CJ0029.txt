Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 15 March 2011. Heiko Koelzsch v État du Grand Duchy of Luxemburg. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour d'appel - Luxembourg. Rome Convention on the law applicable to contractual obligations - Contract of employment - Choice made by the parties - Mandatory rules of the law applicable in the absence of choice - Determination of that law - Notion of the country in which the employee ‘habitually carries out his work’ - Employee carrying out his work in more than one Contracting State.
