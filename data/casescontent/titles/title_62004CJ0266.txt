Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 27 October 2005.   Nazairdis SAS; now Distribution Casino France SAS and Others v Caisse nationale de l'organisation autonome d'assurance vieillesse des travailleurs non salariés des professions industrielles et commerciales (Organic).   References for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal des affaires de sécurité sociale de Saint-Étienne (C-266/04 to C-270/04 and C-276/04) and Cour d'appel de Lyon (C-321/04 to C-325/04) - France.   Concept of aid - Tax assessed on the basis of sales area - Hypothecation of the tax revenue.
