Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 29 November 2011.   Tresplain Investments Ltd v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeals - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Articles 8(4) and 52(1)(c) - Community figurative mark Golden Elephant Brand - Application for a declaration of invalidity based on a non-registered national figurative mark GOLDEN ELEPHANT - Reference to the national law governing the earlier trade mark - Common-law action for passing-off.
