Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 13 July 2006. Roche Nederland BV and Others v Frederick Primus and Milton Goldenberg. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands. Brussels Convention - Article 6(1) - More than one defendant - Jurisdiction of the courts of the place where one of the defendants is domiciled - Action for infringement of a European patent - Defendants established in different Contracting States - Infringements committed in a number of Contracting States.
