Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 30 November 2017. X-cen-tek GmbH &amp; Co. KG v European Union Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO). Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — EU trade mark — Definition and acquisition of an EU trade mark — Figurative mark representing a triangle — Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 — Registration — Article 7(1)(b) — Absolute grounds for refusal — Mark devoid of distinctive character — Appeal in part manifestly inadmissible and in part manifestly unfounded.
