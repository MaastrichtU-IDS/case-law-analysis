Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 14 April 2016. Sparkasse Allgäu v Finanzamt Kempten. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesfinanzhof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Freedom of establishment — Article 49 TFEU — Legislation of a Member State requiring credit institutions to notify the tax authorities of deceased customers’ assets for purposes related to the collection of inheritance tax — Application of that legislation to branches established in another Member State in which banking secrecy prohibits; in principle; the disclosure of such information.
