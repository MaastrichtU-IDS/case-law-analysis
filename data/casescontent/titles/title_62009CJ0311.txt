Judgment of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 6 May 2010.   European Commission v Republic of Poland.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 89/105/EEC - Inclusion of medicinal products for human use in the national health insurance system - Article 6(1) - List of medicinal products covered by the national health insurance system establishing three different categories of reimbursement subject to conditions - Time-limit for adopting a decision on an application for inclusion of a medicinal product in the categories of that list offering the most favourable reimbursement conditions.
