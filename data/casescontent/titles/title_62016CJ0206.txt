Request for a preliminary ruling from the Consiglio di Stato. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Company law — Directive 2004/25/EC — Takeover bids — Second paragraph of Article 5(4) — Possibility of changing the price of the offer in specific circumstances and according to clearly determined circumstances and criteria — National law providing an option for the supervisory authority to increase the price of a takeover bid in the event of collusion between the offeror or the persons acting in concert with it and one or more sellers).
