Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber); 25 October 2012. Déborah Prete v Office national de l’emploi. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Cour de cassation (Belgium). Freedom of movement for persons – Article 39 EC – National of a Member State seeking employment in another Member State – Equal treatment – Tideover allowance for young persons seeking their first job – Grant subject to completion of at least six years’ studies in the host State.
