Order of the Court of 20 October 2000.   Johann Vogler v Landwirtschaftliche Alterskasse Schwaben.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Sozialgericht Augsburg - Germany.   Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Social security - Freedom of establishment - Determination of the applicable legislation - Self-employed activities in more than one Member State - Articles 13(1) and 14a(2) of Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 - Legislation of only one Member State applicable.
