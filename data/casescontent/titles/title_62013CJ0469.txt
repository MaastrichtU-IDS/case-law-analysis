Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 17 July 2014.   Shamim Tahir v Ministero dell'Interno and Questura di Verona.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale di Verona - Italy.   Request for a preliminary ruling - Area of freedom; security and justice - Directive 2003/109/EC - Articles 2; 4(1); 7(1) and 13 - ‘Long-term resident’s EU residence permit’ - Terms for conferring long-term resident status - Legal and continuous residence in the host Member State for five years prior to the submission of the permit application - Person with family connections to the long-term resident - More favourable national provisions - Effects.
