Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 15 June 2000.   Brinkmann Tabakfabriken GmbH v Hauptzollamt Bielefeld.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Düsseldorf - Germany.   Directive 92/80/EEC - National tax consisting either in a specific duty for products which are not above a certain price; or in an ad valorem duty for products which are above that price.
