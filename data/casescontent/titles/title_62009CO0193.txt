Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 4 March 2010.   Kaul GmbH v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Application for registration of Community word mark ARCOL - Opposition by proprietor of Community word mark CAPOL - Implementation by OHIM of a judgment annulling a decision of its Boards of Appeal - Right to be heard - Likelihood of confusion - Minimal degree of similarity of the marks required - Rejection for manifest irrelevance of new evidence adduced before the Board of Appeal - Articles 8(1)(b); 61(2); 63(6); 73; second sentence; and 74(2) of Regulation No 40/94.
