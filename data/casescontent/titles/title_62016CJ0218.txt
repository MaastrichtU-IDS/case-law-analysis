Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 12 October 2017. Proceedings brought by Aleksandra Kubicka. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Sąd Okręgowy w Gorzowie Wielkopolskim. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Area of Freedom; Security and Justice — Regulation (EU) No 650/2012 — Succession and the European Certificate of Succession — Scope — Immovable property located in a Member State in which legacies ‘per vindicationem’ do not exist — Refusal to recognise the material effects of such a legacy.
