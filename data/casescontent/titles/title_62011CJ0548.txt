Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 18 April 2013.   Edgard Mulders v Rijksdienst voor Pensioenen.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Arbeidshof te Antwerpen - Belgium.   Social security - Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 - Article 1(r) - Definition of ‘periods of insurance’ - Article 46 - Calculation of retirement pension - Periods of insurance to be taken into consideration - Frontier workers - Period of incapacity for work - Aggregation of similar benefits paid by two Member States - No account taken of a period of incapacity for work as a period of insurance - Residence requirement - Nation rules precluding the cumulation of benefits.
