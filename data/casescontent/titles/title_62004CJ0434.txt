Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 28 September 2006.   Criminal proceedings against Jan-Erik Anders Ahokainen and Mati Leppik.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Korkein oikeus - Finland.   Free movement of goods - Articles 28 EC and 30 EC - National legislation prohibiting; without prior authorisation; the importation of undenatured ethyl alcohol of an alcoholic strength of more than 80% - Measure having equivalent effect to a quantitative restriction - Justification on the grounds of protection of public health and public order.
