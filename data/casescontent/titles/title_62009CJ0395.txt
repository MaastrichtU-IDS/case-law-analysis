Judgment of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 30 September 2010. Oasis East sp. z o.o. v Minister Finansów. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Naczelny Sąd Administracyjny - Poland. Sixth VAT Directive - Directive 2006/112/EC - Accession of a new Member State - Right to deduct input tax - National legislation excluding the right to deduct tax on the provision of certain services - Commercial partners established in a territory classified as a ‘tax haven’ - Option for Member States to retain rules excluding the right to deduct at the time when the Sixth VAT Directive entered into force.
