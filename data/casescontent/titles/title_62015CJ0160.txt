Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 8 September 2016. GS Media BV v Sanoma Media Netherlands BV and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Copyright and related rights — Directive 2001/29/EC — Information society — Harmonisation of certain aspects of copyright and related rights — Article 3(1) — Communication to the public — Definition — Internet — Hyperlinks giving access to protected works; made accessible on another website without the rightholder’s consent — Works not yet published by the rightholder — Posting of such links for a profit.
