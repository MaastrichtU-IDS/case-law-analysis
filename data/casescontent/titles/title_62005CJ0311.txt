Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 4 October 2007.   Naipes Heraclio Fournier SA v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Community trade mark - Figurative marks comprising the representation of a ‘sword’; a ‘knight of clubs’ and a ‘king of swords’ in a pack of cards - Declaration of invalidity of the mark.
