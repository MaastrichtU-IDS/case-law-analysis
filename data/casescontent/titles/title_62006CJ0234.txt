Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 13 September 2007.   Il Ponte Finanziaria SpA v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Registration of the trade mark BAINBRIDGE - Opposition by the proprietor of earlier national trade marks all having the component ‘Bridge’ in common - Opposition rejected - Family of trade marks - Proof of use - Concept of ‘defensive trade maks’.
