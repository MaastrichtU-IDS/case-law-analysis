Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 15 November 2016. Gorka Salaberria Sorondo v Academia Vasca de Policía y Emergencias. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal Superior de Justicia del País Vasco. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Equal treatment in employment and occupation — Directive 2000/78/EC — Article 2(2) and Article 4(1) — Discrimination on grounds of age — Recruitment of police officers of the Autonomous Community of the Basque Country restricted to candidates under 35 years of age — Concept of ‘genuine and determining occupational requirement’ — Objective pursued — Proportionality.
