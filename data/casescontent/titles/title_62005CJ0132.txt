Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 26 February 2008. Commission of the European Communities v Federal Republic of Germany. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Regulation (EEC) No 2081/92 - Protection of geographical indications and designations of origin for agricultural products and foodstuffs - ‘Parmigiano Reggiano’ cheese - Use of the name ‘Parmesan’ - Obligation on a Member State to proceed on its own initiative against the abuse of a protected designation of origin.
