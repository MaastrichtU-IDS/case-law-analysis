Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 11 January 2001.   Westdeutsche Landesbank Girozentrale v Friedrich Stefan and Republik Österreich.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landesgericht für Zivilrechtssachen Wien - Austria.   National rules prohibiting the registration of mortgages in foreign currencies - Breach of that prohibition before Community law entered into force in Austria - Interpretation of Article 73b of the EC Treaty (now Article 56 EC) - Whether Community law can operate to remedy the registration.
