Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 5 March 2015. Copydan Båndkopi v Nokia Danmark A/S. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Østre Landsret. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Copyright and related rights — Directive 2001/29/EC — Articles 5(2)(b) and 6 — Reproduction right — Exception — Copying for private use — Reproductions made with the aid of mobile telephone memory cards — Fair compensation — Levy on reproduction media — Equal treatment — Reimbursement of the levy — Minimal prejudice.
