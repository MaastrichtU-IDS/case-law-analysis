Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 12 February 2004. Koninklijke KPN Nederland NV v Benelux-Merkenbureau. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Gerechtshof te 's-Gravenhage - Netherlands. Approximation of laws - Trade marks - Directive 89/104/EEC - Article 3(1) - Grounds for refusal to register - Taking account of all the relevant facts and circumstances - Prohibition on registering a mark in respect of certain goods or services if they do not possess a particular characteristic - Word made up of components each of which describes characteristics of the goods or services concerned.
