Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 21 February 2013. Banif Plus Bank Zrt v Csaba Csipai and Viktória Csipai. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Fővárosi Bíróság (now the Fővárosi Törvényszék). Directive 93/13/EEC — Unfair terms in consumer contracts — Examination by the national court; of its own motion; as to whether a term is unfair — Obligation on the national court; once it has found; of its own motion; that a term is unfair; to invite the parties to submit their observations before drawing conclusions from that finding — Contractual terms to be taken into account in the assessment of that unfairness.
