Judgment of the Court (Tenth Chamber); 18 December 2014. Generali-Providencia Biztosító Zrt v Közbeszerzési Hatóság Közbeszerzési Döntőbizottság. Request for preliminary ruling from the Fővárosi Közigazgatási és Munkaügyi Bíróság. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Public procurement — Contracts falling below the threshold laid down in Directive 2004/18/EC — Articles 49 TFEU and 56 TFEU — Applicability — Certain cross-border interest — Grounds for exclusion from a tendering procedure — Exclusion of an economic operator having committed an infringement of national competition rules; established by a judgment given not more than five years ago — Lawfulness — Proportionality.
