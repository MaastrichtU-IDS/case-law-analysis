Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 27 April 2016. X v Staatssecretaris van Financiën. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Common Customs Tariff — Regulation (EC) No 1186/2009 — Article 3 — Relief from import duties — Personal property — Transfer of residence from a third country to a Member State — Definition of ‘normal place of residence’ — Impossible to have at the same time a normal place of residence in a Member State and in a third country — Criteria for determining the normal place of residence.
