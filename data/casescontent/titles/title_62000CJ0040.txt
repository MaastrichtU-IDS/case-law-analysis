Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 14 June 2001.   Commission of the European Communities v French Republic.   Failure by a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Article 17(2) and (6) of the Sixth VAT Directive - Reintroduction; after the date of entry into force of the Directive; of a total abolition of the right to deduct VAT charged on diesel used as fuel for vehicles and machines on the purchase of which no VAT is deductible.
