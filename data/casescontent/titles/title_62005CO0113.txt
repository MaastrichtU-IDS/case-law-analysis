Order of the Court (Third Chamber) of 30 March 2006.   European Federation for Cosmetic Ingredients (EFfCI) v Council of the European Union and European Parliament.   Appeal - Directive 2003/15/EC - Action for annulment - Cosmetic products - Protection of public health - Experiments on animals - Prohibition of substances which are carcinogenic; mutagenic or toxic for reproduction - Appeal in part clearly inadmissible and in part clearly unfounded.
