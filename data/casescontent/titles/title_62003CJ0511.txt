Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 20 October 2005.   Staat der Nederlanden (Ministerie van Landbouw; Natuurbeheer en Visserij) v Ten Kate Holding Musselkanaal BV and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands.   Human and animal health - Protection against bovine spongiform encephalopathy (mad cow disease) - Feeding of ruminants with proteins derived from species other than ruminants - Liability of a Member State for damage caused to individuals by breaches of Community law for which it may be held responsible - Applicable law - Obligation to bring an action against the Commission for failure to act.
