Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 6 September 2012. Laimonis Treimanis v Valsts ieņēmumu dienests. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Administratīvā rajona tiesa. Regulation (EEC) No 918/83 — Articles 1(2)(c); 2 and 7(1) — Relief from import duties on personal property — The term ‘property intended … for meeting … household needs’ — Motor vehicle imported into the European Union — Vehicle used by a member of the family of the importing owner.
