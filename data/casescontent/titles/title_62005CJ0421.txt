Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 18 January 2007.   City Motors Groep NV v Citroën Belux NV.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Rechtbank van koophandel te Brussel - Belgium.   Competition - Distribution agreement relating to motor vehicles - Block exemption - Regulation (EC) No 1400/2002 - Article 3(4) and (6) - Termination by the supplier - Right to refer the dispute to an expert or arbitrator or to apply to a national court - Express termination clause - Compatibility with the block exemption - Validity of the grounds for the termination - Effective review.
