Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 10 September 2009. Zentrale zur Bekämpfung unlauteren Wettbewerbs eV v Adolf Darbo AG. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Oberlandesgericht München - Germany. Harmonisation of laws - Directive 95/2/EC - Part A of Annex III - Directive 2001/113/EC - Second paragraph of Part II of Annex I - Extra jam with a soluble dry matter content of 58% and containing potassium sorbate (E 202) as a preservative - Term ‘low-sugar jam’.
