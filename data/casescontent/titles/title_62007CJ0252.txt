Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 27 November 2008. Intel Corporation Inc. v CPM United Kingdom Ltd. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Court of Appeal (England &amp; Wales) (Civil Division) - United Kingdom. Directive 89/104/EEC - Trade marks - Article 4(4)(a) - Trade marks with a reputation - Protection against the use of a later identical or similar mark - Use which takes or would take unfair advantage of; or is or would be detrimental to; the distinctive character or the repute of the earlier trade mark.
