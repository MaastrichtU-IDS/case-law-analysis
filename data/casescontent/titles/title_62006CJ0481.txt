Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 18 December 2007.   Commission of the European Communities v Hellenic Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Public procurement - Infringement of Article 6(3) of Directive 93/36/EC - General principles of the Treaty - Principle of equal treatment and obligation of transparency - National rules allowing use of the negotiated procedure for public supply contracts relating to certain medical equipment.
