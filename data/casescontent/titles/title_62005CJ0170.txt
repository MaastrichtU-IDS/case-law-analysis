Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 14 December 2006. Denkavit Internationaal BV and Denkavit France SARL v Ministre de l'Économie; des Finances et de l'Industrie. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Conseil d'État - France. Freedom of establishment - Corporation tax - Payment of dividends - Exemption for dividends paid to resident companies - Withholding tax levied on dividends paid to non-resident companies - Double taxation convention - Possibility of setting off the amount withheld against tax due in another Member State.
