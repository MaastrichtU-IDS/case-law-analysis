Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 18 November 2010.   NDSHT Nya Destination Stockholm Hotell &amp; Teaterpaket AB v European Commission.   Appeal - State aid - Complaint by a competitor - Admissibility - Regulation (EC) No 659/1999 - Articles 4; 10; 13 and 20 - Commission decision not to examine the complaint further - Classification by the Commission of measures as partially not constituting State aid and partially as existing aid compatible with the common market - Article 230 EC - Concept of ‘an act open to challenge’.
