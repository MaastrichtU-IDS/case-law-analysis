Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 11 March 2004.   Saatgut-Treuhandverwaltungsgesellschaft mbH v Werner Jäger.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Oberlandesgericht Düsseldorf - Germany.   Plant varieties - System of protection - Article 14(3) of Regulation (EC) No 2100/94 and Articles 3(2) and 8 of Regulation (EC) No 1768/95 - Organisation of holders - Meaning - Organisation's obligation to act only for its members - Use by farmers of the product of the harvest - Obligation to provide information to the holder of the Community right.
