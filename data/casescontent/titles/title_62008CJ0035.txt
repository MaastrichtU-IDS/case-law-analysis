Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 15 October 2009. Grundstücksgemeinschaft Busley and Cibrian Fernandez v Finanzamt Stuttgart-Körperschaften. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Baden-Württemberg - Germany. Free movement of capital - Immovable property - Income tax - Deductibility of rental losses from the taxable income of a person liable to tax - Application of the decreasing-balance method of depreciation to the costs of acquisition or construction - More favourable tax treatment confined to immovable property situated on the national territory.
