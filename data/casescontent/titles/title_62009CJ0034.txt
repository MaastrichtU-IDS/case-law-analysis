Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 8 March 2011. Gerardo Ruiz Zambrano v Office national de l’emploi (ONEm). Reference for a preliminary ruling: tribunal du travail de Bruxelles - Belgium. Citizenship of the Union - Article 20 TFEU - Grant of right of residence under European Union law to a minor child on the territory of the Member State of which that child is a national; irrespective of the previous exercise by him of his right of free movement in the territory of the Member States - Grant; in the same circumstances; of a derived right of residence; to an ascendant relative; a third country national; upon whom the minor child is dependent - Consequences of the right of residence of the minor child on the employment law requirements to be fulfilled by the third-country national ascendant relative of that minor.
