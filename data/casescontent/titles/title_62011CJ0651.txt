Judgment of the Court (Ninth Chamber); 30 May 2013. Staatssecretaris van Financiën v X BV. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden. VAT — Sixth Directive 77/388/EEC — Article 5(8) — Concept of ‘transfer of a totality of assets or part thereof’ — Disposal of 30% of the shares in a company to which the transferor supplies services that are subject to VAT.
