Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber); 16 January 2014. Andreas Kainz v Pantherwerke AG. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberster Gerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Jurisdiction in civil and commercial matters — Regulation (EC) No 44/2001 — Liability for a defective product — Product manufactured in one Member State and sold in another Member State — Interpretation of the concept of ‘the place where the harmful event occurred or may occur’ — Place of the event giving rise to the damage.
