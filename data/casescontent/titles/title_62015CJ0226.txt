Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 21 July 2016. Apple and Pear Australia Ltd and Star Fruits Diffusion v European Union Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO). Appeal — EU trade mark — Application for EU word mark English pink — Opposition by the proprietor of the word mark PINK LADY and the figurative marks including the word elements ‘Pink Lady’ — Opposition dismissed — Decision of an EU trade mark court — Alteration — Res judicata.
