Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 11 July 2002.   Deutsche Paracelsus Schulen für Naturheilverfahren GmbH v Kurt Gräbner.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Oberster Gerichtshof - Austria.   Freedom of establishment - Freedom to provide services - Scope of Directive 92/51/EEC - National legislation reserving medical practice; including the activities which Heilpraktikers are permitted to pursue in Germany; to qualified doctors - National legislation restricting medical training to certain institutions and prohibiting advertising for such training.
