Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 17 November 2011. Hypoteční banka a.s. v Udo Mike Lindner. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Okresní soud v Chebu - Czech Republic. Jurisdiction and the enforcement of judgments in civil and commercial matters - Mortgage loan contract concluded by a consumer who is a national of one Member State with a bank established in another Member State - Legislation of a Member State making it possible; in the case where the exact domicile of the consumer is unknown; to bring an action against the latter before a court of that State.
