Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 21 June 2007.   Firma Laub GmbH &amp; Co. Vieh &amp; Fleisch Import-Export v Hauptzollamt Hamburg-Jonas.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Hamburg - Germany.   Export refunds - Regulation (EEC) No 3665/87 - Definition of ‘undue payment of a refund’ - Payment of a refund on the basis of incomplete documentation - Possibility of completing the documents relating to the payment of the refund after the expiry of the periods referred to in Articles 47(2) and 48(2)(a) of Regulation (EEC) No 3665/87 in the context of a subsequent recovery procedure.
