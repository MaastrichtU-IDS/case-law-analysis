Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 18 March 2004.   Siemens AG Österreich and ARGE Telekom &amp; Partner v Hauptverband der österreichischen Sozialversicherungsträger.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesvergabeamt - Austria.   Public contracts - Directive 89/665/EEC - Review procedures concerning the award of public contracts - Effects of a decision by the body responsible for review procedures annulling the decision by the contracting authority not to revoke the procedure by which a contract was awarded - Restriction on the use of subcontracting.
