Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 12 November 2009.   Christian Grimme v Deutsche Angestellten-Krankenkasse.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundessozialgericht - Germany.   Freedom of movement for persons - Member of the managing board of a company limited by shares governed by Swiss law; director of a branch in Germany - Compulsory membership of the German pension insurance scheme - Exemption from that obligation for members of managing boards of companies limited by shares governed by German law.
