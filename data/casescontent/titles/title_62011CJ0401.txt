Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 11 April 2013. Blanka Soukupová v Ministerstvo zemědělství. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Nejvyšší správní soud. Agriculture — EAGGF — Regulation (EC) No 1257/1999 — Support for rural development — Early retirement support — Transferor not less than 55 years old but not yet of normal retirement age at the time of transfer — Concept of ‘normal retirement age’ — National legislation determining a retirement age which varies depending on the sex of the applicant and; for women; on the number of children raised — General principles of equal treatment and non-discrimination.
