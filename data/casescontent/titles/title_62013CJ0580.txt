Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 16 July 2015. Coty Germany GmbH v Stadtsparkasse Magdeburg. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesgerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Intellectual and industrial property — Directive 2004/48/EC — Article 8(3)(e) — Sale of counterfeit goods — Right to information in the context of proceedings for infringement of an intellectual property right — Legislation of a Member State which allows banking institutions to refuse a request for information relating to a bank account (banking secrecy).
