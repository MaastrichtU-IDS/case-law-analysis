Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 4 March 2002.   Marie-Josée Verwayen-Boelen v Rijksdienst voor Arbeidsvoorziening.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Arbeidshof Antwerpen - Belgium.   Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Answer to the question admitting of no reasonable doubt - Article 67(3) of Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 - Aggregation of periods of insurance or employment for the purpose of acquisition of a right to unemployment benefits - Requirement that periods of insurance or employment be completed lastly in accordance with the legislation under which the benefits are claimed.
