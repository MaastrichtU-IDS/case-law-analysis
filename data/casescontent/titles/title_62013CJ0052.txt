Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 13 March 2014.   Posteshop SpA - Divisione Franchising Kipoint v Autorità Garante della Concorrenza e del Mercato and Presidenza del Consiglio dei Ministri.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Consiglio di Stato - Italy.   Request for a preliminary ruling - Directive 2006/114/EC - Concepts of ‘misleading advertising’ and ‘comparative advertising’ - National legislation providing that misleading advertising and unlawful comparative advertising are two separate unlawful acts.
