Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 16 April 2015. Nemzeti Fogyasztóvédelmi Hatóság v UPC Magyarország kft. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Kúria. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2005/29/EC — Unfair commercial practices — Erroneous information provided by a telecommunications undertaking to one of its subscribers which has resulted in additional costs for the latter — Classification as a ‘misleading commercial practice’.
