Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 26 November 2015. Administrador de Infraestructuras Ferroviarias (ADIF) v Luis Aira Pascual and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal Superior de Justicia de la Comunidad Autónoma del País Vasco. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2001/23/EC — Article 1(1) — Transfers of undertakings — Safeguarding of employees’ rights — Obligation on the transferee to take on workers — Public undertaking responsible for a public service — Provision of the service by another undertaking pursuant to a public service operating agreement — Decision not to extend that agreement following its expiry — Retention of identity of the economic entity — Activity based essentially on equipment — Employees not taken on.
