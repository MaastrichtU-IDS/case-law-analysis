Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 17 January 2013. European Commission v Kingdom of Spain. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations — Value added tax — Directive 2006/112/EC — Application of a reduced rate — Articles 96 and 98(2) — Points 3 and 4 of Annex III — ‘Pharmaceutical products normally used for health care; prevention of illnesses and as treatment for medical and veterinary purposes’ — ‘Medical equipment; aids and other appliances normally intended to alleviate or treat disability; for the exclusive personal use of the disabled’.
