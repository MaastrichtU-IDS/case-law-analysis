Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 23 March 2006.   Enirisorse SpA v Sotacarbo SpA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale di Cagliari - Italy.   State aid - Articles 87 EC and 88 EC - Definition of aid - Shareholding of a public undertaking in the capital of a private undertaking - Right of withdrawal subject to a prior relinquishment of all claims over a company's assets.
