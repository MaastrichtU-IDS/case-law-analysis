Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 16 July 2015. Proceedings brought by BVVG Bodenverwertungs- und -verwaltungs GmbH. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesgerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — State aid — Article 107(1) TFEU — Sale of agricultural land by public authorities — National provision allowing the competent authorities to object to the sale of agricultural land where the price offered is considered ‘grossly disproportionate’ to the market value — Advantage granted to certain undertakings or for the production of certain goods — Private investor test — Determination of the ‘value of the contract’.
