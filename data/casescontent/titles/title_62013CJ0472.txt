Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 26 February 2015. Andre Lawrence Shepherd v Bundesrepublik Deutschland. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bayerischer Verwaltungsgerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Area of freedom; security and justice — Asylum — Directive 2004/83/EC — Article 9(2)(b); (c); and (e) — Minimum standards for the qualification and status of third-country nationals or stateless persons as refugees — Conditions for obtaining refugee status — Acts of persecution — Criminal penalties for a member of the armed forces of the United States for refusing to serve in Iraq.
