Judgment of the Court (Seventh Chamber); 12 December 2013. Dirextra Alta Formazione srl v Regione Puglia. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale amministrativo regionale per la Puglia. Request for a preliminary ruling — Freedom to provide services — Grants of public money; co-financed by the European Social Fund; for students enrolled in post-graduate specialist programmes of study — Regional legislation designed to enhance the level of education locally and making the award of grants subject to conditions targeting providers of post-graduate programmes of study — Condition requiring 10 years’ continuous experience.
