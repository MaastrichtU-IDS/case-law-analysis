Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 13 July 2004. Bacardi France SAS; formerly Bacardi-Martini SAS v Télévision française 1 SA (TF1); Groupe Jean-Claude Darmon SA and Girosport SARL. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour de cassation - France. Article 59 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Article 49 EC) - Directive 89/552/CEE - Television without frontiers - Television broadcasting - Advertising - National measure prohibiting television advertising for alcoholic drinks marketed in that Member State; in the case of indirect television advertising arising from the appearance on screen of hoardings visible during the transmission of sporting events - "Loi Evin'.
