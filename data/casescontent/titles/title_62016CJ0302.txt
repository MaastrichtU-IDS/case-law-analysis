Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 11 May 2017. Bas Jacob Adriaan Krijgsman v Surinaamse Luchtvaart Maatschappij NV. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Rechtbank Noord-Nederland. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Air transport — Regulation (EC) No 261/2004 — Article 5(1)(c) — Compensation and assistance to passengers in the event of cancellation of a flight — Exemption from the obligation to pay compensation — Contract for carriage concluded through an online travel agent — Air carrier having informed the travel agent in good time of a change to the scheduled time for the flight — Travel agent having communicated that information to a passenger by email 10 days before the flight.
