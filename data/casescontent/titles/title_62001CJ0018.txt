Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 22 May 2003.   Arkkitehtuuritoimisto Riitta Korhonen Oy; Arkkitehtitoimisto Pentti Toivanen Oy and Rakennuttajatoimisto Vilho Tervomaa v Varkauden Taitotalo Oy.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Kilpailuneuvosto - Finland.   Directive 92/50/EEC - Public service contracts - Definition of contracting authority - Body governed by public law - Company set up by a regional or local authority to promote the development of industrial or commercial activities on the territory of that authority.
