Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 22 November 2017. Edward Cussens and Others v T. G. Brosman. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Supreme Court (Ireland). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Common system of value added tax (VAT) — Sixth Directive 77/388/EEC — Article 4(3)(a) and Article 13B(g) — Exemption of the supply of buildings; and of the land on which they stand; other than as described in Article 4(3)(a) — Principle that abusive practices are prohibited — Applicability in the absence of national provisions transposing that principle — Principles of legal certainty and of the protection of legitimate expectations.
