Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 23  September  2004.   Commission of the European Communities v French Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 91/271/EEC - Urban waste water treatment - Article 5(1) and (2) and Annex II - Failure to identify sensitive areas - Meaning of "eutrophication" - Failure to implement more stringent treatment of discharges into sensitive areas.
