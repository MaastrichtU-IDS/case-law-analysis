Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 27 November 2008.   Mirja Juuri v Fazer Amica Oy.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Korkein oikeus - Finland.   Social policy - Directive 2001/23/EC - Safeguarding of employees’ rights - Transfer of undertakings - Article 4(2) - Substantial change in working conditions involved by a transfer - Collective agreement - Termination of the contract of employment by the employee - Termination for which the employer is regarded as responsible - Consequences - Financial compensation for which the employer is liable.
