Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 22 December 2010.   Yellow Cab Verkehrsbetriebs GmbH v Landeshauptmann von Wien.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Unabhängiger Verwaltungssenat Wien - Austria.   Freedom to provide services - Freedom of establishment - Competition rules - Cabotage transport operations - National transportation of persons by bus service - Application to operate a service - Licence - Authorisation - Conditions - Requirement of a seat or permanent establishment in the national territory - Reduction of income compromising the profitability of a service already licensed.
