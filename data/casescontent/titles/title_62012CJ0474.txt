Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber); 4 September 2014. Schiebel Aircraft GmbH v Bundesminister für Wirtschaft; Familie und Jugend. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Verwaltungsgerichtshof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Freedom of establishment — Freedom of movement for workers — Non-discrimination — Article 346(1)(b) TFEU — Protection of a Member State’s essential security interests — Legislation of a Member State under which the statutory representatives of a business engaged within the national territory in the trading of arms; munitions and war material must hold the nationality of that Member State.
