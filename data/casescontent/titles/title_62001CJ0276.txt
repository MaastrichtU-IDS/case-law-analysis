Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 10 April 2003.   Joachim Steffensen.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Amtsgericht Schleswig - Germany.   Directive 89/397/EEC - Official control of foodstuffs - Second subparagraph of Article 7(1) - Analysis of samples - Right to a second opinion - Direct effect - Admissibility of the results of analyses as evidence in the event of an infringement of the right to a second opinion.
