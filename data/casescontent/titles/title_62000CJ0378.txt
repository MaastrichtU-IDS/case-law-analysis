Judgment of the Court of 21 January 2003.   Commission of the European Communities v European Parliament and Council of the European Union.   Comitology - Council Decision 1999/468/EC laying down the procedures for the exercise of implementing powers conferred on the Commission - Criteria for choosing between the different procedures for adopting implementing measures - Effects - Obligation to state reasons - Annulment in part of Regulation (EC) No 1655/2000 of the European Parliament and of the Council concerning the Financial Instrument for the Environment (LIFE).
