Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 29 November 2017. VCAST Limited v RTI SpA. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale di Torino. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Approximation of laws — Copyright and related rights — Directive 2001/29/EC — Article 5(2)(b) — Private copying exception — Article 3(1) — Communication to the public — Specific technical means — Provision of a cloud computing service for the remote video recording of copies of works protected by copyright; without the consent of the author concerned — Active involvement of the service provider in the recording.
