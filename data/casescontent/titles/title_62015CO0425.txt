Order of the Court (First Chamber) of 29 October 2015. Udo Voigt v President of the European Parliament and European Parliament. Action for annulment — Article 53(2) of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Article 54; second paragraph; of the Statute of the Court of Justice — Refusal to make available to the applicant; a member of the European Parliament; Parliament premises for the purposes of hosting a delegation comprising two Russian citizens; inter alia; for a press conference — Prohibition on the Russian citizens on the list of participants of the meeting from entering European Parliament premises — Manifest lack of jurisdiction — Referral back to the General Court of the European Union.
