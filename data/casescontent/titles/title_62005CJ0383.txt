Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 22 March 2007. Raffaele Talotta v Belgian State. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Cour de cassation - Belgium. Freedom of establishment - Article 52 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Article 43 EC) - Non-resident taxpayer carrying out a self-employed activity - Setting of minimum tax bases applicable only to non-resident taxpayers - Justified by requirements of general interest - Effectiveness of fiscal supervision - Not justified.
