Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 13 December 2007. United Pan-Europe Communications Belgium SA and Others v Belgian State. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Conseil d’État - Belgium. Article 49 EC - Freedom to provide services - National legislation requiring cable operators to broadcast programmes transmitted by certain private broadcasters (‘must carry’) - Restriction - Overriding reason relating to the general interest - Maintenance of pluralism in a bilingual region.
