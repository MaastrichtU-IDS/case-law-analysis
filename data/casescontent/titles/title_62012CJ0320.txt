Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 27 June 2013.   Malaysia Dairy Industries Pte. Ltd v Ankenævnet for Patenter og Varemærker.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Højesteret - Denmark.   Approximation of laws - Directive 2008/95/EC - Article 4(4)(g) - Trade marks - Conditions for obtaining and continuing to hold a trade mark - Refusal of registration or invalidation - Concept of ‘bad faith’ of the applicant - Whether the applicant knows of the existence of a foreign mark.
