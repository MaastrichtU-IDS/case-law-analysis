Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 23 September 2008.   Birgit Bartsch v Bosch und Siemens Hausgeräte (BSH) Altersfürsorge GmbH.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesarbeitsgericht - Germany.   Equal treatment in employment and occupation - Article 13 EC - Directive 2000/78/EC - Occupational pension scheme excluding the right to a pension of a spouse more than 15 years younger than the deceased former employee - Age discrimination - Link with Community law.
