Judgment of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 18 January 2018. Frédéric Jahin v Ministre de l'Économie et des Finances and Ministre des Affaires sociales et de la Santé. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Conseil d'État (France). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Free movement of capital — Articles 63 TFEU and 65 TFEU — Regulation (EC) No 883/2004 — Article 11 — Levies on income from assets contributing to the financing of the social security scheme of a Member State — Exemption for nationals of the European Union affiliated to a social security scheme of another Member State — Natural persons affiliated to a social security scheme of a third country — Difference of treatment — Restriction — Justification.
