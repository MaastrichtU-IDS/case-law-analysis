Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 18 June 2002.   Kingdom of Spain v Commission of the European Communities.   State aid - Plans notified - No decision by the Commission within the two-month time-limit - Time-limit of 15 working days for initiating the formal investigation procedure - Calculation of the time-limit - Conditions governing prior notice by the Member State and notification of the Commission's decision - Service by fax.
