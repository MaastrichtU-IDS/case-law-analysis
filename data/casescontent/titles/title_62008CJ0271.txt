Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 15 July 2010. European Commission v Federal Republic of Germany. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directives 92/50/EEC and 2004/18/EC - Public service contracts - Occupational old-age pensions of local authority employees - Direct award of contracts; without a call for tenders at European Union level; to pension providers designated in a collective agreement concluded between management and labour.
