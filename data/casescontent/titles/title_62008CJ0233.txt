Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 14 January 2010. Milan Kyrian v Celní úřad Tábor. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Nejvyšší správní soud - Czech Republic. Mutual assistance for the recovery of claims - Directive 76/308/EEC - Jurisdiction to review of the courts of the Member State in which the requested authority is situated - Enforceability of an instrument permitting enforcement - Lawfulness of notification of the instrument to the debtor - Notification in a language not understood by the addressee.
