Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 3 April 2014. Udo Rätzke v S+K Handels GmbH. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Thüringer Oberlandesgericht. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Energy — Energy labelling of televisions — Delegated Regulation (EU) No 1062/2010 — Responsibilities of dealers — Television supplied to the dealer without the label before the regulation became applicable — Dealer’s obligation to label that television from the date on which the regulation became applicable and to obtain a label subsequently.
