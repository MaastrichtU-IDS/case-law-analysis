Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 10 November 2016. Maria Isabel Harmon v Owen Pardue. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Dublin Metropolitan District Court. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Preamble and Articles 6; 20; 41; 47 and 48 — Powers of the national criminal prosecution authorities — Failure to implement EU law — Article 53(2) of the Rules of Procedure of the Court — Clear lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
