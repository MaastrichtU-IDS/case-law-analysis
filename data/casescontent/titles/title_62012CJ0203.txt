Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 17 October 2013.   Billerud Karlsborg AB and Billerud Skärblacka AB v Naturvårdsverket.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Högsta domstolen - Sweden.   Directive 2003/87/EC - Scheme for greenhouse gas emission allowance trading - Penalty for excess emissions - Concept of excess emission - Equated with infringement of the obligation to surrender; within the time periods prescribed by the directive; a sufficient number of allowances to cover the emissions from the previous year - No exculpatory cause in the event of actual holding of non-surrendered allowances; unless force majeure - No possibility of varying the amount of the penalty - Proportionality.
