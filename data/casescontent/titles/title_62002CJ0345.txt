Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 15 July 2004. Pearle BV; Hans Prijs Optiek Franchise BV and Rinck Opticiëns BV v Hoofdbedrijfschap Ambachten. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands. State aid - Definition of aid - Collective advertising campaigns in favour of one sector of the economy - Financing by means of a special contribution payable by undertakings in that sector - Action taken by a body governed by public law.
