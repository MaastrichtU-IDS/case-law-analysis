Judgment of the Court of 11 July 2002. Marie-Nathalie D'Hoop v Office national de l'emploi. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal du travail de Liège - Belgium. Citizenship of the Union - Principle of non-discrimination - National legislation granting the right to tideover allowances to its nationals only on condition that they have completed their secondary education in an educational establishment in their own Member State - National seeking first employment having completed her secondary education in an educational establishment in another Member State.
