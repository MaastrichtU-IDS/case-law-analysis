Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 13 December 2012. Transcatab SpA v European Commission. Appeal — Competition — Agreements; decisions and concerted practices — Italian market for the purchasing and primary processing of raw tobacco — Price fixing and market sharing — Attribution of liability for the unlawful conduct of a subsidiary to its parent company — Presumption of innocence — Rights of the defence — Obligation to state the reasons on which a decision is based — Principle of equal treatment.
