Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 24 September 2009. Erste Group Bank AG (C-125/07 P); Raiffeisen Zentralbank Österreich AG (C-133/07 P); Bank Austria Creditanstalt AG (C-135/07 P) and Österreichische Volksbanken AG (C-137/07 P) v Commission of the European Communities. Appeal - Agreements; decisions and concerted practices - Fixing by Austrian banks of credit and debit interest rates - 'Lombard Club' - Effect on trade between Member States - Calculation of fines - Takeover of undertakings - Concrete impact on the market - Implementation of the cartel.
