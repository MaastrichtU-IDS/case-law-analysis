Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber); 26 September 2013. Industrie du bois de Vielsalm &amp; Cie (IBV) SA v Région wallonne. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Cour constitutionnelle (Belgium). Directive 2004/8/EC — Scope — Cogeneration and high efficiency cogeneration — Article 7 — Regional support scheme providing for the grant of ‘green certificates’ to cogeneration plants — Grant of a larger number of green certificates to cogeneration plants processing principally forms of biomass other than wood or wood waste — Principle of equality and non-discrimination — Articles 20 and 21 of the Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union.
