Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 29 November 2012.   Václav Hrbek v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeals - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 207/2009 - Article 8(1)(b) - Relative ground for refusal - Likelihood of confusion - Figurative mark - Opposition by the proprietor of an earlier trade mark - Appeal clearly inadmissible and clearly unfounded.
