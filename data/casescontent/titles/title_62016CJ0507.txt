Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 15 November 2017. Entertainment Bulgaria System EOOD v Direktor na Direktsia „Obzhalvane i danachno-osiguritelna praktika“ - Sofia. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Administrativen sad Sofia-grad. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Taxation — Directive 2006/112/EC — Article 168(a); Article 169(a); Article 214(1)(d) and (e); and Articles 289 and 290 — Deductibility of input value added tax (VAT) due or paid — Output transactions carried out in other Member States — Tax exemption scheme in the Member State in which the right to deduct is exercised.
