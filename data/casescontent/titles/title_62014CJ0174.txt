Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 29 October 2015. Saudaçor – Sociedade Gestora de Recursos e Equipamentos da Saúde dos Açores SA v Fazenda Pública. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Supremo Tribunal Administrativo. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Value added tax — Directive 2006/112/EC — Article 13(1) — Treatment as a non-taxable person — Concept of ‘body governed by public law’ — Limited company which is responsible for the provision of services in respect of the planning and management of the health service of the Autonomous Region of the Azores — Determination of the detailed arrangements for those services; including their remuneration; in programme agreements concluded between that company and that region.
