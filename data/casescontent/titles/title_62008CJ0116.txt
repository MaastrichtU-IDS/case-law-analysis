Directive 96/34/EC - Framework agreement on parental leave concluded by UNICE; CEEP and the ETUC - Interpretation of Clause 2.6 and 2.7 - Part-time parental leave - Dismissal of a worker before the end of parental leave without observing the statutory period of notice - Calculation of compensation.
