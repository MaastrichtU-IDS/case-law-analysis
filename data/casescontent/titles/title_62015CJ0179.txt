Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 3 March 2016. Daimler AG v Együd Garage Gépjárműjavító és Értékesítő Kft. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Fővárosi Törvényszék. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Trade marks — Directive 2008/95/EC — Article 5(1) — Advertisements relating to a third party accessible on the internet — Unauthorised use of the mark — Advertisements published online without the knowledge and without the consent of that third party or maintained online despite the opposition of that third party — Action of the trade mark proprietor against that third party.
