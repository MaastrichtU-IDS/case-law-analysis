Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 15 March 2012. Società Consortile Fonografici (SCF) v Marco Del Corso. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Corte d’appello di Torino. Copyright and related rights in the information society — Direct applicability of the Rome Convention; the TRIPS Agreement and the WPPT in the European Union legal order — Directive 92/100/EC — Article 8(2) — Directive 2001/29/EC — Concept of ‘communication to the public’ — Communication to the public of phonograms broadcast by radio in a dental practice.
