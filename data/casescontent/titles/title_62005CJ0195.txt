Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 18 December 2007.   Commission of the European Communities v Italian Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Environment - Directives 75/442/EEC and 91/156/EEC - Concept of waste - Food scraps from the agro-food industry intended for the production of animal feed - Leftovers from the preparation of food in kitchens; intended for shelters for pet animals.
