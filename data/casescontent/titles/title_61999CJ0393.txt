Judgment of the Court of 19 March 2002.   Institut national d'assurances sociales pour travailleurs indépendants (Inasti) v Claude Hervein and Hervillier SA (C-393/99) and Guy Lorthiois and Comtexbel SA (C-394/99).   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal du travail de Tournai - Belgium.   Freedom of movement for workers and freedom of establishment - Social security - Determination of the legislation applicable - Persons who are simultaneously employed and self-employed in the territory of different Member States - Cover by the social security legislation of each of those States - Validity of Article 14c(1)(b); now Article 14c(b); of and Annex VII to Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71.
