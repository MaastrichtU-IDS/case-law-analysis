Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 28 January 2016. BP Europa SE v Hauptzollamt Hamburg-Stadt. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesfinanzhof. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Taxation — General arrangements for excise duty — Directive 2008/118/EC — Occurrence of an irregularity during a movement of excise goods — Movement of goods under a duty suspension arrangement — Goods missing on delivery — Levying of excise duty in the absence of proof of destruction or loss of the goods.
