Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 2 October 2001.   European Investment Bank v Michel Hautem.   Appeal - Members of the staff of the European Investment Bank - Dismissal - Interpretation of the Staff Regulations of the European Investment Bank - Plea alleging mistaken characterisation of the legal nature of the facts and an error in the statement of reasons - Alleged infringement of the rules applicable to relations between the European Investment Bank and its staff.
