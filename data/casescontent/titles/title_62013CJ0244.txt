Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 10 July 2014. Ewaen Fred Ogieriakhi v Minister for Justice and Equality and Others. Request for a preliminary ruling from the High Court (Ireland). Reference for a preliminary ruling — Directive 2004/38/EC — Article 16(2) — Right of permanent residence for family members of a Union citizen who are third-country nationals — Situation where spouses no longer live together — Immediate installation with other partners during a continuous period of residence of five years — Regulation (EEC) No 1612/68 — Article 10(3) — Conditions — Infringement of EU law by a Member State — Examination of the nature of the infringement at issue — Need for a reference for a preliminary ruling.
