Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 8 June 2006.   Commission of the European Communities v French Republic.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 2001/19/EC - General system for the recognition of professional qualifications - Mutual recognition of diplomas; certificates and other qualifications - Nurse; dentist; veterinary surgeon; midwife; architect; pharmacist and doctor - Failure to transpose within the prescribed period.
