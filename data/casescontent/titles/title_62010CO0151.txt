Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 7 April 2011. Dai Cugini NV v Rijksdienst voor Sociale Zekerheid. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Arbeidshof te Antwerpen - Belgium. First subparagraph of Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Directive 97/811/EC - Equal treatment of part-time and full-time workers - Discrimination - Administrative obstacle likely to limit the opportunities for part-time work - Mandatory publication and retention of contracts and work-schedules.
