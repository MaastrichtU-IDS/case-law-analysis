Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 23 November 2017. Proceedings brought by A Oy. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Helsingin hallinto-oikeus. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Freedom of establishment — Direct taxation — Corporation tax — Directive 90/434/EEC — Article 10(2) — Transfer of assets — Non-resident permanent establishment transferred; in the course of a transfer of assets; to a receiving company also non-resident — Right of the Member State of the transferring company to tax that establishment’s profits or capital gains resulting from the transfer of assets — National legislation providing for immediate taxation of the profits or capital gains in the year of transfer — Collection of the tax due as revenue of the tax year in which the transfer of assets took place.
