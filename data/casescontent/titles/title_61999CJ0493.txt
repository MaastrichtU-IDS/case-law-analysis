Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 25 October 2001.   Commission of the European Communities v Federal Republic of Germany.   Failure by a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Articles 52 and 59 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Articles 43 EC and 49 EC) - National legislation on the contracting out of labour in the construction industry - Exclusion of undertakings not party to a collective agreement for that industry and not having an establishment in the Member State in which services are to be provided - Proportionality.
