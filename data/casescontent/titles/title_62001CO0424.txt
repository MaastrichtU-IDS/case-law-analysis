Order of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 9 April 2003.   CS Communications &amp; Systems Austria GmbH v Allgemeine Unfallversicherungsanstalt.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesvergabeamt - Austria.   Public contracts - Directive 89/665/EEC - Review procedures for the award of public contracts - Action for annulment of a decision of the contracting authority - Application for interim measures - Duty or discretion of the body responsible for review procedures to take account of the prospects of success of the substantive claim - Article 104(3) of the Rules of Procedure - Question the answer to which does not admit of any reasonable doubt.
