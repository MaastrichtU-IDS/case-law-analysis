Judgment of the Court of 7 January 2003.   Banque internationale pour l'Afrique occidentale SA (BIAO) v Finanzamt für Großunternehmen in Hamburg.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Hamburg - Germany.   Fourth Directive 78/660/EEC - Annual accounts of certain types of companies - Jurisdiction of the Court to interpret Community law in a context where it is not directly applicable - Provisions for risk under a loan guarantee - Taking into account of the individual situation of the debtor and of its State of establishment - Date on which the risk may or must be evaluated and entered on the balance sheet.
