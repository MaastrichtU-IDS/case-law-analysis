Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 14 September 2000.   Renato Collino and Luisella Chiappero v Telecom Italia SpA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Pretore di Pinerolo - Italy.   Directive 77/187/EEC - Safeguarding employees' rights in the event of transfers of undertakings - Transfer of an entity managed by a public body forming part of the State administration to a private company whose capital is publicly owned - Definition of an employee - Taking into account of remployees' total length of service by the transferee.
