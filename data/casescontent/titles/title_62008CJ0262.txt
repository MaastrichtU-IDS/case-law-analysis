Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 10 June 2010. CopyGene A/S v Skatteministeriet. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Østre Landsret - Denmark. Sixth VAT Directive - Exemptions - Article 13A(1)(b) - Hospital and medical care - Closely related activities - Duly recognised establishments of a nature similar to hospitals or centres for medical treatment or diagnosis - Private stem cell bank - Services of collection; transportation; analysis and storage of umbilical cord blood of newborn children - Possible autologous or allogeneic use of stem cells.
