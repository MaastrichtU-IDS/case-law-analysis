Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 5 October 2006.   Transalpine Ölleitung in Österreich GmbH and Others v Finanzlandesdirektion für Tirol and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Austria.   State aid - Last sentence of Article 88(3) EC - Partial rebate on energy taxes - Failure to give notice of the aid - Commission decision - Declaration of the compatibility of the aid with the common market in respect of a particular period in the past - Effect on rebate applications made by undertakings not benefiting from the aid - Powers of national courts and tribunals.
