Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 21 December 2011. Centre hospitalier universitaire de Besançon v Thomas Dutrueux and Caisse primaire d'assurance maladie du Jura. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Conseil d'État - France. Directive 85/374/EEC - Liability for defective products - Scope - National rules requiring public healthcare establishments to pay compensation; even when they are not at fault; for damage sustained by a patient as a result of the failure of equipment or products used in the course of treatment.
