Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 12 September 2013. Anton Schlecker v Melitta Josefa Boedeker. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Hoge Raad der Nederlanden. Rome Convention on the law applicable to contractual obligations — Contract of employment — Article 6(2) — Applicable law in the absence of a choice made by the parties — Law of the country in which the employee ‘habitually carries out his work’ — Contract more closely connected with another Member State.
