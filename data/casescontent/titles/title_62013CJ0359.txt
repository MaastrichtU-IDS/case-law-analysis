Reference for a preliminary ruling — Freedom of movement for persons — Articles 20 TFEU and 21 TFEU — National of a Member State — Residence in another Member State — Studies pursued in an overseas country or territory — Maintenance of the grant of funding for higher education — ‘Three-out-of-six-years’ residence rule — Restriction — Justification.
