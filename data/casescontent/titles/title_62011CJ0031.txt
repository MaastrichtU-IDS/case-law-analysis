Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 19 July 2012. Marianne Scheunemann v Finanzamt Bremerhaven. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Bundesfinanzhof. Freedom of establishment — Free movement of capital — Direct taxation — Inheritance tax — Conditions for the calculation of the tax — Acquisition through inheritance of a shareholding; as sole shareholder; in a capital company established in a third country — National legislation excluding shareholdings in such companies from tax advantages.
