Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 30 November 2017. IJDF Italy Srl v Violeta Fernando Dionisio and Alex Del Rosario Fernando. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale di Torino. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Consumer contracts — Directive 93/13/EEC — National legislation which permits the main debtor and the guarantor to be brought together before the same court — Derogation from the rules establishing the domicile of the consumer — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court.
