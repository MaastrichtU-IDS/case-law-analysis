Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 11 September 2008. Hans Eckelkamp and Others v Belgische Staat. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hof van beroep te Gent - Belgium. Free movement of capital - Articles 56 EC and 58 EC - Inheritance tax - National rules concerning the assessment of duties on the transfer of immovable property which do not allow for mortgage-related charges relating to the immovable property to be deducted from the value of that property on the ground that; at the time of death; the person whose estate is being administered was residing in another Member State - Restriction - Justification - None.
