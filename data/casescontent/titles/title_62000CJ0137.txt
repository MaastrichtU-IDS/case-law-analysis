Judgment of the Court of 9 September 2003.   The Queen v The Competition Commission; Secretary of State for Trade and Industry and The Director General of Fair Trading; ex parte Milk Marque Ltd and National Farmers' Union.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: High Court of Justice (England and Wales); Queen's Bench Division (Crown Office) - United Kingdom.   Common agricultural policy - Articles 32 EC to 38 EC - Regulation (EEC) No 804/68 - Common organisation of the market in milk and milk products - Target price for milk - Regulation No 26 - Application of certain competition rules to the production of and trade in agricultural products - Whether Member States may apply national competition rules to milk producers who choose to organise themselves into cooperatives and hold market power.
