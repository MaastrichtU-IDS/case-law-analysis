Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 15 November 2011. European Commission v Federal Republic of Germany. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations – Court of Auditors’ declared intention to carry out audits in a Member State – Member State’s objection – Powers of the Court of Auditors – Article 248 EC – Audit of the cooperation of the national administrative authorities in the field of value added tax – Regulation (EC) No 1798/2003 – Community revenue – Own resources accruing from value added tax.
