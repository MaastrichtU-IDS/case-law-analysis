Order of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 22 April 2015. Casa Judeţeană de Pensii Brăila v E.S. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Curtea de Apel Galați. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court — Social security for migrant workers — Regulation (EC) No 883/2004 — Article 8(1) — Applicability of social security conventions between Member States — Repatriated refugee originating in a Member State — Completion of periods of employment within the territory of another Member State — Application for the grant of an old-age benefit — Application refused.
