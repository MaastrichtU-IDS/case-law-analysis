Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 10 November 2016. 100 % Capri Italia Srl v European Union Intellectual Property Office. Appeal — Article 181 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — EU trade mark — Figurative mark containing the word elements “100% Capri” — Opposition from the proprietor of the figurative mark containing the word element “CAPRI” — Annulment by the General Court of the initial decision of the First Board of Appeal refusing registration — Decision of the Second Board of Appeal refusing registration in part.
