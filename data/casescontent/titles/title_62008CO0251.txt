Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 17 March 2009.   Ammayappan Ayyanarsamy v Commission of the European Communities and Federal Republic of Germany.   Appeal manifestly inadmissible and unfounded - Alleged infringement of the appellant’s fundamental rights by the national authorities - Action for annulment of the Commission’s letter stating that it had no power to intervene before the national courts - Concept of ‘challengeable act’ for the purposes of Article 230 EC.
