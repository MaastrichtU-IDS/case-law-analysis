Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 11 November 1999.   Firma Söhl &amp; Söhlke v Hauptzollamt Bremen.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Bremen - Germany.   Community Customs Code and implementing Regulation - Exceeding of time-limits for the customs clearance of non-Community goods in temporary storage - Failure having "no significant effect on the correct operation of the temporary storage or customs procedure in question" - Extension of period - "Obvious negligence".
