Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 16 February 2017. Verwertungsgesellschaft Rundfunk GmbH v Hettegger Hotel Edelweiss GmbH. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Handelsgericht Wien. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Intellectual property — Directive 2006/115/EC — Article 8(3) — Exclusive right of broadcasting organisations — Communication to the public — Places accessible to the public against payment of an entrance fee — Communication of broadcasts by TV sets installed in hotel rooms.
