Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 23 February 2016. Francisca Garzón Ramos and José Javier Ramos Martín v Banco de Caja España de Inversiones; Salamanca y Soria SA and Intercotrans SL. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Audiencia Provincial de les Illes Balears. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Right to effective judicial protection — Mortgage enforcement proceedings — Powers of the court hearing the declaratory proceedings — No implementation of EU law — Article 53(2) of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice — Clear lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
