Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 11 July 1996.   Siegfried Otte v Bundesrepublik Deutschland.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hessischer Verwaltungsgerichtshof - Germany.   Social security for migrant workers - Community rules - Matters covered - Benefit paid to workers in the coal industry who have passed a specified agelimit and have been laid off as a result of closure of the undertaking employing them or rationalization measures (adaptation allowance) - Benefit paid by way of subsidy - Method of calculating benefits - Taking into account of a pension paid under the legislation of another Member State - Conditions and limits.
