Judgment of the Court of 9 September 2003.   Monsanto Agricoltura Italia SpA and Others v Presidenza del Consiglio dei Ministri and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale amministrativo regionale del Lazio - Italy.   Regulation (EC) No 258/97 - Novel foods - Placing on the market - Safety assessment - Simplified procedure - Substantial equivalence to existing foods - Foods produced from genetically modified maize - Presence of residues of transgenic protein - Measure by a Member State temporarily restricting or suspending the trade in or use of a novel food in its territory.
