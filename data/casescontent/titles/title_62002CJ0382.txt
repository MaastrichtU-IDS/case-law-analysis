Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 16 September 2004. Cimber Air A/S v Skatteministeriet. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Vestre Landsret - Denmark. Sixth VAT Directive - Article 15(6); (7) and (9) - Exemption of exports outside the Community - Meaning of "aircraft used by airlines operating chiefly on international routes' - Exemption for fuelling and provisioning of domestic flights.
