Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale di Vicenza - Italy.   Articles 85; 86 and 90 of the EC Treaty (now Articles 81 EC; 82 EC and 86 EC) - Compulsory affiliation to a body providing insurance against accidents at work - Whether such a body is to be treated as an undertaking.
