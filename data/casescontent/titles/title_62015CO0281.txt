Order of the Court (First Chamber) of 12 May 2016. Soha Sahyouni v Raja Mamisch. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Oberlandesgericht München. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 53(2) of the Rules of Procedure of the Court — Judicial cooperation in civil matters — Regulation (EU) No 1259/2010 — Scope — Recognition of a private divorce pronounced by a religious court in a third country — Manifest lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
