Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 13 September 2016. Secretary of State for the Home Department v CS. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Upper Tribunal (Immigration and Asylum Chamber) London. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Citizenship of the Union — Article 20 TFEU — Third-country national having a young dependent child who is a Union citizen — Right to reside in the Member State of which the child is a national — Criminal convictions of the child’s parent — Decision to expel the parent resulting in the indirect expulsion of the child concerned.
