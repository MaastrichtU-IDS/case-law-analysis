Order of the Court (Seventh Chamber) of 15 January 2010.   Messer Group GmbH v Air Products and Chemicals Inc and Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM).   Appeal - Article 119 of the Rules of Procedure - Community trade mark - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 8(1)(b) - Word marks Ferromix; Inomix and Alumix - Earlier word marks FERROMAXX; INOMAXX and ALUMAXX - Opposition by the proprietor of the trade mark - Relevant public - Degree of similarity - Limited distinctive character of the earlier mark - Likelihood of confusion.
