Order of the Court (Third Chamber); 12 July 2012. Gennaro Currà and Others v Bundesrepublik Deutschland. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunale ordinario di Brescia. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 92(1) of the Rules of Procedure — Action brought by the victims of massacres against a Member State as the party liable for acts committed by its armed forces in wartime — Charter of Fundamental Rights of the European Union — Clear lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
