Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 7 April 2011. Ioan Tatu v Statul român prin Ministerul Finanţelor şi Economiei and Others. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunalul Sibiu - Romania. Internal taxation - Article 110 TFEU - Pollution tax charged on first registration of motor vehicles - Neutrality of tax between imported second-hand motor vehicles and similar vehicles already on the domestic market.
