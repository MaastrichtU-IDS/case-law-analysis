Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 8 June 2000.   Grundstückgemeinschaft Schloßstraße GbR v Finanzamt Paderborn.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany.   Turnover taxes - Common system of value added tax - Article 17 of the Sixth Directive 77/388/EEC - Deduction of input tax - Deduction precluded by an amendment to national legislation removing the possibility of opting for taxation of the letting of immovable property.
