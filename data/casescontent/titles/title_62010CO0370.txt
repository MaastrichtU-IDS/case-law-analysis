Order of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 14 March 2011.   Ravensburger AG v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs).   Appeal - Community trade mark - Figurative mark EDUCA Memory game - Application for a declaration of invalidity made by the proprietor of the national and international word marks MEMORY - Rejection of the application for a declaration of invalidity by the Board of Appeal - Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Article 8(1)(b) and (5) - Relative grounds for refusal.
