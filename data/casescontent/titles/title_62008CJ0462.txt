Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 21 January 2010.   Ümit Bekleyen v Land Berlin.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Oberverwaltungsgericht Berlin-Brandenburg - Germany.   EEC-Turkey Association Agreement - Second paragraph of Article 7 of Decision No 1/80 of the Association Council - Right of the child of a Turkish worker to respond to any offer of employment in the host Member State in which that child has completed a vocational training course - Start of the vocational training course after the parents have permanently left that Member State.
