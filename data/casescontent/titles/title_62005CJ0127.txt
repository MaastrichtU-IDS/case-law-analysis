Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 14 June 2007.   Commission of the European Communities v United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Social policy - Protection of the safety and health of workers - Directive 89/391/EEC - Article 5(1) - Employer’s duty to ensure the safety and health of workers in every aspect related to the work - Employer’s liability.
