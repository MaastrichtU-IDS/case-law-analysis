Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 17 October 2013.   Sky Italia Srl v Autorità per le Garanzie nelle Comunicazioni and Commissione di Garanzia dell’Attuazione della Legge sullo Sciopero nei Servizi Pubblici Essenziali.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale amministrativo regionale per il Lazio - Italy.   Electronic communications networks and services - Directive 2002/20/EC - Article 12 - Administrative charges imposed on undertakings in the sector concerned - National legislation making operators of electronic communications subject to the payment of a charge intended to cover the operating costs of the national regulatory authorities.
