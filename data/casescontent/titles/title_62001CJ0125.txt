Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 18 September 2003.   Peter Pflücke v Bundesanstalt für Arbeit.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Sozialgericht Leipzig - Germany.   Protection of workers - Insolvency of the employer - Guarantee of payment of outstanding salary - National provision laying down a two-month time-limit for lodging applications for payment and providing for an extension of that time-limit.
