Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 20 September 2007.   Sari Kiiski v Tampereen kaupunki.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tampereen käräjäoikeus - Finland.   Equal treatment for men and women - Protection of pregnant employees - Article 2 of Directive 76/207/EEC - Right to maternity leave - Articles and 11 of Directive 92/85/EEC - Effect on the right to obtain an alteration of the duration of ‘child-care leave’.
