Judgment of the Court of 23 September 2003.   Secretary of State for the Home Department v Hacene Akrich.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Immigration Appeal Tribunal - United Kingdom.   Freedom of movement for workers - National of a non-Member State who is the spouse of a national of a Member State - Spouse under a prohibition on entering and remaining in that Member State - Temporary establishment of the couple in another Member State - Establishment with a view to acquisition by spouse of a right under Community law to enter and remain in the first Member State - Abuse.
