Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber); 19 June 2012. Chartered Institute of Patent Attorneys v Registrar of Trade Marks. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Person Appointed by the Lord Chancellor under Section 76 of The Trade Marks Act 1994; on Appeal from the Registrar of Trade Marks (United Kingdom). Trade marks — Approximation of laws of the Member States — Directive 2008/95/EC — Identification of the goods or services for which the protection of a trade mark is sought — Requirements of clarity and precision — Use of class headings of the Nice Classification for the purposes of the registration of trade marks — Whether permissible — Extent of protection of the trade mark.
