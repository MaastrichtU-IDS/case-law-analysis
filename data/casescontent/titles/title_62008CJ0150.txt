Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 7 May 2009. Siebrand BV v Staatssecretaris van Financiën. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands. Combined Nomenclature - Tariff headings 2206 and 2208 - Fermented beverage containing distilled alcohol - Beverage produced from fruit or from a natural product - Addition of substances - Effects - Loss of the taste; smell and appearance of the original beverage.
