Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 15 July 2015. Itales OOD v Direktor na Direktsia „Obzhalvane i danachno-osiguritelna praktika“ Varna pri Tsentralno upravlenie na Natsionalnata agentsia za prihodite. Reqeust for a preliminary ruling from the Administrativen sad - Varna. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court — Taxation — VAT — Directive 2006/112/EC — Principle of tax neutrality — Deduction of input VAT — Meaning of ‘supply of goods’ — Conditions for establishing a supply of goods — No proof that the direct supplier was actually in possession of the goods.
