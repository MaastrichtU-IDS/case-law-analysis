Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber); 12 July 2012. SC Volksbank România SA v Autoritatea Naţională pentru Protecţia Consumatorilor — Comisariatul Judeţean pentru Protecţia Consumatorilor Călăraşi (CJPC). Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Judecătoria Călăraşi. Consumer protection — Credit agreements for consumers — Directive 2008/48/EC — Articles 22; 24 and 30 — National legislation designed to transpose that directive — Applicability to agreements not included in the material and temporal scope of the directive — Obligations not provided for by the directive — Limitation on the bank charges capable of being levied by the creditor — Articles 56 TFEU; 58 TFEU and 63 TFEU — Obligation to put in place; in national law; adequate and effective out-of-court dispute resolution procedures.
