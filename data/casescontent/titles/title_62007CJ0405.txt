Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 6 November 2008. Kingdom of the Netherlands v Commission of the European Communities. Appeal - Article 95(5) EC - Directive 98/69/EC - Measures to be taken against air pollution by emissions from motor vehicles - Derogating national provision advancing the lowering of the Community limit on emissions of particulate matter produced by certain new diesel-powered vehicles - Commission’s refusal - Specificity of the problem - Duties of care and to state reasons.
