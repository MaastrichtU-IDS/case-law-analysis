Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 18 December 2007. Asociación Profesional de Empresas de Reparto y Manipulado de Correspondencia v Administración General del Estado. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Audiencia Nacional - Spain. Public procurement - Liberalisation of postal services - Directives 92/50/EEC and 97/67/EC - Articles 43 EC; 49 EC and 86 EC - National legislation allowing public authorities to conclude agreements for the provision of both reserved and non-reserved postal services with a publicly owned company; namely the provider of universal postal service in the Member State concerned; without regard to the rules governing the award of public service contracts.
