Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 10 July 2014. Apple Inc. v Deutsches Patent- und Markenamt. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Bundespatentgericht. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Trade marks — Directive 2008/95/EC — Articles 2 and 3 — Signs capable of constituting a trade mark — Distinctive character — Representation; by design; of the layout of a flagship store — Registration as a trade mark for ‘services’ connected with the products on sale in such a store.
