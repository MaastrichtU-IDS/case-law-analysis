Judgment of the Court of 5 November 2002.   Commission of the European Communities v Republic of Austria.   Failure by a Member State to fulfil its obligations - Conclusion and application by a Member State of a bilateral 'open skies' agreement with the United States of America - Secondary legislation governing the internal air transport market (Regulations (EEC) Nos 2299/89; 2407/92; 2408/92; 2409/92 and 95/93) - External competence of the Community - Article 52 of the EC Treaty (now; after amendment; Article 43 EC) - Article 5 of the EC Treaty (now Article 10 EC).
