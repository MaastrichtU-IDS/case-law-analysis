Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 17 January 2008.   Theodor Jäger v Finanzamt Kusel-Landstuhl.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesfinanzhof - Germany.   Free movement of capital - Articles 73b and 73d of the EC Treaty (now Articles 56 EC and 58 EC) - Inheritance tax - Valuation of assets forming part of the estate - Agricultural and forestry assets in another Member State - Less favourable method of valuation of assets and calculation of the tax payable.
