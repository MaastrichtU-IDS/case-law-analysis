Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 19 December 2013. Alfred Hirmann v Immofinanz AG. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Handelsgericht Wien. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Company law — Second Directive 77/91/EEC — Liability of a public limited liability company for breach of its obligations in respect of advertising — Inaccurate information in share prospectus — Extent of liability — Legislation of a Member State providing for repayment of the price paid by the purchaser for purchased shares.
