Judgment of the Court of 5 April 1995.   Zoulika Krid v Caisse nationale d'assurance vieillesse des travailleurs salariés (CNAVTS).   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal des affaires de sécurité sociale de Nanterre - France.   EEC-Algeria Cooperation Agreement - Article 39 (1) - Direct effect - Principle of non-discrimination - Scope - Widow of Algerian worker who had been employed in a Member State - Supplementary allowance from the National Solidarity Fund.
