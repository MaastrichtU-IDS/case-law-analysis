Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 26 April 2012. Asociación Nacional de Expendedores de Tabaco y Timbre (ANETT) v Administración del Estado. Reference for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal Supremo. Free movement of goods – Articles 34 TFEU and 37 TFEU – National legislation prohibiting tobacco retailers from importing tobacco products – Rule concerning the existence and operation of a monopoly on the marketing of tobacco products – Measures having equivalent effect to quantitative restrictions – Justification – Consumer protection.
