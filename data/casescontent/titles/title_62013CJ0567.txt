Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 12 February 2015. Nóra Baczó and János István Vizsnyiczai v Raiffeisen Bank Zrt. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Fővárosi Törvényszék. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Consumer protection — Directive 93/13/EEC — Article 7 — Mortgage loan agreement — Arbitration clause — Unfairness — Action by consumer — National procedural rule — Lack of jurisdiction of the court hearing the action by a consumer for a declaration of invalidity of a standard contract to hear the application for a declaration of unfairness of terms in the same contract.
