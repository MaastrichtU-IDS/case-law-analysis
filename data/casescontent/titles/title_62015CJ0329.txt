Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 13 September 2017. ENEA S.A. v Prezes Urzędu Regulacji Energetyki. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Sąd Najwyższy. Reference for a preliminary ruling — State aid — Concept of ‘aid granted by a Member State or through State resources’ — Obligation on a limited liability company in the energy sector; wholly owned by the State; to purchase energy produced by cogeneration with the production of heat.
