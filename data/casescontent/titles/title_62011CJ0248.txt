Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 22 March 2012.   Criminal proceedings against Rareş Doralin Nilaş and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Curtea de Apel Cluj - Romania.   Directive 2004/39/EC - Markets in financial instruments - Article 4(1)(14) - Concept of ‘regulated market’ - Authorisation - Functional requirements - Market whose legal nature is not specified; but which is managed; after a merger; by a legal person also managing a regulated market - Article 47 - Not included on the list of regulated markets - Directive 2003/6/EC - Scope - Market manipulation.
