Order of the Court (Ninth Chamber) of 10 October 2013.   Ferenc Tibor Kovács v Vas Megyei Rendőr-főkapitányság.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Szombathelyi Törvényszék - Hungary.   Reference for a preliminary ruling - Article 45 TFEU - Free movement of workers - National legislation under which a driver using a vehicle with foreign registration plates must prove the lawfulness of its use on the spot; during a police inspection; on pain of a fine.
