Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 25 May 2000.   Jean-Marie Podesta v Caisse de retraite par répartition des ingénieurs cadres &amp; assimilés (CRICA) and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal de grande instance de Paris - France.   Social policy - Equal pay for men and women - Private; inter-occupational; supplementary retirement pension scheme based on defined contributions and run on a "pay-as-you-go" basis - Survivors' pensions for which the age conditions for grant vary according to sex.
