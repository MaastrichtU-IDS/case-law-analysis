Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 27 April 2017. FSL Holdings and Others v European Commission. Appeal — Competition — Agreements; decisions and concerted practices — European banana market in Greece; Italy and Portugal — Coordination in the fixing of prices — Admissibility of evidence transmitted by national tax authorities — Rights of the defence — Calculation of the amount of the fine — Scope of judicial review — Classification as an ‘agreement having as its object the restriction of competition’.
