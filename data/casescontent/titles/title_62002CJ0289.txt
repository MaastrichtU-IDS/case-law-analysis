Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 11 December 2003.   AMOK Verlags GmbH v A &amp; R Gastronomie GmbH.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Oberlandesgericht München - Germany.   Freedom to provide services - Lawyer established in one Member State working in conjunction with a lawyer established in another Member State - Legal costs to be reimbursed by the unsuccessful party in a dispute to the successful party - Limitation.
