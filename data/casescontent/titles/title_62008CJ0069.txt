Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 16 July 2009. Raffaello Visciano v Istituto nazionale della previdenza sociale (INPS). Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale di Napoli - Italy. Social policy - Protection of workers - Insolvency of employer - Directive 80/987/EEC - Obligation to pay all outstanding claims up to a pre-established ceiling - Nature of an employee’s claims against a guarantee institution - Limitation period.
