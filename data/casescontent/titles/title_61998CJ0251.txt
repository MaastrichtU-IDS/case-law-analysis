Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 13 April 2000.   C. Baars v Inspecteur der Belastingen Particulieren/Ondernemingen Gorinchem.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Gerechtshof 's-Gravenhage - Netherlands.   Freedom of establishment - Assets invested in shares in companies established in the taxing Member State - Exemption from wealth tax - Assets invested in shares in companies established in another Member State - No exemption.
