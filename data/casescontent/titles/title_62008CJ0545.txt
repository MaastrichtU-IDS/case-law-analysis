Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 6 May 2010.   European Commission v Republic of Poland.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Electronic communications- Directives 2002/21/EC and 2002/22/EC - High-speed internet access provider - Obligation to obtain authorisation for the access tariffs to high-speed internet services and to fix those tariffs on the basis of the costs of the provision of such services - No market analysis.
