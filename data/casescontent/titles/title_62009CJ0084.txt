Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 18 November 2010. X v Skatteverket. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Regeringsrätten - Sweden. VAT - Directive 2006/112/EC - Article 2; first paragraph of Article 20 and Article 138(1) - Intra-Community acquisition of a new sailing boat - Immediate use of the goods purchased in the Member State of acquisition or in another Member State before transporting it to its final destination - Time-limit within which transport of goods to place of destination commences - Maximum duration of transport - Relevant point in time for determining whether a means of transport is new for the purposes of taxation thereof.
