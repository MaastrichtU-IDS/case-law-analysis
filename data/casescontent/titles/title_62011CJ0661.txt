Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber); 19 September 2013. Martin y Paz Diffusion SA v David Depuydt and Fabriek van Maroquinerie Gauquie NV. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Cour de cassation (Belgium). Trade marks — Directive 89/104/EEC — Article 5 — Consent on the part of the proprietor of a trade mark to the use; by a third party; of a sign which is identical with that mark — Consent given in a situation where there is shared use — Possibility for that proprietor to put an end to the shared use and regain the exclusive use of its mark.
