Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 3 May 2001.   Commission of the European Communities v Kingdom of Belgium.   Failure by a State to fulfil its obligations - Social security - Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 - Article 13(2)(f) - Legislation of a Member State providing for social security contributions to be levied on occupational disease benefits payable to persons who do not reside in that State and are no longer subject to its social security scheme.
