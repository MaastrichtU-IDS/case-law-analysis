Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 27 June 1991.   Al-Jubail Fertilizer Company (Samad) and Saudi Arabian Fertilizer Company (Safco) v Council of the European Communities.   Application for a declaration that Council Regulation (EEC) Nº 3339/87 of 4 November 1987 imposing a definitive anti-dumping duty on imports of urea originating in Libya and Saudi Arabia is void.
