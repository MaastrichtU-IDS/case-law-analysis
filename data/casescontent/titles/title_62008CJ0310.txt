Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 23 February 2010.   London Borough of Harrow v Nimco Hassan Ibrahim and Secretary of State for the Home Department.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Court of Appeal (England &amp; Wales) (Civil Division) - United Kingdom.   Freedom of movement for persons - Right of residence of a national of a non-member country who is the spouse of a national of a Member State; and of their children who are themselves nationals of a Member State - National of a Member State ceasing to work and leaving the host Member State - Enrolment of the children at a school - No means of subsistence - Regulation (EEC) No 1612/68 - Article 12 - Directive 2004/38.
