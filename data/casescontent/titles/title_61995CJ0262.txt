Judgment of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 7 November 1996.   Commission of the European Communities v Federal Republic of Germany.   Failure to fulfil obligations - Non-transposition of Directives 82/176/EEC; 83/513/EEC; 84/156/EEC; 84/491/EEC and 86/280/EEC on the discharge of certain dangerous substances into the aquatic environment.
