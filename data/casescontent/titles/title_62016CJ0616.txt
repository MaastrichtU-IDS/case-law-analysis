Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 24 January 2018. Presidenza del Consiglio dei Ministri and Others v Gianni Pantuso and Others. Requests for a preliminary ruling from Corte suprema di cassazione. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Coordination of provisions laid down by law; regulation or administrative action in respect of activities of doctors — Directives 75/363/EEC and 82/76/EEC — Specialist medical training — Appropriate remuneration — Application of Directive 82/76/EEC to training begun before the prescribed deadline for the Member States to transpose it and completed after that date.
