Judgment of the Court of 23 September 2003.   Bundesverband Güterkraftverkehr und Logistik eV (BGL) v Bundesrepublik Deutschland.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Bundesgerichtshof - Germany.   Free movement of goods - External transit operation - Transport under cover of a TIR carnet - Offences or irregularities - Possibility for a guaranteeing association to prove the place where the offence or irregularity was committed - Time-limit for furnishing proof - Existence of an obligation for the Member State which detects an offence or irregularity to investigate the place where it was committed.
