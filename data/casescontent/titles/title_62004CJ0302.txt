Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 10 January 2006.   Ynos kft v János Varga.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Szombathelyi Városi Bíróság - Hungary.   Article 234 EC - Directive 93/13/EEC - Consumers - Unfair terms - National legislation adapted to comply with the directive after conclusion by a non-member State of an association agreement with the European Communities and before that State acceded to the European Union - Lack of jurisdiction of the Court.
