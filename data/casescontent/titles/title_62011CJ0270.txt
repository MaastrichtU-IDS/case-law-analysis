Judgment of the Court (Fourth Chamber) of 30 May 2013.   European Commission v Kingdom of Sweden.   Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Directive 2006/24/EC - Retention of data generated or processed in connection with the provision of electronic communications services - Judgment of the Court establishing a failure to fulfil obligations - Non-compliance - Article 260 TFEU - Pecuniary penalties - Imposition of a lump sum payment.
