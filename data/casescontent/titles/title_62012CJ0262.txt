Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber); 19 December 2013. Association Vent De Colère! Fédération nationale and Others v Ministre de l’Écologie; du Développement durable; des Transports et du Logement and Ministre de l’Économie; des Finances et de l’Industrie. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Conseil d’État (France). Reference for a preliminary ruling — State aid — Concept of ‘intervention by the State or through State resources’ — Wind-generated electricity — Obligation to purchase at a price higher than the market price — Offsetting in full — Charges payable by final consumers of electricity.
