Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 25 October 2001.   Toshiba Europe GmbH v Katun Germany GmbH.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Landgericht Düsseldorf - Germany.   Comparative advertising - Marketing of spare parts and consumable items - References made by a supplier of non-original spare parts and consumable items to the product numbers specific to the original spare parts and consumable items - Directive 84/450/EEC and Directive 97/55/EC.
