Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 2 March 2017. Panrico; SA v European Union Intellectual Property Office. Appeal — European Union trade mark — Regulation (EC) No 40/94 — Article 52 — Article 8(1)(b) and Article 8(5) — Figurative mark containing the word elements ‘krispy kreme doughnuts’ — National and international word and figurative marks containing the elements ‘donut’; ‘donuts’ and ‘doughnuts’ — Application for a declaration of invalidity — Dismissal.
