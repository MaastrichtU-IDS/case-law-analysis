Judgment of the Court (Third Chamber) of 10 September 2015. Aliny Wojciechowski v Office national des pensions (ONP). Request for a preliminary ruling from the Tribunal du travail de Bruxelles. Reference for a preliminary ruling — Retired EU official who has; prior to entering the service; pursued an activity in an employed capacity in the Member State in which she is posted — Pension rights by virtue of the national pension scheme for employed persons — Occupational record unit — Refusal to pay the retirement pension for employed persons — Principle of sincere cooperation.
