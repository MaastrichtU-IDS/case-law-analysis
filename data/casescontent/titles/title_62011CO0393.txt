Order of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 7 March 2013.   Autorità per l'energia elettrica e il gas v Antonella Bertazzi and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Consiglio di Stato - Italy.   Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure of the Court of Justice - Social policy - Directive 1999/70/EC -Framework agreement on fixed-term work concluded by ETUC; UNICE and CEEP - Clause 4 - Fixed-term employment contracts in the public sector - Stabilisation procedure -Recruitment of workers employed for a fixed term as career civil servants without a public competition - Determination of length of service - Complete disregard of periods of service completed under fixed-term employment contracts - Principle of non-discrimination.
