Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 18 September 2003.   Bosal Holding BV v Staatssecretaris van Financiën.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands.   Freedom of establishment - Taxation - Taxes on company profits - Limitation of the deductibility in one Member State of costs connected with holdings of a parent company in its subsidiaries established in other Member States - Coherence of the tax system.
