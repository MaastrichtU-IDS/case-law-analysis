Judgment of the Court (Fifth Chamber) of 16 September 1999.   Oumar Dabo Abdoulaye and Others v Régie nationale des usines Renault SA.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Conseil de Prud'hommes; Le Havre - France.   Interpretation of Article 119 of the EC Treaty (Articles 117 to 120 of the EC Treaty have been replaced by Articles 136 EC to 143 EC) and of Directives 75/117/EEC and 76/207/EEC - Collective agreement providing for an allowance for pregnant women going on maternity leave.
