Order of the Court (Tenth Chamber) of 20 June 2013.   Consiglio Nazionale degli Ingegneri v Comune di Castelvecchio Subequo and Comune di Barisciano.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale amministrativo regionale per l’Abruzzo - Italy.   Article 99 of the Rules of Procedure - Public contracts - Directive 2004/18/EC - Article 1(2)(a) and (d) - Services - Support activities relating to the drawing up of a reconstruction plan for certain parts of the territory of a municipality damaged by an earthquake - Contract concluded between two public entities; one a university - Public entity likely to be classified as an economic operator - Extraordinary circumstances.
