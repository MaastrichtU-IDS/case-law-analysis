Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 30 June 2011. Wienand Meilicke and Others v Finanzamt Bonn-Innenstadt. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Finanzgericht Köln - Germany. Free movement of capital - Income tax - Certificate relating to corporation tax actually paid on dividends of foreign origin - Prevention of double taxation of dividends - Tax credit for dividends paid by resident companies - Proof required as to the foreign tax deductible.
