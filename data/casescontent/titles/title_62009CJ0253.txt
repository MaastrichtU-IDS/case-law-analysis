Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 1 December 2011. European Commission v Republic of Hungary. Failure of a Member State to fulfil obligations - Freedom of movement for persons - Freedom of establishment - Purchase of property for use as a new principal residence - Establishing the basis of assessment for the tax levied on the purchase of real property - Deduction of the value of the residence sold from the value of the residence purchased - Exclusion of that deduction if the property sold is not situated within the national territory.
