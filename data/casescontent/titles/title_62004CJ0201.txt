Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 23 February 2006.   Belgische Staat v Molenbergnatie NV.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hof van beroep te Antwerpen - Belgium.   Community Customs Code - Post-clearance recovery of import or export duties - Duty to notify the debtor of the amount of duty owed as soon as it has been entered in the accounts and before the expiry of a period of three years from the date when the debt was incurred - Definition of "appropriate procedures'.
