Appeal - Community trade mark - Article 8(1)(b) of Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Likelihood of confusion - Figurative mark 'EMILIO PUCCI' - Opposition by the proprietor of the national figurative marks 'EMIDIO TUCCI' - Similarity between the goods.
