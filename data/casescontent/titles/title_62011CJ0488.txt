Judgment of the Court (First Chamber); 30 May 2013. Dirk Frederik Asbeek Brusse and Katarina de Man Garabito v Jahani BV. Request for a preliminary ruling from the Gerechtshof te Amsterdam. Directive 93/13/EEC — Unfair terms in consumer contracts — Residential tenancy agreement between a landlord acting on a commercial basis and a tenant acting on a non-commercial basis — Examination by the national court; of its own motion; as to whether a contractual term is unfair — Penalty clause — Annulment of the clause.
