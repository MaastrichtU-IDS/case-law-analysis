Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 10 February 2011. Missionswerk Werner Heukelbach eV v Belgian State. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal de première instance de Liège - Belgium. Direct taxation - Free movement of capital - Inheritance tax - Legacies in favour of non-profit-making bodies - Refusal to apply a reduced rate where those bodies have their centre of operations in a Member State other than that in which the deceased had actually lived or worked - Restriction - Justification.
