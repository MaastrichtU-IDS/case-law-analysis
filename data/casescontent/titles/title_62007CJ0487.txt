Judgment of the Court (First Chamber) of 18 June 2009. L'Oréal SA; Lancôme parfums et beauté &amp; Cie SNC and Laboratoire Garnier &amp; Cie v Bellure NV; Malaika Investments Ltd and Starion International Ltd. Reference for a preliminary ruling: Court of Appeal (England &amp; Wales) (Civil Division) - United Kingdom.
