Judgment of the Court (Grand Chamber) of 9 March 2010.   Raffinerie Mediterranee (ERG) SpA; Polimeri Europa SpA and Syndial SpA v Ministero dello Sviluppo economico and Others.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunale amministrativo regionale della Sicilia - Italy.   Polluter pays’ principle - Directive 2004/35/EC - Environmental liability - Applicability ratione temporis - Pollution occurring before the date laid down for implementation of that directive and continuing after that date - National legislation imposing liability on a number of undertakings for the costs of remedying the damage connected with such pollution - Requirement for fault or negligence - Requirement for a causal link - Public works contracts.
