Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 11 November 2004.   Portuguese Republic v Commission of the European Communities.   Agriculture - Common agricultural policy - Financing by the EAGGF - Actual expenditure of a Member State which is less than the forecast expenditure notified by it to the Commission - Power of the Commission to reduce sums paid as advances - Letter from a Director-General of the Commission informing the Member State of that reduction - Act having binding legal effects.
