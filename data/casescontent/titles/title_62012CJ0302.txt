Judgment of the Court (Second Chamber) of 21 November 2013.   X v Minister van Financiën.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Hoge Raad der Nederlanden - Netherlands.   Request for a preliminary ruling - Article 43 EC - Motor vehicles - Use in a Member State of a private motor vehicle registered in another Member State - Taxation of that vehicle in the first Member State when it was first used on the national road network and also in the second Member State when it was registered - Vehicle used by the citizen concerned for both private use and for going; from the Member State of origin; to the place of work situated in the first Member State.
