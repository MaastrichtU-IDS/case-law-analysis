Order of the Court (Sixth Chamber) of 2 March 2011. Claro SA v Office for Harmonisation in the Internal Market (Trade Marks and Designs) (OHIM). Appeal - Community trade mark - Refusal of registration - Admissibility of the appeal before the Board of Appeal - Failure to file a statement setting out the grounds of appeal - Article 59 of Regulation (EC) No 40/94 - Rule 49(1) of Regulation (EC) No 2868/95 - Appeal manifestly unfounded.
