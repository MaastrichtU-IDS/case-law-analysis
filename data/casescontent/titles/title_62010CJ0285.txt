Judgment of the Court (Eighth Chamber) of 9 June 2011.   Campsa Estaciones de Servicio SA v Administración del Estado.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Tribunal Supremo - Spain.   Sixth VAT Directive - Articles 11A(1) and 27 - Taxable amount - Extension of the rules on application for private use to transactions between connected parties where prices are patently lower than open market prices.
