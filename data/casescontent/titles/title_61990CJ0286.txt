Judgment of the Court of 24 November 1992.   Anklagemyndigheden v Peter Michael Poulsen and Diva Navigation Corp.   Reference for a preliminary ruling: Kriminal- og Skifteretten i Hjørring - Denmark.   Conservation of fishery resources - Salmon caught in the North Atlantic outside the waters under the sovereignty and jurisdiction of the Member States - Prohibition on transporting and storing fish in the areas under the sovereignty and jurisdiction of the Member States - Application of that prohibition to a vessel flying the flag of a non-member country.
